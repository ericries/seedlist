## About

definity is a Chicago-based agentic data engineering platform purpose-built to operate and optimize enterprise lakehouse and Spark data pipelines [^1][^2]. The company was co-founded by Roy Daniel (CEO), Ohad Raviv (CTO), and Tom Bar-Yacov (VP R&D) [^2]. On April 29, 2026, definity announced an oversubscribed $12M Series A led by GreatPoint Ventures, with participation from Dynatrace and existing investors StageOne Ventures and Hyde Park Venture Partners, bringing total funding to $16.5M [^1][^2][^3].
