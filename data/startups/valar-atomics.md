## About

Valar Atomics is an El Segundo, California-based startup founded in 2023 that builds clusters of small nuclear reactors to power AI data centers [^1]. In November 2025, the company achieved zero-power criticality at Los Alamos National Laboratory, becoming the first company to reach that milestone under the US Department of Energy's Nuclear Reactor Pilot Programme [^2].
