## About

Gizmo is a London-based AI-powered learning platform that transforms students' notes, documents, and web content into interactive study materials including flashcards, adaptive quizzes, and gamified challenges [^1]. Founded in 2021 by University of Cambridge graduates Petros Christodoulou, Robin Jack, and Paul Evangelou, the platform has attracted more than 13 million users across over 120 countries [^1]. The company had just seven employees prior to the raise and plans to scale to around 30 [^1].
