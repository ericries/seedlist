## About

Shield AI is a defense technology company founded in 2015 by Ryan Tseng, Brandon Tseng, and Andrew Reiter [^1]. The company builds AI-powered autonomous systems for military applications, including the Hivemind autonomy stack and the V-BAT drone platform [^1]. Shield AI is headquartered in San Diego, California.
