## About

Musely is a direct-to-consumer telemedicine platform specializing in compounded prescription treatments for skin, hair, and menopause care, delivered through asynchronous consultations with board-certified dermatologists and OB-GYNs [^1]. The company was originally launched in 2013 as Trusper, a social platform for lifestyle and beauty tips, and pivoted to teledermatology services in 2019 [^3]. Musely is led by founder and CEO Jack Jia, a serial entrepreneur whose prior ventures include Baynote (acquired 2014) and Interwoven [^3]. The company is based in Silicon Valley, California [^3].

As of the May 2026 financing announcement, Musely reported having served more than 1.2 million patients and described the business as cash-flow positive for years, averaging 50% year-over-year revenue growth [^1]. The 2026 round of $360M+ from General Catalyst's Customer Value Fund (CVF) is structured as a non-dilutive revenue-share agreement rather than equity or interest-bearing debt [^1][^2].
