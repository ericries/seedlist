## About

Clover Health is a health insurance technology company founded in 2014 by Vivek Garipalli and Kris Gale [^1]. The company offers Medicare Advantage plans and uses its proprietary data platform, the Clover Assistant, to analyze patient data and improve health outcomes [^2]. Garipalli previously founded CarePoint Health, a healthcare system in New Jersey, and Gale was formerly VP of Engineering at Yammer [^1]. The company went public on January 8, 2021, via a SPAC merger with Social Capital Hedosophia Holdings Corp. III, led by Chamath Palihapitiya, at a $3.7 billion enterprise value, trading on Nasdaq under ticker CLOV [^3]. In 2025, the company reported $1.9 billion in revenue with 113,803 Medicare Advantage members [^7].

## What Investors Say

Chamath Palihapitiya, Founder and CEO of Social Capital Hedosophia, on the SPAC closing: "I am thrilled to announce the closing of this transaction and bring Clover Health to the public markets. Vivek, Andrew and their team have created a next-generation Medicare Advantage company that combines wide access to healthcare with a technology-enabled operating model." [^3]

Benny Peretz, Partner at Greenoaks Capital, on the Series C: "By combining a data-driven approach to improving health outcomes and a genuine desire to delight its members, Clover offers a Medicare Advantage plan that creates a truly positive impact on member health, while delivering a superior member experience and value that continues to get better and better at a rapid pace." [^5]

Jeff Lo, Founder and General Partner at Arena Ventures, on the Series C: "Clover is constructing a moat that will set it up to become one of the most important healthcare companies of our generation." [^5]

## What Founders Say

Vivek Garipalli, CEO & Co-Founder, on the SPAC closing: "Today marks an important milestone in Clover Health's mission to improve every life by enabling improved clinical decision making and achieving affordability in healthcare." [^3]

Vivek Garipalli on the Series B: "This new capital provides us with the flexibility to expand strategically into new markets, as well as continue to build out our technology stack." [^4]

Kris Gale, CTO & Co-Founder, on the Series C: "As a result of our real-time data feedback loops about members, we can partner with providers to have the highest impact on their health outcomes and make certain that our most at-risk members are receiving the best care possible." [^5]
