## About

Round is a London-based AI-powered finance automation platform that integrates with banks and accounting systems to automate financial workflows [^1]. The platform enables finance teams to automate tasks like invoice approvals, payment execution, and payroll funding while synchronizing data with ERP systems [^1]. Round recently launched its Agentic Workflow Builder, which converts natural language into automated processes, and Autonomous Payroll, which streamlines the complete payroll cycle [^1].
