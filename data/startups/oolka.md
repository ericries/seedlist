## About

Oolka is a Bengaluru-based AI-powered credit management platform founded in 2023 by Utkrishta Kumar [^1][^2]. The company describes itself as India's first agentic AI-powered credit management platform, helping users track, pay, and earn cashback on loan EMIs while managing their credit score; the Android app supports nearly 100 Indian languages and has drawn approximately 2 million users [^2]. Oolka previously raised $7M in seed funding from Lightspeed India and Z47 in 2025 [^3].
