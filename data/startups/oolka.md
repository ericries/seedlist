## About

Oolka is a Bengaluru-based agentic AI credit management platform founded in 2024 by Utkrishta Kumar, a former Meesho executive [^1][^2]. The company describes itself as India's first agentic AI-powered credit companion, helping users track, pay, and earn cashback on loan EMIs while managing their credit health [^3]. As of the Series A announcement, Oolka reports approximately 6 million registered users, $2.5 million in annual recurring revenue, and partnerships with lenders including IDFC First Bank, DMI Finance, and L&T Finance [^1][^2].
