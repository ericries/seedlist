## About

Fun is a New York-based global payments infrastructure company founded in 2022 [^1]. The company powers deposits, withdrawals, and settlement flows for internet-native capital markets platforms including Polymarket, Lighter, and Aave, processing more than $18 billion in annual transaction volume across over 100 countries [^1][^2]. Its infrastructure connects traditional fiat payment rails with blockchain-based settlement, enabling end-users to move value into and out of crypto-native applications [^2][^3].
