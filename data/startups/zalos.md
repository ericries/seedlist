## About

Zalos is a London and San Francisco-based startup building AI Computer Agents for enterprise finance operations [^1][^2]. Founded in 2025 by William Fairbairn and Hung Hoang, the company's agents log into ERPs, Excel, email, and internal tools to automate repetitive finance workflows such as billing, reconciliations, and accounts payable [^1][^2]. The platform converts screen recordings of finance workflows into autonomous agents that operate systems the way humans do [^1].
