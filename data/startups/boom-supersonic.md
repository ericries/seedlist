## About

Boom Supersonic (formerly Boom Technology) is an aerospace company founded by Blake Scholl in September 2014 in Denver, Colorado [^1]. The company is developing Overture, a Mach-1.7 commercial airliner designed to carry 64-80 passengers using 100% sustainable aviation fuel [^2]. Boom participated in Y Combinator's Winter 2016 batch [^3]. The company achieved the first privately developed supersonic flight with its XB-1 demonstrator in January 2025 [^2]. Boom has secured orders from United Airlines, American Airlines, and Japan Airlines [^2]. In late 2025, the company also announced Superpower, a natural gas turbine product for powering AI data centers [^4]. Total funding exceeds $700 million across multiple rounds [^4].
