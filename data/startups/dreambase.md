## About

Dreambase is an Austin-based AI-powered analytics platform that lets companies build data-driven workflows without hiring a dedicated data team [^1]. Led by CEO Andy Keil, Dreambase has developed AI-native data agents to handle analytical work historically performed by data teams [^1]. The technology is Postgres-native and targets the broad Postgres developer ecosystem, including the 7 million developers in Supabase's community [^1].
