## About

Patreon is a creator monetization platform founded in May 2013 by musician Jack Conte and engineer Sam Yam in San Francisco [^1][^2]. The platform enables creators to earn recurring revenue through paid memberships, and has paid out billions of dollars to creators since launch [^2][^3].
