## About

Patlytics is a New York-based AI platform purpose-built for the full patent lifecycle [^1]. IP professionals at law firms and companies use Patlytics to run patent workflows end to end: invention harvesting, application drafting, infringement analysis, invalidity contentions, transactional due diligence, and portfolio management [^1]. Customers report 80% reduction in project time, $30,000+ saved per claim chart, and up to 15 hours recovered per patent application [^1]. Total funding now stands at approximately $65 million, raised in under two and a half years [^1].
