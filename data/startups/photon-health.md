## About

Photon is a Brooklyn-based prescription infrastructure company founded in 2021 that combines digital prescribing and routing infrastructure, a pharmacy network, and a consumer-facing marketplace to bring real-time price transparency to the moment a prescription is written [^1][^2]. The company was founded by CEO Otto Sipe [^1]. On April 30, 2026, Photon announced a $16M Series A led by Healthier Capital, with participation from Notation, Flare Capital, and Evidenced, bringing total funding to $25M [^1][^2][^3].
