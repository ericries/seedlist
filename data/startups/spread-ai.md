## About

SPREAD is a Berlin-based industrial AI company building an "Engineering Intelligence" platform that transforms scattered product data into a central data and AI layer for industrial product development [^1][^2]. Customers include VW and Infineon [^2]. The April 2026 Series B funds global scale-up of the Engineering Intelligence category, framed within Europe's push for AI sovereignty across industrial workflows [^1][^2].
