## About

Schematic builds entitlements and enforcement infrastructure for SaaS and AI companies, allowing companies to manage feature access and pricing rules through a dashboard rather than embedding those controls in code [^1]. The platform enables non-technical teams to adjust pricing tiers, user limits, and feature access, and Stripe has integrated Schematic to handle entitlements enforcement for its billing platform [^1]. The Boulder, Colorado-based company was founded in 2023 by Fynn Glover (CEO), Ben Papillon (CTO), and Gio Hobbins (CPO) [^1].
