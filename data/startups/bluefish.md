## About

Bluefish is a New York-based AI marketing platform that enables Fortune 500 brands to monitor, optimize, and measure their presence across AI systems like ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Claude [^1]. Founded in 2024 by Alex Sherman, Jing Feng, and Andrei Dunca, the company serves approximately 10% of the Fortune 500, including Adidas, American Express, Hearst, LVMH, and Ulta Beauty [^1].
