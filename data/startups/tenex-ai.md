## About

TENEX.AI is a Sarasota, Florida-based cybersecurity startup founded in 2024 that provides AI-native managed detection and response (MDR) services [^1]. The company has achieved 318% year-over-year growth and was ranked #1 on the 2026 Cyber 150 list of fastest-growing cybersecurity companies [^1]. The Series B round values the company at over $1 billion [^2].
