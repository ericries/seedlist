## About

Superstate is a San Francisco-based financial technology company building infrastructure to bring traditional financial assets onto public blockchains [^1]. Founded by Robert Leshner, the creator of Compound Finance, Superstate connects financial assets with crypto capital markets to expand access, improve liquidity, and advance capital formation [^2]. The company manages over $1.2 billion in assets across its tokenized funds, including USTB (a tokenized T-Bill fund) and USCC (a tokenized crypto basis exposure fund) [^2]. Superstate also operates Opening Bell, a platform for compliant onchain equity issuance [^2].
