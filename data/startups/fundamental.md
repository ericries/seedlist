## About

Fundamental is a U.S.-based AI company with R&D operations in Israel, founded in October 2024 by former DeepMind researchers Jeremy Fraenkel and Gabriel Suissa [^1][^2]. The company builds Large Tabular Models (LTMs) — AI models designed for enterprise tabular data to drive predictions, as opposed to large language models [^1]. Its flagship product NEXUS helps enterprises predict outcomes in areas like price optimization, fraud detection, and healthcare [^2]. The company emerged from stealth as a $1.2B unicorn in February 2026 [^2].
