## About

Starfish Space is a Seattle-based company developing the Otter servicing vehicle for autonomous satellite docking, servicing, and deorbiting of aging or defunct satellites in orbit [^1]. The company raised over $100 million in a Series B round announced in April 2026, bringing total funding to at least $129 million [^1].
