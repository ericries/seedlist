## About

Livid is an ad-free video hosting platform for creators and small businesses, founded by StreamYard co-founders Geige Vandentop and Dan Briggs [^1]. The platform offers transparent pricing at $10/month for a Pro tier with up to 2TB of storage, custom player branding, and analytics [^1]. Livid also launched L.O.V.E. (Livid One-click Video Exporter), a free migration tool that bulk-exports entire Vimeo video libraries with metadata preservation [^1]. The founders started Livid after Bending Spoons acquired Vimeo for $1.38 billion in November 2025 and laid off most of Vimeo's staff [^1].
