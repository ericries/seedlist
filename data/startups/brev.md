## About

Brev is a San Francisco-based AI startup building "the AI-native layer between business goals and work," using agents that join standups and business reviews to track progress, surface risks, and update goals across systems like Slack, Jira, and Salesforce [^1][^2]. Founded in 2025 by Chris Pitchford and Vic Hu, the company has worked with operations leaders at Flex, Patlytics, Unified, and RecordPoint [^1].
