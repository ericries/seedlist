## About

Monk is an AI-native accounts receivable platform that automates invoicing, collections, cash application, and dispute resolution for B2B companies. The company was co-founded in 2024 by George Kurdin and Joe Zhou, and reports an average 40% reduction in days sales outstanding, over 25 hours per month in AR team time savings, and a 24% improvement in collections response rates for customers [^1][^2].
