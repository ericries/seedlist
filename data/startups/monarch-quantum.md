## About

Monarch Quantum builds Quantum Light Engines, integrated photonic control systems for quantum computing, sensing, and networking [^1]. Founded in 2025 by photonics veteran Dr. Timothy Day, the company entered 2026 with more than $60 million in customer contracts from Quantinuum, Infleqtion, and NASA [^1].
