## About

Standard Intelligence is a San Francisco-based AI lab focused on training foundation models for computer use [^1][^2]. Founded in 2024 by Galen Mead and Devansh Pandey, who met as teenagers during the Atlas Fellowship in 2022, the company describes itself as an "aligned AGI lab" pretraining models that can learn like humans do in full generality [^2]. The team of six has developed FDM-1, a foundation model optimized for computer use tasks that the company says was trained on 11 million hours of video footage rather than manually annotated screenshots [^1][^3]. On April 30, 2026, Standard Intelligence announced a $75M round led by Sequoia Capital and Spark Capital with participation from a group of angels and advisors including Andrej Karpathy, Stanley Druckenmiller, and Milan Kovac [^1][^2][^3].
