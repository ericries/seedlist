## About

Robinhood is a financial services company that offers commission-free trading of stocks, ETFs, options, and cryptocurrencies through its mobile app [^1]. The company was co-founded in April 2013 by Vlad Tenev and Baiju Bhatt, who were roommates and classmates studying physics at Stanford University [^2]. Robinhood went public on the Nasdaq in July 2021 at a $32 billion valuation, trading under the ticker HOOD [^3]. The company has raised approximately $5.7 billion in total funding across multiple rounds [^4].
