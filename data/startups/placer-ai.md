## About

Placer.ai is a location analytics and foot traffic data platform that provides market research tools for retailers, commercial real estate firms, and other businesses [^1]. Founded in 2018 by Noam Ben-Zvi, Zohar Ben-Yehuda, Ofir Lemel, and Oded Fossfeld, the company is dual-headquartered in Los Altos, California, and Israel [^2]. Placer.ai reached unicorn status with its $100M Series C in January 2022 and surpassed $100 million in annual recurring revenue in early 2024 [^3][^4].
