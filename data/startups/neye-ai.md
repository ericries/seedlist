## About

nEye.ai develops integrated optical interconnects for data center connectivity, pioneering reconfigurable Optical Circuit Switch (OCS) on-a-chip architecture [^1]. By fusing silicon photonics with advanced MEMS technology, nEye.ai delivers ultra-low latency, massive bandwidth, and superior power efficiency for AI fabrics [^1]. Total funding stands at $152 million [^1].
