## About

Deccan AI is a post-training data and AI evaluation company that supplies data and evaluation work for AI models, with much of the work carried out by an India-based workforce of experts [^1]. Founded in October 2024, the company provides services ranging from helping models improve coding and agent capabilities to training systems to interact with external tools [^1]. Deccan AI grew by 10x last year, working with a majority of the 'Magnificent 7' tech companies [^1].
