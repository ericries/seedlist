## About

Eigen is a San Francisco-based stealth AI startup founded in 2025 by 22-year-old Paul Scherer, building AI technology focused on creating "shared, synchronous experiences that bind people together" rather than hyper-personalized AI companions [^1][^2]. The company is framed as a "mutual friend" platform aiming to enrich real-world relationships, with specific product details still under wraps [^1][^2].
