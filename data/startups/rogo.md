## About

Rogo is a New York-based agentic AI platform built for financial services, used by more than 35,000 professionals at over 250 institutions including Rothschild & Co, Jefferies, Lazard, Moelis, and Nomura [^1][^2]. Its AI agent Felix executes multi-step financial workflows autonomously — from deal screening and CIM generation to buyer outreach and data room diligence [^1][^2].
