## About

Avalyn Pharma is a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company developing inhaled formulations of approved antifibrotic therapies for hard-to-treat lung diseases [^1][^2]. The company's lead candidates are AP01, an inhaled formulation of pirfenidone in the global Phase 2b MIST trial for progressive pulmonary fibrosis, and AP02, an inhaled formulation of nintedanib in the global Phase 2 AURA trial for idiopathic pulmonary fibrosis [^1]. The inhaled approach is designed to deliver approved antifibrotic drugs directly to the lungs to improve local drug exposure while reducing systemic side effects [^1]. Prior to the IPO, Avalyn had raised approximately $370 million across four venture rounds; the company was previously known as Genoa Therapeutics [^3].
