## About

Chance Studios is a Los Angeles-based startup building a "superapp" for trading card game (TCG) collectors, with an initial focus on Pokémon TCG [^1][^2]. The platform combines the marketing and sale of physical Pokémon trading card products with a digital app where collectors can discover, trade, and engage with other collectors and communities around the cards [^1][^2]. The company was co-founded by Jun Park (CEO), who has worked across frontier technology and TCG, and Arvin Dabiri, founder of GoatedPullz, a Pokémon TCG buying, selling, and vending brand established over the prior five years [^2]. Within two weeks of opening its public beta, Chance reported more than $100,000 in trading volume in a single day [^1][^2].

Simon Kim, CEO of Hashed: "TCG and collectibles are being redefined faster than any cultural asset class in Asia today, and the winners will be those who love this world most authentically." [^1]

Ethan Sy, Arbitrum Gaming Ventures: "Chance isn't setting out to replicate what's already on the market. The company's mission is to redefine the collectibles industry itself." [^1]
