## About

Fasal Bio is a Zagreb-based renewable materials company founded in 2012 by Krešimir Hagljan that develops wood-based, biodegradable composites to replace conventional plastics in manufactured goods [^1][^2]. Its core platform, Naturion, is produced from wood and other natural raw materials and is engineered to work with standard plastic processing methods including injection moulding and extrusion [^1][^2]. The €7M growth equity investment, announced April 23, 2026, came from BlackPeak Capital and represents the final investment from BlackPeak Southeast Europe Growth Equity Fund I [^1][^2].
