## About

Pillar is a financial risk management platform that provides institutional-grade hedging tools to small and medium-sized enterprises [^1]. Founded in 2023 by CEO Harsha Ramesh and CTO Chinmay Deshpande, the company uses AI to parse data from contracts, ERPs, spreadsheets, and messaging platforms for real-time exposure analysis [^2]. Pillar's platform builds and manages hedge portfolios for clients, automatically adjusting positions based on market conditions, volatility, and risk tolerance [^1]. Clients include Shibuya Sakura Industries, Sigma Recycling, and United Metal Solutions Group [^1]. The company emerged from stealth with the announcement of its seed round [^2].
