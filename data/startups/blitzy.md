## About

Blitzy is a Cambridge, Massachusetts-based autonomous software development platform designed to execute multi-month engineering work across enterprise codebases ranging from one million to over 100 million lines of code [^1][^2]. Co-founders Brian Elliott (CEO) — a West Point graduate, former US Army Ranger, and Harvard MBA — and Sid Pardeshi (CTO) — a former NVIDIA Master Inventor with 27+ patents in generative AI — started the company while at Harvard Business School, with NFX providing first-money seed funding in 2024 [^3]. On May 5, 2026, Blitzy announced a $200M growth round at a $1.4B valuation led by Northzone, bringing total funding to $204.4M [^1][^2]. The company reports a record 66.5% on the SWE-Bench Pro benchmark and counts dozens of Global 2000 enterprises — including State Street and QAD — as customers [^1][^3].
