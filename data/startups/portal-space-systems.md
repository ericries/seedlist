## About

Portal Space Systems is a Bothell, Washington-based aerospace startup developing highly maneuverable spacecraft using solar thermal propulsion technology [^1]. Founded in 2021 by Jeff Thornburg, a former SpaceX engineer who helped build SpaceX's most powerful rocket engine, the company develops the Starburst spacecraft bus and Supernova vehicle designed for rapid orbital transfers [^1][^2]. Portal launched its first payload, Mini Nova, in March 2026 aboard a SpaceX rideshare mission [^2]. The company plans to open a 52,000-square-foot production facility in Bothell in June 2026 with capacity to manufacture 12 Supernova and 16 Starburst vehicles annually [^2].
