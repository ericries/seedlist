## About

NudgeBee is a Pune-based AI-agentic platform for enterprise cloud operations, founded in 2024 by Rakesh Rajendran and Shiv Pratap Singh [^1][^2]. The platform autonomously manages cloud workflows, reducing incident resolution times by up to 80% and lowering cloud costs by approximately 34%, and is already used by enterprise customers including Rackspace [^1][^2]. NudgeBee is targeting mid-market U.S. enterprises and India-based global capability centres that operate cloud workloads at scale [^1].
