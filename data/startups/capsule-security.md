## About

Capsule Security is a Tel Aviv-based cybersecurity startup that emerged from stealth with a platform designed to monitor and control AI agents operating within enterprise environments [^1]. The company has built a "runtime trust layer" that enforces controls within the execution path of AI agents to prevent manipulation, unexpected behavior, or data leakage [^1]. Capsule has published research uncovering two zero-day vulnerabilities in widely used agent platforms, including ShareLeak (CVE-2026-21520) in Microsoft Copilot Studio [^1]. CEO Naor Paz previously worked at F5 and Unit 8200; CTO Lidan Hazout came from Securedtouch and Transmit Security [^1].
