## About

Shepherd is an AI-native commercial insurance platform that insures the physical infrastructure for leading AI and technology companies building frontier data centers, semiconductor facilities, and energy assets [^1]. The company uses real-time construction data from partners including Procore, Autodesk, OpenSpace, DroneDeploy, and Samsara to dramatically speed up underwriting, reducing turnaround times from weeks to hours [^1]. Shepherd insures over $400 billion in project value across more than 1,500 policies for 600+ customers [^1].
