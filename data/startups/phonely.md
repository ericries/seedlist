## About

Phonely is a San Jose-based AI voice agent platform that automates phone calls for businesses [^1]. Three of Phonely's enterprise customers — Etech Global Services, TSA Group, and Engage CX — not only renewed their contracts but also invested in the Series A round [^1]. The company previously raised $3M in seed funding [^1].
