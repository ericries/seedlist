## About

Earlyasset is a Park City, Utah-based startup building infrastructure for the venture secondary market, combining pricing transparency, transaction infrastructure, and capital to expand liquidity for shareholders holding private company equity [^1]. Co-founded by Shawn Bercuson, who first encountered the secondary market in 2010 while trying to exercise options at Groupon, a company he helped found [^1].
