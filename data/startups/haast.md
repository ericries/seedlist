## About

Haast is a New York-based AI-powered enterprise compliance platform founded in 2023 [^1]. The company automates regulatory and policy review within enterprise workflows by embedding organizational policy, risk appetite, and approval logic directly into business tools [^1]. Haast reports 4.5x revenue growth in 12 months and zero customer churn, with significant Fortune 500 customer adoption [^1]. The company also has offices in San Francisco and Sydney, Australia [^1].
