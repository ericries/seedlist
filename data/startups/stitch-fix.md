## About

Stitch Fix is an online personal styling service that uses data science and human stylists to deliver curated clothing selections to customers [^1]. Founded in 2011 by Katrina Lake and Erin Morrison Flynn, the company raised approximately $42 million in venture funding before going public on the NASDAQ in November 2017 at $15 per share [^2][^3].
