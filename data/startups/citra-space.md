## About

Citra Space Corporation is a Colorado Springs-based space technology company that provides modular sensing and software solutions for detecting, tracking, and identifying orbiting objects to ensure safe and sustainable space operations [^1]. The company leverages unique optical, orbital, and radio frequency signatures for space objects to enable positive identification [^2]. Co-founder Tom Nichols brings experience from True Anomaly, the United States Space Force, 50th Space Wing, and Pacific Air Forces [^2].
