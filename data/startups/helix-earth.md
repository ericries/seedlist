## About

Helix Earth is a Houston-based hardware company built on proprietary liquid-gas chemistry with roots in NASA aerospace technology [^1]. The company develops a cost-effective commercial air conditioner add-on that separates the energy required for temperature management from moisture control, achieving up to 50% energy savings in commercial buildings [^1]. Founded in 2022 by two Rice University alumni [^1].
