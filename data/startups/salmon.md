## About

Salmon is a Manila-based consumer finance app founded in 2022 that offers digital banking and lending products — revolving credit lines, installment loans, cash loans, motorbike loans, and deposits — targeting underbanked Filipinos [^1]. Cumulative funding stands at $310M ($160M equity, $150M bonds) [^1]. Salmon was co-founded by Pavel Fedorov, George Chesakov, and Raffy Montemayor [^1].
