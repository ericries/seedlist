## About

Click Therapeutics develops and commercializes software as prescription medical treatments for patients with unmet medical needs [^1]. The company focuses on digital therapeutics across psychiatry, neurology, oncology, immunology, and cardiometabolic diseases [^1]. Its FDA-authorized products include Rejoyn for Major Depressive Disorder and CT-132 for preventive treatment of episodic migraine [^1]. CT-155, an investigational prescription digital therapeutic for schizophrenia negative symptoms, received FDA Breakthrough Device designation in 2024 [^1].
