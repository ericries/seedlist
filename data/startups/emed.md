## About

eMed is a Miami-based AI telehealth company that provides virtual healthcare services [^1]. The company is led by CEO Linda Yaccarino, who previously served as CEO of X (formerly Twitter) [^1][^2]. eMed focuses on leveraging artificial intelligence to deliver telehealth solutions [^1].
