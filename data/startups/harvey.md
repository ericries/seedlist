## About

Harvey is an AI platform for legal professionals, offering tools for document analysis, research, drafting, and workflow automation [^1]. Founded in 2022 by Winston Weinberg (former securities litigator at O'Melveny & Myers) and Gabriel Pereyra (former research scientist at Google DeepMind and Meta), the company serves over 50 of the top AmLaw 100 firms [^6].

## What Founders Say

Winston Weinberg on AI in professional services: "The future of AI will not be a chatbot that compliments your workflow; it will be the platform your workflow is built on" [^2].

Winston Weinberg on building defensible AI: "The bet was that the models were going to get better, and that you want to build systems that the next generation of model cannot do" [^7].

Winston Weinberg on access to justice: "The average price of a lawyer in the United States is $352 an hour. So almost no one can afford a lawyer...there is a massive population of folks that do not have access to our justice system" [^7].
