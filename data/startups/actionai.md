## About

ActionAI develops reliability infrastructure for enterprise AI systems, addressing trust, accuracy, and accountability challenges in mission-critical AI deployments [^1]. Founded by Miriam Haart (CEO), the platform provides granular testing, real-time debugging, human-in-the-loop controls through an "Explainable Exceptions framework," and monitoring tools to detect performance issues and prevent hallucinations [^1]. The company targets industries including finance, manufacturing, retail, insurance, supply chain, and legal systems [^1].
