## About

Blomma is an AI-powered private career coaching app that offers ongoing executive-style coaching to individual users for $25 per month, with no employer sponsorship required [^1] [^2]. The product emphasizes continuity, accountability, and memory across sessions, with goal tracking, session reflection, and integrations with calendar, notes, and performance review tools; user data is privately owned by the user [^1].

The company was founded by Silvia Oviedo López (CEO), an experienced operator who helped scale Pinterest globally to more than 500 million users and later led a 1,500+ person team as SVP of Content and Discovery at Canva, and Siddhartha Dabral (CTO), a serial founder who previously co-founded mobile commerce platform Button and has built infrastructure across mobile payments and crypto [^1] [^2].
