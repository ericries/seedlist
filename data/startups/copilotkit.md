## About

CopilotKit is a Seattle-based developer infrastructure company that builds open-source frameworks for embedding AI agents inside applications, including CoAgents and the AG-UI agent-user interaction protocol [^1]. The company was founded by brothers Atai Barkai (CEO) and Uli Barkai (Growth, Partnerships & Ecosystem Lead); Atai previously led media infrastructure at Meta and worked on flagship applications at Doximity [^2]. CopilotKit announced a $27M Series A on May 5, 2026, led by Glilot Capital Partners with participation from NFX, SignalFire, and 97212 Ventures [^1][^2]. Enterprise users include Deutsche Telekom, Docusign, Cisco, and S&P Global, and the AG-UI protocol has been adopted by Google, Microsoft, Amazon, and Oracle [^1][^2].
