## About

ALP Bio is a Schlieren, Switzerland–based biotech building a hybrid platform that combines human immune organoid biology with generative AI to predict and reduce immunogenicity risk in antibody drug development [^1][^2]. The company was founded in 2025 and operates across Switzerland and the United States [^1][^2].
