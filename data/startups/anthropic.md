## About

Anthropic is an American artificial intelligence company founded in 2021 by Dario Amodei and Daniela Amodei, along with several other former senior members of OpenAI, including Tom Brown, Jack Clark, Sam McCandlish, Jared Kaplan, Christopher Olah, and Benjamin Mann [^1]. Prior to founding Anthropic, Dario served as VP of Research at OpenAI and Daniela served as VP of Safety & Policy [^1]. The Amodei siblings left OpenAI due to directional differences, particularly around the organization's approach to AI safety [^2]. Anthropic is headquartered in San Francisco and is known for developing the Claude family of AI models, with a focus on AI safety research [^1]. The company's name is a nod to humanity, reflecting its mission to ensure transformative AI helps people and society flourish [^2]. SV Angel, founded by Ron Conway, has made investments in both OpenAI and Anthropic [^3].

## What Founders Say

> "I'm deeply uncomfortable with these decisions being made by a few companies, by a few people."
> -- Dario Amodei, speaking on 60 Minutes [^13]

Dario Amodei has stated that Anthropic exists because its founders believed AI safety research needed to happen at the frontier, inside a lab building the most capable models [^13]. He left OpenAI partly because he felt the organization was not focusing enough on safety, though Anthropic now faces its own challenges balancing safety with commercial pressure [^14].
