## About

Helical is a London-based AI startup building a virtual AI lab for pharmaceutical companies, designed to turn bio foundation models into reproducible discovery systems [^1]. Founded in early 2024 by Rick Schneider, Maxime Allard, and Mathieu Klop, the platform enables scientists to test hypotheses in-silico at the speed of inference [^1]. Helical is already deployed with multiple top-20 pharma companies, including a public collaboration with Pfizer on predictive blood-based safety biomarkers [^1].
