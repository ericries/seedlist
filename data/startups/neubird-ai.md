## About

NeuBird AI is a Redwood City, California-based enterprise software company founded in 2023 by Gou Rao (CEO) and Vinod Jayaraman (CTO) [^1][^2]. The company builds an agentic AI platform for autonomous production operations, replacing reactive manual workflows with an agent that continuously analyzes telemetry data, correlates signals across systems, and delivers root cause analysis and remediation in real time [^1]. Since becoming generally available in December 2024, NeuBird AI reports resolving over 1 million alerts and achieving up to 90% reduction in mean time to resolution [^1]. The company's NeuBird AI Falcon engine extends beyond incident resolution to predictive risk detection and infrastructure cost optimization [^1].
