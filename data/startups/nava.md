## About

Nava is an AI development company that builds guardrails for autonomous agents and safety tools for the agentic economy [^1]. The company introduces escrow, dispute resolution, and economic backstops around agent activity, allowing developers to build agents that can safely transact, coordinate tasks, and manage value on behalf of users [^1]. Co-founders Krishnan and Brianna Montgomery previously worked together at EigenLayer [^1].
