## About

Realm is a Helsinki-based AI platform that builds AI agents for enterprise sales teams, automating RFP (Request for Proposal) responses, drafting tailored customer collateral, and answering technical prospect questions. The company was co-founded in 2023 by former Slush leaders Miika Huttunen and Mikko Mäntylä [^1][^2]. Realm integrates with enterprise tools including Salesforce, Slack, Notion, and HubSpot, and maintains ISO-27001, GDPR, and HIPAA compliance [^1]. WIRED magazine included Realm among its "100 hottest startups in Europe" [^1]. The company previously raised a €1.7 million seed round in June 2024 led by Lifeline Ventures [^2].
