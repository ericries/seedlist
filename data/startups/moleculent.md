## About

Moleculent is a Stockholm, Sweden-based life sciences technology company developing a functional profiling platform that maps cell-cell communication in human tissue using a proprietary Proximity Ligation Assay [^1][^2]. The company launched publicly in June 2024 with a $26 million Series A led by ARCH Venture Partners [^3]. On April 30, 2026, Moleculent announced an additional $20 million round led by Rubicon Healthcare Partners, with participation from existing investors ARCH Venture Partners and Eir Ventures, to scale its Techstart Early Access Program and accelerate US commercial expansion [^1][^2]. Co-founder and CEO Olle Ericsson leads the company; as part of the 2026 round, Ole Dahlberg, Managing Partner at Rubicon Healthcare Partners, joined the Board of Directors [^1].
