## About

Cognichip is a San Francisco-based startup building AI-powered tools for semiconductor design, founded in 2024 by semiconductor veteran Faraj Aalaei [^1][^2]. The company introduced ACI (Artificial Chip Intelligence), a purpose-built, physics-informed foundation model that fuses logic and physics-based reasoning to navigate complex chip design spaces [^2]. Cognichip claims to accelerate design cycles from months to days and reduce costs by 75% while optimizing power, performance, and area [^2]. The company is currently engaged with more than 30 semiconductor companies, including many of the top 20 semiconductor leaders [^2].
