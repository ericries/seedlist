## About

Firestorm Labs is a defense technology company building xCell, a containerized expeditionary manufacturing platform that produces combat-ready unmanned systems and parts at the tactical edge [^1][^2]. The April 2026 Series B funds production scale-up and field deployment of xCell units, which replace centralized supply chains with deployable on-site manufacturing [^1][^2].
