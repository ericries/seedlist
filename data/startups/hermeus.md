## About

Hermeus is a Los Angeles-based aerospace and defense startup building what it calls the "fastest unmanned aircraft" [^1]. Co-founded by AJ Piplica (CEO), the company develops autonomous hypersonic aircraft capable of flying at Mach 5+ speeds [^1]. In April 2026, the company raised $350 million ($200M equity + $150M debt), pushing its valuation to $1 billion [^1]. Hermeus is partnering with RTX subsidiary Pratt & Whitney to modify the F100 engine to power its hypersonic aircraft [^1].
