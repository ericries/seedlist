## About

OpenFX is a cross-border payments platform founded in 2024 by FalconX co-founder Prabhakar Reddy [^1]. The company uses stablecoins as an intermediary settlement rail to enable near-instant FX conversion and cross-border settlement, processing over $45 billion in annualized payment volume across 40+ trading pairs [^1][^2].
