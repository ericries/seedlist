## About

OpenFX is a cross-border payments and FX infrastructure platform founded in 2024 by FalconX co-founder Prabhakar Reddy [^1][^2]. The company uses stablecoins as an intermediary settlement rail to enable near-instant FX conversion and cross-border settlement, providing institutional-grade liquidity across more than 40 trading pairs with over 98% of transactions settling in under 60 minutes [^1][^3]. By the time of its Series A, OpenFX had grown from $4 billion to over $45 billion in annualized payment volume and had onboarded more than 100 global institutional customers, including MoneyGram, Yellow Card, and alfred [^1][^3].
