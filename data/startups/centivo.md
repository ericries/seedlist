## About

Centivo is a health plan administrator for self-funded employers, founded in 2017 and headquartered in Buffalo, NY [^1][^2]. The company partners with local health systems to offer primary care-centered health plans designed to lower the total cost of care by 30% or more compared to traditional insurance carriers [^2]. Co-founders Ashok Subramanian and Alan Cohen previously co-founded Liazon, a benefits exchange platform [^1]. As of September 2024, Centivo had raised approximately $171 million in equity financing [^3].
