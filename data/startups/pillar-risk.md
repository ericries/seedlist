## About

Pillar is a financial risk management platform offering institutional-grade hedging tools to small and mid-market enterprises [^1]. The platform modernizes treasury operations, currency hedging, interest rate exposure, and regulatory compliance, replacing outdated spreadsheet-based systems with modern APIs and user interfaces [^2]. Pillar targets companies that have "outgrown spreadsheets but don't need — or can't afford — enterprise solutions" for risk management [^2].
