## About

InstaSwitch is a fintech infrastructure platform that automates account-switching workflows for business banking institutions, founded in 2025 by Daniel West [^1][^2]. The company is headquartered in New York with an office in Chicago [^1]. InstaSwitch's product moves payroll, income platforms, vendor payments, and client direct deposits between banks using an agentic AI execution model, with the goal of converting opened business accounts into active primary banking relationships [^1][^2]. According to the company, approximately 5.4 million U.S. small businesses attempt to switch bank providers each year and most never complete the move [^1].
