## About

Iridius is a Seattle-based enterprise AI infrastructure company building a compliance-by-design platform for regulated industries, particularly life sciences and pharmaceuticals [^1][^2]. Founded in 2024 by Alistair Lowe-Norris and CEO Mike Kropp, both veterans of Microsoft and AWS, the company transforms regulatory standards and operating procedures into machine-readable logic embedded in workflows, ensuring continuous compliance enforcement and audit evidence generation [^1][^3]. Its solution includes a knowledge engine that converts regulations into structured logic and an intelligent factory orchestrating compliant workflows across pharmaceutical operations including regulatory submissions, pharmacovigilance, and manufacturing [^1].
