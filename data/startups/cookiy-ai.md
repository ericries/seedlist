## About

Cookiy AI is a Palo Alto-based startup that uses agentic AI with voice AI to conduct AI-moderated qualitative interviews, identifying consumer motivations, preferences, and mindsets [^1]. Founder Davin YC Dong was one of the first engineers at Instagram and previously held roles at TikTok and Baidu [^1]. The company surpassed $1M ARR within six months of founding [^2].
