## About

TextQL develops an AI-powered agentic data analytics platform that combines an AI agent with a purpose-built data warehouse operating within customer environments [^1]. The platform automatically maps relationships across datasets to create a business-friendly knowledge layer, enabling analysis of raw enterprise data without extensive pre-modeling or schema design [^1]. The company previously raised a $4.1M seed round co-led by DCM and Neo in January 2024 [^2].
