## About

STORM Therapeutics is a Cambridge, UK-based clinical-stage biotechnology company targeting RNA modifications to reprogram cells and develop novel cancer therapies [^1]. The company's lead program is STC-15, a first-in-class oral small-molecule inhibitor of METTL3, which has demonstrated durable tumor regression across multiple sarcoma subtypes in Phase 1 [^1].
