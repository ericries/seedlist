## About

Hugging Face is an open-source AI and machine learning platform founded in 2016 in New York City by French entrepreneurs Clément Delangue, Julien Chaumond, and Thomas Wolf [^1]. The company originally developed a chatbot app for teenagers before pivoting to build open-source NLP tools, releasing the Hugging Face Transformers library which became widely adopted by researchers and engineers [^1]. Today, the platform hosts over 100,000 pre-trained models and 10,000 datasets, serving as the primary hub for AI model sharing and collaboration [^2]. By 2024, the company reported $130.1M in revenue and 50,000 customers [^3].
