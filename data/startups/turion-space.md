## About

Turion Space provides sovereign-grade orbital intelligence and operations through its DROID satellite fleet and STARFIRE operating system [^1]. The company is the first NOAA-licensed commercial provider of resolved Non-Earth Imaging (NEI) in the United States [^1]. Its first two missions (DROID.001 and DROID.002) have delivered over 40,000 images [^1].
