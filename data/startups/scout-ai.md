## About

Scout AI is a Silicon Valley-based defense AI lab building Fury, a foundation model for unmanned warfare, founded in 2024 by Colby Adcock and Collin Otis [^1][^2]. The reasoning layer turns commander intent into coordinated action across air, land, sea, and space unmanned systems [^1][^2]. The company operates a training range called Foundry at a military base, and its first product Ox bundles command-and-control software with hardened computing hardware (GPUs, comms, cameras) for individual soldiers to orchestrate drones and autonomous ground vehicles via natural-language prompts [^1][^2].
