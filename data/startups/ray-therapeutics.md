## About

Ray Therapeutics is a Berkeley-based clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company developing gene therapies for vision restoration in patients with severe retinal degeneration [^1][^2]. Founded in 2020, its lead clinical program RTx-015 received FDA Regenerative Medicine Advanced Therapy (RMAT) Designation for retinitis pigmentosa [^2]. The April 2026 Series B was upsized and oversubscribed at $125M [^1][^3].
