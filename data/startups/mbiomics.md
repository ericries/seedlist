## About

mbiomics is a Munich-based clinical-stage techbio company developing scalable microbiome-based therapeutics for severe and chronic diseases [^1][^2]. The lead candidate MBX-116 is being prepared for Phase 1B clinical evaluation in 2027 as an immune checkpoint inhibitor co-therapy in advanced melanoma [^1][^2]. The April 2026 announcement marks the third closing of the company's Series A, totaling €30M [^1][^2].
