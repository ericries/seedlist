## About

Anvil Robotics is an eight-month-old startup that builds customizable robots described as "Legos for robots" [^1]. The San Francisco-based company operates as an integrated hardware, software, and manufacturing platform, enabling physical AI teams to build, configure, and rapidly deploy robotic systems [^1]. Robots are typically priced between $1,900 and $10,000, and the company has delivered over 100 units globally since beginning shipments in September 2025 [^1]. Anvil has a significant manufacturing presence in Taiwan, enabling 1-2 day shipping via air freight [^1].
