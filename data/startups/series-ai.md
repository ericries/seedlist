## About

Series is an AI-powered social network that operates entirely through iMessage, founded in 2025 by Yale seniors Nathaneo Johnson (CEO) and Sean Hargrow [^1]. The platform reports presence on 750+ college campuses with an 82% Day 30 retention rate, with offices in Chelsea, New York [^1].
