## About

Liquid is a 24/7 multi-asset trading platform consolidating crypto, equities, commodities, FX, prediction markets, and pre-IPO secondaries into a single interface across iOS, Android, and desktop, with up to 200x leverage on more than 500 markets [^1][^2]. Founded in 2025 by CEO Franklyn Wang, a 25-year-old Harvard graduate and former Two Sigma quantitative researcher, the platform launched in August 2025 and has reportedly attracted ~40,000 users and generated $3B+ in trading volume [^1][^2][^3].
