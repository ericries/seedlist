## About

Windmill is a workforce-intelligence platform that builds an AI-powered "context graph" of how employees and teams perform across an organization, with each insight cited back to a specific Slack thread, pull request, or document [^1][^2]. The first product is performance reviews that "write themselves" from this graph [^1]. Windmill was founded in 2025 by Max Shaw, Brian Distelburger, and Mark Tanner [^1][^2].
