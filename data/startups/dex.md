## About

Dex is a London-based AI talent agent for engineers, founded by Paddy Lambros in early 2025 — engaging candidates in detailed conversations to match them with companies, providing research, compensation benchmarking, and interview preparation [^1][^2]. More than 15,000 engineers have signed up and 50+ technology companies (including Lovable, ElevenLabs, Synthesia, Granola, and Fyxer) pay for the service [^2]. The seed extension funds office openings in New York and San Francisco in 2026 [^1][^2].
