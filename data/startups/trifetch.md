## About

TriFetch is an AI automation platform for specialty clinics that streamlines patient scheduling, referral processing, and insurance prior authorization through voice agents and workflow automation [^1]. The pre-seed round was led by Nexus Venture Partners with angel participation from operators at Google, Hippocratic AI, Mercor, and MIT [^1].
