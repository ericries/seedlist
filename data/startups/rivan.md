## About

Rivan (Rivan Industries) is a UK-based synthetic fuel producer that designs, manufactures, and deploys modular synthetic fuel plants combining off-grid solar power, water electrolysis, direct air capture, and Sabatier reactors to produce 99.9% pure synthetic methane that can be injected directly into gas grids as a drop-in replacement for fossil natural gas [^1][^3]. Founded by serial entrepreneur Harvey Hodd, the company is based in Lewisham, South London, and targets hard-to-abate sectors such as steel, cement, and chemicals [^2][^3]. The April 2026 round is intended to scale domestic synthetic fuel production across Europe [^1].
