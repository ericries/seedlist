## About

Zum is a San Francisco-based startup providing software and services for modernizing U.S. student transportation, including electric buses, route optimization, and tracking tools [^1]. Founded in 2016 by Ritu Narayan, the company serves more than 4,500 schools across 17 U.S. states [^1]. After TPG's $100 million investment, Zum's total funding stands at $430 million with a valuation of $1.7 billion [^1].
