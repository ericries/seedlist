## About

Actively is a New York-based "Intelligence-Led Revenue" platform that deploys per-account AI agents to continuously work every account in a company's pipeline, training a private model on each customer's product, historical data, and sales playbook [^1][^2]. The company was founded by CEO Mihir Garimella and co-founder Anshul Gupta and is opening a San Francisco office with the new Series B [^1][^2].
