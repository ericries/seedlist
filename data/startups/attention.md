## About

Attention is a New York-based AI platform that automates CRM updates and sales workflow actions from customer conversations [^1]. Founded in 2021 by Anis Bennaceur and Matthias Wickenburg, the company enables revenue teams to unlock and utilize valuable data from customer interactions, eliminating manual data entry for sales teams [^2]. Attention raised a $3.1 million seed round led by Eniac Ventures in January 2023 [^2], followed by a $14 million Series A led by Alven in 2024 [^3].
