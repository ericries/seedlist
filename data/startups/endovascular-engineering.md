## About

Endovascular Engineering (E2) is a Menlo Park-based commercial-stage medical technology company focused on advancing the treatment of venous thromboembolism (VTE) [^1]. The company developed the Helo Thrombectomy Platform for the treatment of pulmonary embolism, featuring a patented dual-action mechanism that integrates aspiration with mechanical clot disruption [^1].
