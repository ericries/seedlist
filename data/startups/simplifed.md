## About

SimpliFed is an Ithaca, New York-based virtual maternal healthcare platform originally focused on breastfeeding and baby feeding support, now expanding into comprehensive virtual OB care [^1]. The company is on track to serve 5% of all US births in 2026 and is in-network with major commercial health plans and Medicaid across several states [^1]. SimpliFed is investing in a proprietary "Maternal Health Operating System" with EMR interoperability standards and APIs [^1].
