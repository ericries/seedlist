## About

Artemis is a cybersecurity startup that emerged from stealth in April 2026, building an AI-native security operations platform that delivers real-time threat detection and automated response for AI-powered attacks [^1][^2]. Founded in late 2025 by Shachar Hirshberg (who built AWS GuardDuty) and Dan Shiebler (who led AI/ML at Abnormal Security), the company raised $70 million in combined seed and Series A funding within six months of founding [^1]. Clients include Mercury, Wix, Lemonade, and Abnormal AI [^1].

"Every CISO our team spoke with at RSA this year echoed the same refrain: AI will change security more than the cloud did, and right now, none of them feel ready." -- Felicis, investment announcement blog post, April 2026 [^2]
