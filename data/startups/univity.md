## About

UNIVITY is a French SpaceTech startup building a sovereign VLEO (Very Low Earth Orbit) satellite constellation to provide high-speed space-based connectivity for telecom operators, positioned as a European alternative to Starlink-style networks [^1][^2]. The €27M Series A, announced April 23, 2026, was led by Blast and Expansion with participation from the Deeptech 2030 fund managed by Bpifrance on behalf of the French State (as part of France 2030), alongside two family offices [^1][^2].
