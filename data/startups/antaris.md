## About

Antaris develops the Antaris Intelligence platform for AI-powered satellite mission design, simulation, manufacturing, and operations [^1]. The platform includes the TrueTwin simulation environment, enabling customers to virtually fly entire missions before committing to hardware [^1]. Antaris supports U.S. Department of Defense satellite initiatives and international projects including a 16-satellite constellation in Saudi Arabia [^1].
