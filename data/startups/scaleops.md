## About

ScaleOps is an autonomous cloud and AI infrastructure resource management platform that optimizes Kubernetes environments [^1]. The company has achieved over 350% year-over-year growth and counts Adobe, Wiz, DocuSign, and Coupa among its customers [^1]. The Series C values the company at over $800 million [^1].
