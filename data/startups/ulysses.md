## About

Ulysses is a San Francisco-based company building networked autonomous vehicles that operate above and below ocean surfaces [^1]. Founded in Dublin in 2023 by Akhil Voorakkara, Will O'Brien, Jamie Wedderburn, and Colm O'Brien, the company develops small, modular underwater drones with capabilities that can be added like "snapping on LEGOs" — batteries for range, propellers for speed, GPU modules for computing power [^2]. Ulysses serves both commercial markets (energy, marine management, ocean habitat restoration) and defense customers including the US Navy, the Australian government, and the Great Barrier Reef Foundation [^1][^2]. The company currently employs 20 people and has generated over $5 million in revenue [^1].
