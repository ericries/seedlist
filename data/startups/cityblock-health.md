## About

Cityblock Health is a value-based healthcare provider focused on underserved urban populations, primarily Medicaid beneficiaries and lower-income Medicare recipients [^1]. The company was founded in 2017 as a spinout of Alphabet's Sidewalk Labs urban innovation incubator [^2]. Headquartered in Brooklyn, New York, Cityblock provides personalized primary care, behavioral health, and social services to members in low-income communities [^1]. In 2022, co-founder Dr. Toyin Ajayi succeeded Iyah Romm as CEO [^3]. The company restructured in 2023, cutting 12% of its workforce as it pursued profitability [^4].
