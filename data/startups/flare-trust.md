## About

Flare is a Copenhagen-based startup building trust infrastructure for knowledge validation in the AI era [^1]. The company combines structured data systems with collective intelligence to help users distinguish verified from unverified information at scale [^1]. The founding team comes from Google, Unity, IBM, Amazon, and Corti [^1].
