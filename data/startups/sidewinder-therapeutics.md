## About

Sidewinder Therapeutics is a San Diego-based biotech company founded in 2023 that develops next-generation bispecific antibody-drug conjugates (ADCs) designed to target receptor co-complexes found in solid tumors [^1]. The company aims to target tumors more precisely and reduce damage to healthy tissue [^1]. Sidewinder plans to advance its lead program into clinical development in 2027 [^1].
