## About

Spade is an AI-powered transaction data enrichment platform for banks and fintechs, delivering 99.9% coverage of U.S. and Canadian merchants with 99%+ accuracy [^1]. Founded in 2021, Spade processes up to 1.9 billion transactions daily for clients including Stripe, Bilt, and Mercury [^1]. The company achieved 470% YoY revenue growth in 2025 [^1].
