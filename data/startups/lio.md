## About

Lio (formerly askLio) is a Germany-based company building the world's first multi-agent AI system for enterprise procurement [^1]. Founded in 2023 by Vladimir Keil, Lukas Heinzmann, and Till Wagner, the platform deploys specialized AI agents that work in parallel to research vendors, negotiate terms, manage approvals, and track deliveries [^2]. Since launch, Lio's agents have managed billions of dollars in enterprise spend and serve dozens of Global 2000 and Fortune 500 companies worldwide [^2].
