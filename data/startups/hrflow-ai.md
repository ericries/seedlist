## About

HrFlow.ai is a Paris-based AI platform applied to human resources data, positioning itself as "Hiring SuperIntelligence" [^1][^2]. Founded in 2016 by researchers from École Normale Supérieure and École Polytechnique, the company processes candidate profiles and career paths, surpassing 50 million profiles processed in a single calendar year in 2025 [^1]. The 35-person team includes 21 researchers and engineers focused on AI algorithms and orchestration for HR data [^1]. With the pre-Series A round, the company aims to become the global standard for AI applied to HR data and scale its platform for enterprise hiring [^1].
