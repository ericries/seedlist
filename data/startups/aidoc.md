## About

Aidoc is a clinical AI company whose CARE foundation model and aiOS enterprise platform are deployed in nearly 2,000 hospitals worldwide, having analyzed more than 110 million patient cases and supporting decision-making for roughly 60 million patients each year [^1][^2].
