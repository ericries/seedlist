## About

Lithosquare is a Paris-based startup using AI to accelerate the discovery of critical mineral deposits — copper, rare earths, and lithium — for the energy transition [^1][^2]. The company combines foundation models, field data, and internal geology expertise to automate work that has traditionally been done manually by geologists, claiming to compress exploration analysis timelines from months to days and speed up discovery processes by roughly 10x [^1][^2]. Lithosquare operates with an outcome-based model that includes revenue-sharing agreements with mining partners and is working with mining companies in the US, Europe, Africa, and Latin America [^1][^2]. Founded in 2024 by mining engineer Aymeric Préveral-Etcheverry, the company plans to roughly double headcount to about 40 employees and open a US office with the new funding [^2].
