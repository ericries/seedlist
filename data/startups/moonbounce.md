## About

Moonbounce is an Oakland, California-based startup building an AI control engine that converts content moderation policies into consistent, predictable AI behavior in real time [^1][^2]. Founded in 2024 by Brett Levenson (former head of Meta's Integrity unit) and Ash Bhardwaj (former Apple engineering leader), the company serves dating platforms, AI chat applications, and generative content sites [^1][^2]. The platform processes over 50 million content evaluations daily across 250 million monthly active users, with sub-300ms response times [^2][^3]. Customers include Civitai, Dippy AI, Channel AI, and Moescape [^3].
