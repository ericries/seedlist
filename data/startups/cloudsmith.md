## About

Cloudsmith is a Belfast-based software supply chain management platform founded in 2016 that helps enterprises control, secure, and govern AI-generated and AI-driven software artifacts [^1][^2]. The $72M (€61.5M) Series C was announced on April 23, 2026, led by TCV with continued participation from Insight Partners, which had joined Cloudsmith's Series B in 2025 [^1][^2]. The round positions Cloudsmith as one of Northern Ireland's largest software businesses, with reported valuation approaching the $1B mark [^1].
