## About

Noon is an AI-native product design platform that unifies design and production code in a single environment [^1]. Founded in October 2024 by IIT Guwahati alumni Aditya Bandi and Kushagra Sinha, the company emerged from stealth in April 2026 [^2]. Noon's "dual-canvas" system allows designers to define both visual experience and underlying functionality directly on top of production code [^1]. Both founders are repeat entrepreneurs — Sinha's prior company was acquired by SoftBank-backed Whatfix, and Bandi's was acquired by Yahoo [^2].
