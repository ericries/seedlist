## About

Ramp is a corporate card and spend management platform founded in 2019 by Eric Glyman, Karim Atiyeh, and Gene Lee, headquartered in New York City [^1]. The company provides AI-powered corporate cards, expense management, and bill-payment software, reaching $1 billion in annualized revenue and a $32 billion valuation as of November 2025 [^2].
