## About

Flora Fertility is the first individually-owned fertility insurance platform, providing portable, affordable coverage for reproductive care [^1]. Policies start at $20/month and cover diagnostics, medications, IUI, and IVF treatments [^1]. The platform addresses a significant gap: infertility affects 1 in 6 people globally, with treatment costs averaging $30,000-$50,000, yet fewer than 2% of those needing care can afford it [^1]. Flora's coverage remains portable across jobs and life changes, unlike traditional employer-tied fertility benefits [^1]. The platform reaches over 10 million prospective policyholders across North America [^1].
