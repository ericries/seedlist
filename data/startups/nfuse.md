## About

nFuse is a Sofia, Bulgaria-based AI-powered B2B ordering platform that enables retailers and HoReCa operators to place orders via WhatsApp, Viber, and SMS using text, voice, or images [^1][^2]. Founded by Stoyan Ivanov, a former New Ventures Director for Europe at Coca-Cola with nearly 20 years at the company, and Stefan Radov, who spent a decade at Coca-Cola across distribution, sales, and go-to-market operations [^1]. The platform reports 70%+ adoption rates among enterprise clients compared to 10-15% for traditional B2B platforms, with cost per order targeting below $1 [^1]. The company announced its $2 million funding round on April 7, 2026 [^1].
