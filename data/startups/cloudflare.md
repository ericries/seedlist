## About

Cloudflare is a web infrastructure and cybersecurity company founded in 2009 by Matthew Prince, Michelle Zatlyn, and Lee Holloway [^1]. The company provides content delivery network, DDoS mitigation, and internet security services. Cloudflare launched publicly at TechCrunch Disrupt in September 2010 [^1]. The company went public on the New York Stock Exchange on September 13, 2019 [^2].
