## About

Reliable Robotics is a Mountain View, California-based provider of autonomous aircraft systems for commercial and defense aviation, founded in 2017 by SpaceX alumni Robert Rose (CEO) and Juerg Frefel [^1][^2]. The company is developing certified aircraft autonomy that enables uncrewed cargo flights, and is pursuing FAA certification for its first commercial deployment [^2]. The April 2026 round brings total funding to over $160M and values the company near $1 billion [^3].
