## About

Mistral AI is a Paris-based foundation model company founded in April 2023 by Arthur Mensch (formerly Google DeepMind), Guillaume Lample, and Timothee Lacroix (both formerly Meta) [^1]. The three co-founders met during their studies at Ecole Polytechnique [^1]. Mistral builds and deploys large language models, positioning itself as Europe's leading AI company with a valuation of $14 billion [^2].

## What Investors Say

ASML CEO Christophe Fouquet on leading the Series C: "ASML is proud to enter a strategic partnership with Mistral AI, and to be lead investor in this funding round. The collaboration between Mistral AI and ASML aims to generate clear benefits for ASML customers through innovative products and solutions enabled by AI" [^2].

## What Founders Say

Arthur Mensch on the ASML partnership: "This investment brings together two technology leaders operating in the same value chain. We have the ambition to help ASML and its numerous partners solve current and future engineering challenges through AI, and ultimately to advance the full semiconductor and AI value chain" [^2].

Arthur Mensch on company independence: "We are not for sale. We left big US tech to create a company in Europe, to show that Europe had something to say. That's still the project and so the independence we have is something that we value dearly" [^5].
