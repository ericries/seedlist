## About

NoTraffic is an AI-powered traffic management platform that enables cities to optimize traffic flow in real time [^1]. The company's autonomous traffic management system uses AI and sensor fusion to manage intersections, reducing congestion, emissions, and improving safety [^1][^2]. NoTraffic's platform is deployed across hundreds of intersections in cities throughout the United States and globally [^1].
