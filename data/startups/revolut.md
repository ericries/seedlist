## About

Revolut is a digital banking platform founded in 2015 by Nik Storonsky and Vlad Yatsenko in London [^1]. The company offers multi-currency accounts, money transfers, crypto trading, and other financial services to over 65 million customers across 100 countries [^2]. Revolut was valued at $75 billion as of November 2025 [^2].
