## About

Hightouch is a San Francisco-based data and AI platform for marketing, originally known for pioneering the "Composable CDP" category and now positioned as an agentic marketing platform that lets enterprises run AI agents directly on top of their warehouse data [^1][^2]. Customers include Domino's, PetSmart, DraftKings, Ramp, Whoop, Autotrader, Cars.com, and Aritzia [^1][^3]. The company says it has grown more than 100% in each of the past two years [^1][^3].
