## About

Coral is a New York-based AI platform automating specialty-healthcare administrative work — reading handwritten fax forms, processing prior authorizations, and handling patient intakes in under five minutes [^1][^2]. Founded in 2024 by Ajay Shrihari and Aniket Mohanty.
