## About

Armorblox was a cybersecurity company that used natural language understanding (NLU) and large language models to protect enterprise communications from threats such as business email compromise, phishing, and data loss [^1]. Founded in 2017 in Sunnyvale, California, the company emerged from stealth in February 2019 [^1]. Armorblox's platform integrated via API with email and communication tools including Office 365, Google Workspace, Slack, and OneDrive to analyze textual content and detect socially engineered attacks [^1]. The company served over 9,000 organizations [^3]. Cisco announced its intent to acquire Armorblox on May 31, 2023, and completed the acquisition on July 14, 2023 [^2][^5]. The Armorblox team joined Cisco's Security Business Group to integrate generative AI capabilities across Cisco's security portfolio [^2].
