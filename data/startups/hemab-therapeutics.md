## About

Hemab Therapeutics is a clinical-stage biotechnology company focused on developing prophylactic and on-demand therapies for serious and underserved bleeding and clotting disorders [^1]. The company is dual-headquartered in Cambridge, Massachusetts and Copenhagen, Denmark, and is led by CEO Benny Sorensen, MD, PhD [^1]. Its pipeline includes sutacimig for Glanzmann thrombasthenia and Factor VII deficiency, and HMB-002 for von Willebrand disease [^1].
