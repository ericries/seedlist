## About

ANEUVO is a Los Angeles-based developer of non-invasive neuromodulation technology, founded in 2016 by UCLA neuroengineering experts Yi-Kai Lo and Wentai Liu [^1][^2]. The company advances the ExaStim Stimulation System, a portable bioelectronic device designed for functional neurorehabilitation in patients with spinal cord injuries [^1].
