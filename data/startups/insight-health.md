## About

Insight Health develops patient-facing AI assistants that automate healthcare administrative workflows, including processing referrals, contacting patients to collect clinical history, and scheduling routine procedures [^1]. The company has completed more than 3 million autonomous clinical conversations with patients and partners with clinics including The Oregon Clinic, Pacific Sports & Spine, and Santiam Hospital [^1][^2].
