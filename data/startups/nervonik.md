## About

Nervonik is a Los Angeles-based medical device company developing peripheral nerve stimulation (PNS) technology for chronic pain treatment [^1][^2]. The platform applies controlled electrical currents to peripheral nerves through leads connected to miniaturized implantable pulse generators (IPGs), powered by a small programmable wearable device [^1]. The April 2026 oversubscribed Series B funds continued development and commercialization of the PNS system, including proprietary sensing capabilities [^1].
