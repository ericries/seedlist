## About

Marloo is a London-based AI software platform for financial advisers, wealth managers, and insurance and mortgage professionals, founded in 2024 by Hardy Michel, Shakeel Lala, and Ben Robertson [^1][^2]. The platform records and summarizes meetings, generates client notes and compliance documents, and centralizes client data [^1]. The company reports 42% month-on-month revenue growth for 11 consecutive months and onboarded 650+ advisory firms across the UK, Canada, Australia, Ireland, South Africa, and New Zealand within twelve months [^1].
