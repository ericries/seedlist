## About

Cents is a fully integrated software, hardware, and payments technology platform for the laundry industry [^1]. The company combines AI-native business management software, proprietary hardware, and integrated payments, serving over 4,500 laundromat locations across the U.S. and processing $1 billion in payments annually [^1]. The Series C round is the largest single software investment in the laundry vertical to date [^1].
