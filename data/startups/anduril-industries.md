## About

Anduril Industries is a defense technology company founded in June 2017 by Palmer Luckey (co-founder of Oculus VR), Trae Stephens (formerly of Founders Fund and Palantir), Brian Schimpf (former Palantir engineering director), Matt Grimm, and Joseph Chen [^1]. The company builds autonomous systems, AI-powered surveillance, and weapons platforms for the U.S. Department of Defense and allied governments [^1]. As of 2025, Anduril reported approximately $2.1 billion in revenue [^2].
