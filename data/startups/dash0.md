## About

Dash0 is an agentic observability platform built natively on OpenTelemetry, founded in 2023 by Mirko Novakovic [^1]. Originally founded in Germany, the company is now headquartered in New York [^1]. Dash0 has grown to more than 600 paying customers, including global brands such as Zalando, Taco Bell, and The Telegraph [^2]. The platform aims to evolve from observability into an autonomous nervous system for production — an AI-powered platform that not only monitors systems but actively manages them [^1].
