## About

Canva is an online graphic design platform founded on January 1, 2013, in Sydney, Australia, by Melanie Perkins, Cliff Obrecht, and Cameron Adams [^1]. The idea originated from Perkins and Obrecht's earlier company Fusion Books, an online yearbook design tool launched in 2007 [^2]. Canva's platform enables non-designers to create professional-quality graphics using drag-and-drop tools and templates. The company has raised approximately $572 million in venture funding and was valued at $40 billion as of its September 2021 round [^3] [^4].
