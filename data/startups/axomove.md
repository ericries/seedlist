## About

Axomove is a Lille-based digital health company providing prevention and rehabilitation software, deployed at hospitals, employers, and insurers [^1][^2]. The platform serves 75,000 users across 150 B2B clients, including Air France, Thales, Allianz, and Lille University Hospital, and benefits from French Social Security reimbursement for low-back-pain patients [^1].
