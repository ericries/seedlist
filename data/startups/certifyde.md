## About

Certifyde is a New York–based AI adoption platform whose flagship Productivity Companion is a browser extension that overlays Salesforce, Slack, Google Workspace, and other tools to deliver role-specific contextual AI guidance in the flow of work [^1][^2].

## What Investors Say

George Ruan, Honey co-founder: "Certifyde brings that same in-workflow intelligence to the modern workforce, training thousands of healthcare professionals in real time" [^2].

## What Founders Say

Skylar Hauswirth, Co-Founder & CEO: "AI fluency is the capability to use AI as a natural, integrated part of their daily work where tasks flow with the use of AI. Like a seamless handoff between teammates, with fluency, AI picks up where the human leaves off" [^3].
