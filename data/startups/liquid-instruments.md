## About

Liquid Instruments is a software-defined test and measurement company that consolidates multiple instruments into a single reconfigurable platform, enabling users to create and deploy custom measurement solutions in minutes [^1][^2]. Headquartered in Canberra and San Diego, the company is targeting aerospace, defense, and semiconductor verticals [^1][^2]. As part of the April 2026 Series C, Keysight Technologies entered into a commercial arrangement to accelerate AI-driven instrumentation alongside the equity investment [^1].
