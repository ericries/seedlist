## About

Ideally is an Auckland-based AI-powered consumer insights and innovation platform offering overnight concept and message testing for brands and agencies [^1]. Founded in August 2023 by James Donald (CEO), Brendan Cervin (CTO), and Joshua Nu'u-Steele (CRO). The company reports 250+ brand customers and ~$6.3M in 2025 revenue, with offices in Sydney, Melbourne, and New York [^1].
