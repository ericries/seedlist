## About

Crosby is an AI-native law firm that pairs licensed attorneys with proprietary AI software to review commercial contracts at fixed per-document pricing [^1]. The company has reviewed over 13,000 contracts and achieved 400% revenue growth since October 2025 [^1][^2].
