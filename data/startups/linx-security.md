## About

Linx Security is a 100-person identity security startup founded in 2023 and headquartered in New York [^1]. The company provides an AI-native identity security and governance platform that monitors and controls all types of identities — human, non-human, and AI agents — across enterprises [^1]. Linx delivers unified visibility, continuous risk detection, and autonomous remediation, and has signed multimillion-dollar contracts with banks, healthcare companies, and Fortune 500 firms governing millions of identities globally [^1]. The company recently introduced Linx Autopilot, an autonomous AI agent for identity governance [^1].
