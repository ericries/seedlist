## About

Sigma Automate is an AI-native IT automation platform that emerged from stealth on April 9, 2026 [^1]. The company offers no-code orchestration with visual workflow builders and autonomous self-healing capabilities for enterprise IT teams [^1][^2]. CEO Richard Shaaya previously led IT operations and cybersecurity teams at The Home Depot, WellStar Health System, Corning, and Volkswagen [^2].
