## About

Serif Biomedicines is a Cambridge-based biotech founded in 2021 within Flagship Labs that is developing modified DNA as a new class of genetic medicine, integrating nucleic acid chemistry, synthetic biology, delivery science, and AI to create programmable, scalable, durable, and redosable DNA-based therapeutics [^1][^2]. Modified DNA aims to combine the best features of mRNA and gene therapy while mitigating their limitations [^1].
