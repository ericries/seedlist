## About

Notion is a productivity and collaboration platform founded in 2013 by Ivan Zhao and Simon Last [^1]. The company provides an all-in-one workspace for notes, docs, project management, and wikis, and has grown to over 100 million users [^2]. Notion was valued at $11 billion as of January 2026 [^3].
