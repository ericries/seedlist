## About

Avoca is a New York-based AI workforce platform for service businesses, founded in 2023 by Tyson Chen and Apurva Shrivastava [^1][^2]. The company builds AI agents that handle chat, email, voice calls, and SMS across the customer journey for HVAC, plumbing, automotive, moving, and other home-services operators [^1]. The April 2026 announcement disclosed $125M+ raised across Seed, Series A, and Series B at a $1B valuation, with customers including Turnpoint, 1-800-GOT-JUNK?, and Goettl, and Avoca on track to book $1B in jobs in 2026 [^1][^2].
