## About

Trayd is a construction payroll and compliance platform offering an all-in-one back-office operating system with payroll, HR, scheduling, and field tracking tools for specialty trade contractors [^1]. Founded in 2021 by CEO Anna Berger and CTO Cara Kessler, the company has reduced average weekly payroll processing time from 14 hours to 27 minutes and achieved over 600% YoY revenue growth [^1].
