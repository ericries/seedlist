## About

Wiliot is an Israeli/U.S. IoT company developing self-powered, battery-free Bluetooth sensors that enable Physical AI for supply chains, providing location and condition monitoring for products and packaging [^1][^2]. The April 2026 strategic investment from Avery Dennison Corporation is a returning investor and brings Avery Dennison onto the board [^1][^2].
