## About

Dropbox is a cloud storage and file-sharing platform founded in 2007 by MIT students Drew Houston and Arash Ferdowsi [^1][^2]. The company participated in Y Combinator's Summer 2007 batch, receiving an initial $15,000 investment for 7% equity [^2][^3]. Sequoia Capital provided $1.2 million in convertible debt shortly after [^3]. Dropbox went public on the Nasdaq on March 23, 2018 [^1]. Sequoia Capital owned approximately 25% of the company before the IPO, yielding a return of $2-3 billion on its early investments [^3].
