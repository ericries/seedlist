## About

Snabbit is a Bengaluru-based on-demand home services startup founded in 2024 by Aayush Agarwal, a former Zepto executive [^2][^4]. The platform processes over 40,000 jobs daily across 15,000+ women service professionals in 140 micromarkets across Mumbai, Delhi NCR, Bengaluru, Pune, and Hyderabad, offering cleaning, dishwashing, and laundry services [^1][^2]. The April 2026 Series D valued the company at roughly $350-360M (up from $180M six months prior); the company scaled from approximately 400 jobs per day at the start of FY26 to over 40,000 by year-end [^1][^2].
