## About

Snabbit is a Bengaluru-based on-demand home services startup processing 40,000+ jobs daily across 15,000+ workers in five Indian cities, offering cleaning, dishwashing, and laundry services [^1]. The April 2026 Series D valued the company at roughly $350M (up from $180M six months prior); losses per order declined ~50% and customer-acquisition costs ~65% [^1].
