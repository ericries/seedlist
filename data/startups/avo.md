## About

Avo is a clinical AI platform that transforms Electronic Health Records into intelligent systems of action [^1]. The platform synthesizes patient data, proposes diagnoses and care plans, assists with orders and documentation, and surfaces evidence-based guidance through copilots like Chart Assist and Ask Avo [^1]. Avo integrates with major EHR systems including Epic, athenahealth, and MEDITECH [^2].
