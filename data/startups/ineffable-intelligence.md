## About

Ineffable Intelligence is a UK-based AI lab pursuing artificial superintelligence through reinforcement learning, founded in late 2025 by David Silver, former lead of DeepMind's reinforcement learning team and a UCL professor [^1][^2]. The company is building a "superlearner" designed to acquire knowledge and skills from its own experience rather than human-generated data, extending the self-play paradigm Silver pioneered with AlphaZero [^1][^2]. The April 2026 round at $5.1B valuation is reportedly Europe's largest seed financing on record [^2][^3].
