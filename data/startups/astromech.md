## About

Astromech is a Dallas-based AI-biotech company spun out of Colossal Biosciences, building a predictive engine for biology that aims to forecast evolution, disease risk, and system vulnerabilities before they happen [^1]. Co-founded by Ben Lamm and Harvard geneticist George Church, the company initially raised $30 million in seed funding in September 2025 [^2]. A $10.5 million seed extension brought total funding to $40.5 million, with the company reaching a reported $2 billion valuation just nine months after spin-out [^1].
