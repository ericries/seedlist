## About

Flipboard is a social magazine and news aggregation app co-founded in January 2010 by Mike McCue and Evan Doll [^1][^2]. McCue previously co-founded Tellme Networks (acquired by Microsoft in 2007) and Doll was an early engineer on the iPhone team at Apple [^1]. The company launched in July 2010 on iPad, reimagining how people consume content from social networks and the web in a magazine-style layout [^2][^3]. Apple named Flipboard its iPad App of the Year in 2010 [^1]. The company is headquartered in Palo Alto, California [^3]. Flipboard has raised approximately $236 million in total funding across six rounds [^4].
