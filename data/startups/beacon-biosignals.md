## About

Beacon Biosignals is a Boston, Massachusetts-based neurotechnology company founded in 2019 [^1]. The company combines FDA-cleared wearable EEG technology with advanced AI to capture and analyze real-world neural data, enabling breakthroughs across neurology, psychiatry, and sleep medicine [^1]. Co-founded by Jacob Donoghue (CEO), Jarrett Revels, Sydney Cash, and Brandon Westover, the company upsized its Series B financing to over $97 million in April 2026, bringing cumulative funding to over $132 million [^1].
