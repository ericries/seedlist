## About

Sillage is a Paris-based GTM (go-to-market) agent platform that helps enterprise sales teams detect and act on buying signals from social media, hiring data, and competitor activity [^1][^2]. The company's AI agents surface high-value leads and deliver real-time signals inside Slack, CRMs, and other sales tools, enabling teams to run warm outbound strategies [^2]. Sillage was founded in 2025 by Arnaud Weiss (CEO) and Arthur Coudouy, and was selected for STATION F's Future 40 cohort and F/AI acceleration program [^2][^3]. Early customers include LumApps, Modjo, and Yoobic [^2].
