## About

Kumo.AI is a predictive AI platform that enables users to train and run graph neural network (GNN) models on relational data in enterprise data warehouses [^1]. The company was founded in 2021 by Vanja Josifovski (former CTO at Airbnb Homes and Pinterest), Jure Leskovec (Stanford professor and co-creator of PyTorch Geometric), and Hema Raghavan (former Sr. Director of Engineering at LinkedIn) [^1][^2]. Kumo emerged from stealth in April 2022 [^2]. The core graph ML technology underpinning the platform was developed over five years through Stanford and Dortmund University AI labs and the PyTorch Geometric (PyG) open-source framework [^2]. The company is based in Mountain View, California [^3].
