## About

Saronic is an Austin, Texas-based autonomous shipbuilder developing uncrewed surface vessels for the U.S. Navy and allied forces [^1][^2]. The company builds autonomous ships designed to modernize naval fleets, with a focus on maritime autonomy technology [^2]. Saronic secured a $392 million U.S. Navy contract and plans to build more than 20 ships per year by 2027 [^1][^3]. The company is developing a new shipyard, dubbed Port Alpha, with Texas as a leading candidate for its location [^1].
