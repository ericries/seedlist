## About

Manna Air Delivery is a Dublin, Ireland-based drone delivery company operating one of the most active consumer drone delivery networks in the world, with more than 250,000 regulated commercial UAV flights completed [^1][^2]. Founded by Bobby Healy, the company has operated in six locations across Ireland, Finland, and Texas over seven years, delivering items including books, medical supplies, food, and clothing [^1]. Manna has partnerships with Uber, Deliveroo, Just Eat, and DoorDash [^1][^2].
