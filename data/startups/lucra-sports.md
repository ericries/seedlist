## About

Lucra Sports is a Palo Alto-based gamification platform that transforms corporate loyalty programs into interactive esports-style competitions, founded by CEO Dylan Robbins [^1][^2]. The platform enables tournaments, head-to-head competition, and cash or merchandise prizes; customers include Five Iron Golf, Chess Kings, and Dave & Buster's [^1]. The April 2026 round marked ARK Invest Venture Fund's first-ever lead investment in an early-stage startup [^1].
