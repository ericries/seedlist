## About

Variance is an agentic AI platform that autonomously executes fraud, risk, compliance, and KYC/AML workflows for financial institutions and Fortune 500 companies [^1]. Founded by Karine Mellata and Michael Lin, who previously worked together on Apple's fraud engineering and algorithmic risk team, the platform processes more than 70 million context signals per day and executes approximately 300,000 automated enforcement actions across customer environments [^1][^2].
