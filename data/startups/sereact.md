## About

Sereact is a Stuttgart-based AI robotics company building vision-language-action models that enable any robot to adapt to new tasks using natural language [^1][^2]. The platform is live in over 200 robotic systems across Europe, powering operations for industrial customers including BMW, Daimler Truck, PepsiCo, and H&M [^1][^2]. The Series B will fund development of new AI capabilities and a new U.S. headquarters in Boston [^1].
