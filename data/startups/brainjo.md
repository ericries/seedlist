## About

brainjo is a Regensburg, Germany-based digital health startup developing VR-based therapeutic software that complements traditional care, with its first product a prescribable digital health app (DiGA) for children with ADHD [^1][^2]. The platform translates clinical therapy content into immersive VR environments where children practice coping skills through structured tasks and rewards, and is being developed in partnership with MEDICE, a European pharmaceutical company specializing in ADHD medication [^1]. Founded in 2022 by Markus Wensauer and Christian Gnerlich, brainjo is running a clinical study with roughly 100 children and targets DiGA market approval in Germany by 2028 [^1].
