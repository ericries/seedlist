## About

Ambient Clinical Analytics is a Rochester, MN-based company developing clinical analytics, decision support, and workflow tools [^1]. The company's flagship product is the AWARE platform, an FDA Class II-cleared system built on clinically validated algorithms and technology licensed from the Mayo Clinic [^1]. The platform delivers real-time clinical analytics and clinical decision support across bedside care, command centers, and remote monitoring [^1].
