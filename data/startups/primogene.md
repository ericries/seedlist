## About

Primogene is a Leipzig-based biotech startup, founded in 2023 by Dr.-Ing. Reza Mahour, Valerian Grote, and Linda Karger, that uses a proprietary enzymatic platform to produce nature-identical bioactive molecules at industrial scale for nutrition, personal care, and pharmaceutical raw materials [^1][^2]. Markets include infant nutrition, adult health, pharma, and personal care [^1].
