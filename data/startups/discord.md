## About

Discord is a communication platform originally built for gamers, founded by Jason Citron and Stan Vishnevskiy. The platform launched publicly on May 13, 2015, under the name Hammer & Chisel [^1]. Citron previously founded OpenFeint, a social gaming platform, while Vishnevskiy founded Guildwork [^1]. Discord grew to become a general-purpose communication platform used by communities of all types. The company has raised approximately $978 million in venture funding and was valued at $15 billion as of its 2021 Series I round [^2].
