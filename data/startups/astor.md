## About

Astor is an SEC-registered, AI-native investment advisory platform that connects to users' existing brokerage accounts and delivers personalized investment recommendations through conversational AI [^1][^2]. Founded by Bruno Koba (CEO, former Monashees fintech investor and Nubank data scientist) and Daniel Tulha (former Stripe and Robinhood software engineer), the platform has attracted thousands of users and over $200M in connected accounts since launch [^1][^2].
