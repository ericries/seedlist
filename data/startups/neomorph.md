## About

Neomorph is a San Diego-based biotechnology company developing molecular glue degraders to target previously undruggable proteins across oncology, cardiometabolic disease, rare disease, neurology, and immunology [^1]. The company's lead program, NEO-811, induces targeted degradation of the ARNT (HIF-1β) protein and is currently in a Phase 1/2 clinical trial for locally advanced or metastatic clear cell renal cell carcinoma [^1]. Neomorph has a $1.64 billion collaboration with AbbVie for molecular glue discovery [^2].
