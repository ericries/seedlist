## About

Remission Medical is a virtual rheumatology platform based in Henrico, Virginia, that contracts directly with health systems to embed Advanced Practice Providers for specialty care delivery [^1]. The company operates RemissionOS, an AI-native workforce platform managing provider capacity, scheduling, documentation, and revenue cycle operations across multiple health systems [^1]. Founded in 2021 by Blake Wehman, the company launched from Richmond, Virginia, initially offering virtual-first rheumatology services [^2]. Current health system partners include Sentara, OrthoVirginia, WellSpan, Ochsner, Campbell Clinic, Gundersen, MultiCare, and St. Francis [^1]. Remission Medical has also entered a know-how agreement with Mayo Clinic to transform rheumatology care delivery [^3].
