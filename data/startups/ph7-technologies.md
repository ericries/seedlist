## About

pH7 Technologies is a Vancouver-based cleantech company that uses a proprietary closed-loop organo-electrochemical process to recover metals from low-grade ores, tailings, and complex feedstocks that have historically been uneconomic to process [^1]. The company has commercialized its technology at its Vancouver facility for extraction of Platinum Group Metals from spent catalysts and is now expanding into copper extraction for the mining sector [^1]. pH7 has also secured venture debt financing from RBCx [^2].
