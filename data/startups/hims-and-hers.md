## About

Hims & Hers Health is a telehealth and direct-to-consumer wellness company founded in November 2017 by Andrew Dudum, Jack Abraham, Hilary Coles, and Joe Spector as part of the Atomic Labs venture studio portfolio [^1][^2]. The company provides access to treatments for hair loss, skin care, sexual health, and mental health. Hims went public via a SPAC merger with Oaktree Acquisition Corp. (NYSE: OAC) on January 21, 2021, at an enterprise value of approximately $1.6 billion [^3]. It trades on the NYSE under the ticker HIMS.
