## About

Hims & Hers Health is a telehealth and direct-to-consumer wellness company founded in 2017 by Andrew Dudum, Jack Abraham, and Hilary Coles as part of the Atomic Labs portfolio [^1]. The company provides access to treatments for hair loss, skin care, sexual health, and mental health, and went public via a SPAC merger with Oaktree Acquisition Corp in January 2021 at a $1.6 billion valuation [^2]. It trades on the NYSE under the ticker HIMS.
