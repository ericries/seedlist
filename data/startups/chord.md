## About

Chord is an AI context platform for AI-driven commerce, built by the team behind Glossier — unifying data, decisions, and actions into a single system that lets AI run core business operations across mid-market and enterprise commerce brands [^1][^2]. Customers include MrBeast, Ritual, Ruggable, Rodan + Fields, and Blue Bottle Coffee, collectively representing over $1B in annual revenue [^1][^2].
