## About

Auctor is a New York-based AI-native platform for the enterprise software implementation lifecycle, enabling professional services teams and system integrators to deliver faster and more consistently [^1]. The platform curates execution-ready artifacts including rough orders of magnitude, resource plans, process flows, and user stories, with customers reporting upwards of 80% efficiency gains across discovery and design [^1]. Auctor is a Y Combinator alumnus [^1].
