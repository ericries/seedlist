## About

Sift is an El Segundo, California-based startup building the intelligence layer for mission-critical machines, transforming raw sensor data from hardware systems into structured, queryable data [^1]. Co-founded by former SpaceX engineers Karthik Gollapudi and Austin Spiegel, the platform serves space, defense, manufacturing, and autonomous vehicle industries by enabling both engineers and AI systems to monitor and operate complex hardware systems [^1][^2].
