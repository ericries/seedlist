## About

Wealth.com is an AI-powered platform for estate and tax planning serving wealth management firms and financial advisors [^1]. Its core technology, Ester Intelligence, is a proprietary AI engine built specifically for estate planning, tax planning, and advanced wealth scenarios [^1]. The company has recorded at least 3x revenue growth annually over each of the past four years and has agreements with three of the top five U.S. domestic banks [^1].
