## About

Syneron Bio is a Beijing-based biotech company specializing in intelligent platform-driven macrocyclic peptide drug discovery [^1]. The company operates the proprietary Synova platform and develops treatments across oncology, autoimmune, metabolic, and rare diseases [^1]. The company previously closed nearly $100M in Series A and A+ rounds with backing from AstraZeneca and its China joint fund [^2].
