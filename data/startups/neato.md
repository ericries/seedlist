## About

Neato is a Las Vegas-based second-party (2P) e-commerce platform that buys inventory directly from brands and manages the full lifecycle of selling it online, from listings and creative to advertising, logistics, and reporting [^1]. Founded in 2018 by Anthony Connelly, the company manages a portfolio of large CPG brands across pet care, hard goods, grocery, beauty, supplements, and personal care categories, primarily on Amazon [^1].
