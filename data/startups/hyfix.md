## About

HYFIX Spatial Intelligence is a Santa Clara-based semiconductor company developing a U.S.-made system-on-a-chip (SoC) platform for drones and autonomous systems, integrating flight control, positioning, communications, and onboard computing into a single chip [^1]. The company positions its technology as a U.S. alternative to Chinese drone electronics [^2].
