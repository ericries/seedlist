## About

Terremoto Biosciences is a biotechnology company leveraging lysine-based covalency to develop selective AKT1-inhibitor medicines for oncology and hereditary hemorrhagic telangiectasia [^1]. The company plans to initiate Phase 1 clinical trials for its lead candidates in the coming months [^1].
