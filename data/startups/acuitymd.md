## About

AcuityMD is a Boston-based AI-enabled commercial intelligence platform for the medical device industry, founded in 2020 and led by CEO Mike Monovoukas [^1][^2]. The platform aggregates data into a proprietary MedTech ontology that maps physicians, facilities, networks, procedures, and reimbursement dynamics, supporting commercial teams at 16 of the top 20 MedTech companies and over 400 customers [^1]. The April 2026 Series C closed at a $955M post-money valuation, bringing total funding to over $160M [^1][^2].
