## About

Ambrosia Biosciences is a Boulder, Colorado-based drug discovery company developing orally delivered, small molecule-based therapies for obesity and other cardiometabolic disorders [^1]. The company's lead program is an oral small molecule GLP-1 receptor agonist, positioning it to compete in the growing obesity therapeutics market beyond current peptide-based treatments [^2].
