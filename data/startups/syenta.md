## About

Syenta is a Sydney, Australia-based next-generation semiconductor company spun out of the Australian National University, developing Localised Electrochemical Manufacturing (LEM) — a lithography-free process for high-density chip-to-chip interconnects [^1][^2][^3]. Early results indicate micron-scale interconnects with up to 40% fewer process steps versus conventional advanced-packaging methods, without requiring major changes to existing manufacturing infrastructure [^1]. The company plans to begin semiconductor production in 2027 and is expanding into the United States [^1][^2]. Pat Gelsinger, former Intel CEO and General Partner at Playground Global, joined Syenta's board as part of the round [^1][^3].
