## About

VNYX is an Amsterdam-based fashion technology company developing an AI-powered robot that automates the processing of unique garments for online resale of overstock, returns, and secondhand clothing [^1][^2]. The company aims to make resale more profitable than dumping or burning excess inventory and was a winner of the Amsterdam Circular Changemaker Award [^1][^2].
