## About

Copperhelm is a Tel Aviv-based cybersecurity company building an agentic cloud security platform in which AI agents continuously monitor cloud environments, investigate threats, and execute real-time remediation while keeping security teams in control [^1][^2]. Founded in late 2025 by CEO Shimon Tolts, CPO Eyar Zilberman, and CTO Roman Labunsky, the company emerged from stealth on April 23, 2026 with $7M in seed funding and says it is already working with paying Fortune 500 customers [^1].
