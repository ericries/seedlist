## About

Moda is an AI design platform that gives every professional a brand-aligned design agent, creating presentations, social posts, reports, and other visual assets on a fully editable canvas with layers [^1]. Founded by three repeat founders with combined exits worth over $1.2B: Anvisha Pai (Dover), Ravi Parikh (Heap, Airplane/Airtable), and John Holliman (Expanse/Palo Alto Networks) [^1].
