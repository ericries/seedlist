## About

LighthouseAI is a Los Angeles-based platform serving as the central system for pharmaceutical licensing and compliance across manufacturers, distributors, logistics providers, and pharmacies [^1]. Founded by CEO Sumeet Singh, the company reports doubling its customer base since January 2025 to 250 customers, including 15+ pharmaceutical enterprises with over $1 billion in annual revenue [^1].
