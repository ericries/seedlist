## About

Scalvy is a distributed power delivery company creating next-generation power systems for AI data centers, energy storage, and electric mobility [^1]. Its patented Power Neuron platform delivers ultra-high, grid-interactive power in a compact footprint, reducing cost, complexity, and risk as systems grow [^1].
