## About

Synaps is a Vienna, Austria-based AI design startup building what it describes as "the AI canvas for architectural design" — a browser-based, multiplayer design tool aimed at architects and interior designers, often described as "Figma for architects" and pitched as a challenger to AutoCAD [^1][^2][^4]. The company was founded by three Albanian-born entrepreneurs — Brendon Ahmeti (CEO), Kevin Çobaj, and Agron Bajraktari — who met in Vienna [^1][^3][^5].

The product combines a multiplayer canvas, AI-powered floor plan tracing, asset libraries, and AI draft-to-scene rendering, intended to compress workflows that traditionally span multiple desktop applications [^4][^1]. As of the April 2026 funding announcement, the company reported approximately 60,000 total users, around 1,500 daily active users, and hundreds of paying customers, while still in beta [^1].
