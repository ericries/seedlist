## About

ElevenLabs is an AI audio research and deployment company co-founded in 2022 by Mati Staniszewski (ex-Palantir) and Piotr Dabkowski (ex-Google ML engineer), childhood friends from Poland who later studied at universities in the UK [^1][^2]. The company's AI models generate realistic speech synthesis, voice cloning, dubbing, and audio content in over 30 languages [^3]. ElevenLabs is headquartered in New York with offices in London and Warsaw, and had approximately 400 employees as of early 2026 [^4][^5]. The company closed 2025 with over $330 million in ARR [^5].

## What Investors Say

"We are strong believers that generative AI tools will revolutionize the creative suite — unlocking more creativity by enabling professionals to produce more content with higher quality." — Jennifer Li, Bryan Kim, and Justine Moore, a16z, Series A investment announcement [^8].

"Mati and Piotr are exceptional founders and leaders. They have built ElevenLabs into one of the most successful and most impactful companies in the global AI ecosystem. From world-leading research to mind-bending creative tools to enterprise voice agents at scale, ElevenLabs is changing how people communicate and interface with technology, with organizations and institutions, and with each other." — Andrew Reed, Partner at Sequoia Capital, on joining the board at Series D [^13].
