## About

ZYNT is a Warsaw-based AI-powered B2B sales intelligence platform founded by Cezar Reszel, who previously helped scale Booksy to unicorn status [^1][^2]. The platform monitors public sources — news, job postings, funding databases, social signals, and tender announcements — to surface contextualized buying signals for B2B sales teams, integrating with HubSpot and Salesforce [^2]. The company targets sales teams across Poland and Central/Eastern Europe, positioning itself as a done-for-you alternative to enterprise sales intelligence tools [^2].
