## About

Aaru is a New York-based AI research platform founded in 2024 that deploys simulated agents to predict consumer and political behavior [^1]. The company was founded by Cameron Fink, Edward Koh, and John Kessler [^1]. The $80M Series A, announced April 23, 2026, was led by Redpoint Ventures and brings total funding to $88M [^1].
