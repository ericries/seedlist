## About

Nox Mobility is a Berlin-based startup reinventing European night trains with fully private rooms at prices comparable to short-haul flights [^1][^2]. The company was founded by CEO Janek Smalla (formerly at FlixTrain and Bolt) and CMO Thibault Constant (known for the Simply Railway YouTube platform), with Artur Hasselbach joining as CFO and co-founder in January 2026 [^3]. Nox plans to launch its first route in 2027 and aims to expand to 100 routes across Europe by 2036, targeting cities including Amsterdam, Paris, Barcelona, Stockholm, Berlin, Vienna, and Rome [^1][^2].
