## About

Naturbeads is a Bath, UK-based materials startup, spun out of the University of Bath in 2018 by Professor Davide Mattia, the late Professor Janet Scott, and Dr Giovanna Laudisio [^1][^2]. Its patented process turns cellulose into uniform biodegradable microspheres that mimic the size, shape, and performance of plastic microparticles, targeted at cosmetics, paints, coatings, detergents, adhesives, leather, and biomedical devices [^1][^2]. A €4.1M EU grant, announced April 23, 2026, will fund Naturbeads' first commercial plant in Puglia, Italy, scheduled to start commercial production in June 2026 [^1][^2]. The grant adds to the company's £7.8M Series A, which closed in November 2024 with Eos Advisory, Progress Tech Transfer, and CDP Venture Capital [^1][^2].
