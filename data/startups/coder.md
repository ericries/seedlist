## About

Coder is an Austin-based company providing AI development infrastructure for enterprises [^1]. Founded in 2017, the company offers a centralized, secure platform where enterprises can build and run software development environments in the cloud, replacing fragmented local setups with standardized, customer-controlled workspaces [^1].

| 2026-04-01 | Series C | $90M | KKR | Qube Research & Technologies, Uncork Capital [^1] |

Both KKR and QRT are also customers of Coder [^1]. Proceeds will support platform innovation for enterprise AI workflows and geographic expansion across Europe, Asia, and North America [^1].
