## About

Nexthop AI is a Santa Clara, California-based developer of custom-engineered networking hardware, software, and optics for AI data centers [^1]. Founded in 2023 by Anshul Sadana, who spent 17 years at Arista Networks as COO scaling sales from $0 to over $5B [^1]. The company combines hyperscale-grade performance, open software, and a co-development model built in lockstep with the world's largest AI operators [^1]. Nexthop has additional offices in Seattle, Vancouver, and Bangalore [^2].
