## About

Mosaic SoC is a Zurich-based ETH spinout building dedicated perception chips that deliver real-time spatial intelligence on energy-constrained devices such as AR glasses, wearables, and smartphone cameras [^1][^2]. The company was co-founded by Moritz Scherer and Alfio Di Mauro, both PhDs from ETH Zurich with deep expertise in system-on-chip architecture [^1][^2].
