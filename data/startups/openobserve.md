## About

OpenObserve is an AI-native observability platform offering open-source tools for logs, metrics, and traces, designed to compete with established observability vendors like Datadog and Splunk on cost and openness [^1][^2]. The company was founded in 2022 by Prabhat Sharma and is headquartered in San Francisco [^2][^3]. The open-source platform is used by more than 6,000 organizations — including many Fortune 100 companies — and the project has accumulated more than 18,000 stars on GitHub [^1][^2].

Alongside its Series A, OpenObserve announced "Observability 3.0," a set of AI-native capabilities including an autonomous AI SRE, anomaly detection, and LLM observability [^1][^2].
