## About

Cloaked is a Boston-based consumer privacy platform founded in 2020 by brothers Arjun and Abhijay Bhatnagar [^1][^3]. The platform generates unlimited unique phone numbers, email addresses, and passwords on demand, automatically removes personal information from data brokers, and provides AI-powered call screening [^2]. Cloaked has grown to more than 350,000 users and stops millions of threats per day [^2].
