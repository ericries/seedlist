## About

Vivacta Bio (Vivacta Biotechnology (Shanghai) Co., Ltd.) is a Shanghai-based biotechnology company developing in vivo CAR-T cell therapies for cancer and autoimmune diseases [^1][^2]. The company was founded and is led by Dr. Liu Yarong [^1]. Its lead program, GT801, is a next-generation in vivo CAR-T therapy that has shown early clinical signal in hematologic malignancies and autoimmune conditions [^1][^2].

On April 29, 2026, Vivacta announced the close of Series A and Series A+ financing totaling over US$50 million [^1][^2][^3]. The Series A was led by Decheng Capital and the Series A+ was led by Loyal Valley Capital [^1][^2]. Participating investors included OrbiMed, Hankang Capital, Eisai Innovation Inc., and C&D Emerging Industry Equity Investment, alongside existing shareholders Qiming Venture Partners, Beijing Shunxi, and Apricot Capital [^1][^2]. Proceeds will fund GT801 clinical trials, regulatory submissions, research team expansion, and global operations [^1].
