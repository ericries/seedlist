## About

Techcyte is an AI-powered digital diagnostics platform for anatomic and clinical pathology, headquartered in Orem, Utah [^1]. The company's Fusion platform integrates AI, digitized workflows, and laboratory system interoperability across human, veterinary, and environmental pathology segments [^1] [^2]. Techcyte's veterinary business is currently profitable, and the company expects its environmental and human segments to reach profitability within the next year [^1].

The 2026 round includes access to 17 million de-identified Mayo Clinic slides as a training dataset to improve Techcyte's AI diagnostic accuracy [^2].
