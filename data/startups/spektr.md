## About

Spektr is a Copenhagen-based fintech compliance startup that uses AI agents to automate KYC and KYB compliance workflows for financial institutions [^1]. The founders previously created and sold the digital onboarding startup HelloFlow to Trulioo for more than $50 million [^2]. Spektr has a headcount of 45 and serves clients including Pleo, Santander Leasing, Monta, Phantom, and Mercuryo [^1].
