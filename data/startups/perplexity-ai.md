## About

Perplexity AI is an AI-powered search engine founded in August 2022 by Aravind Srinivas, Denis Yarats, Johnny Ho, and Andy Konwinski [^1][^2]. Srinivas previously held research positions at OpenAI, Google Brain, and DeepMind; Yarats was an AI research scientist at Meta; Ho was an engineer at Quora and quantitative trader at Tower Research Capital; and Konwinski was a co-founder of Databricks [^2]. The company launched its search engine on December 7, 2022 [^1]. As of September 2025, Perplexity was valued at $20 billion and had raised over $1.5 billion in total funding [^3].
