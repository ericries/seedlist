## About

Decameal is a Grindsted, Denmark-based foodtech company founded in 2022 by Happylan Natkunarajah and Leander Hessner [^1][^2]. The company has developed a patent-pending process to convert invasive European shore crabs sourced from small-scale coastal fisheries into protein flour and protein concentrate for poultry, aquaculture, and pet feed, with chitin and chitosan extraction from the shells in development for higher-value markets [^1][^2].
