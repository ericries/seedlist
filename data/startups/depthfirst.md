## About

depthfirst is a San Francisco-based applied AI lab founded in 2024 by technical leaders from DeepMind, Databricks, and Faire, focused on AI-native cybersecurity [^1]. The company develops a platform called General Security Intelligence that deploys custom AI agents to analyze codebases, infrastructure, and workflows to detect vulnerabilities that traditional tools miss [^1][^2].
