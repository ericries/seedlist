## About

Roblox is an online gaming platform and game creation system founded in 2004 by David Baszucki and Erik Cassel in San Mateo, California [^1]. The platform allows users to create and play games built by other users, and had 115 million monthly active players at the time of its Series G [^2]. Roblox went public via direct listing on the NYSE on March 10, 2021, under ticker RBLX [^3].
