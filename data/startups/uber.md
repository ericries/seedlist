## About

Uber is a ride-hailing and logistics platform founded in 2009 by Travis Kalanick and Garrett Camp in San Francisco [^1]. The company went public on the New York Stock Exchange in May 2019, raising $8.1 billion at a $45 per share price and an implied valuation of $82.4 billion [^2].
