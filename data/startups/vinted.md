## About

Vinted is a Vilnius-based second-hand fashion marketplace operating across 22 European countries, having expanded its categories to include electronics, books, toys, and video games [^1][^2]. The April 2026 oversubscribed secondary share sale provided liquidity to early investors and long-serving employees at an €8B equity valuation — a 60% step-up from the €5B valuation set in October 2024 [^1][^2][^3].
