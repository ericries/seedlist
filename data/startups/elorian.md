## About

Elorian is a Palo Alto-based AI research lab and product studio focused on visual reasoning, founded by former Google DeepMind researcher Andrew Dai and former Apple AI researcher Yinfei Yang [^1][^2]. The company emerged from stealth on April 9, 2026 at a $300 million valuation [^1]. Elorian builds purpose-built visual reasoning models for robotics, aerospace, medicine, and manufacturing [^2].
