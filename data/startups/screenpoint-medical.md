## About

ScreenPoint Medical is a Nijmegen, Netherlands-based medical technology company that develops Transpara, an AI-powered breast cancer detection platform used with mammography [^1][^2]. Founded in 2014 by Professor Nico Karssemeijer and Professor Sir Michael Brady, the company says Transpara is deployed in 30+ countries and has processed over 12 million mammograms to date [^1]. The company has received prior Series C backing from Insight Partners and a development partnership with Siemens Healthineers [^1][^3].
