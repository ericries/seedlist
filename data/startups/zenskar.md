## About

Zenskar is an AI-native billing and revenue automation platform for B2B companies, headquartered in New York with offices in Bengaluru [^1][^2]. Founded in 2022 by Apurv Bansal and Saurabh Agrawal, the platform automates billing, accounts receivable, and revenue recognition for companies with complex pricing models including usage-based billing, prepaid credits, multi-entity structures, and multi-currency operations [^1][^3]. The company reported a fivefold increase in revenue over the past year [^1].
