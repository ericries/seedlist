## About

SquareMind is a Paris-based AI and robotics company building the Swan robotic skin imaging platform for dermatology, designed to make high-quality skin exams more efficient and accessible at scale [^1][^2]. The April 2026 round, which combines previously undisclosed pre-Series A financing with new capital, supports commercial launches in the United States and Europe [^1].
