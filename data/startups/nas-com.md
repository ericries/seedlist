## About

Nas.com is an AI platform for the solopreneur economy, providing tools for digital and physical product creation, storefront building, community management, automated marketing, and global payments [^1]. Founded by Nuseir Yassin (creator of Nas Daily, 70 million followers), the platform has 3.5 million members across 150+ countries and 20,000 paying business owners [^1].
