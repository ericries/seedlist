## About

Sonire Therapeutics is a clinical-stage medical device company pioneering next-generation HIFU (High-Intensity Focused Ultrasound) therapy for non-invasive cancer treatment, beginning with pancreatic cancer [^1]. Founded in Tokyo in February 2020, the company is now headquartered in Palo Alto, California [^1]. Sonire received FDA Breakthrough Device Designation in 2024 for its HIFU pancreatic cancer ablation system [^1].
