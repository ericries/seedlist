## About

Era Computer is a software platform that enables hardware makers to build AI agents and orchestrations for AI devices, supporting 130+ LLMs from 14+ providers across form factors including glasses, jewelry, and home speakers [^1]. The company was founded in 2025 by Liz Dorman (CEO, formerly Humane and HP), Alex Ollman (CTO, formerly HP on enterprise agentic frameworks), and Megan Gole (CPO, formerly Sutter Hill Ventures working on the Jony Ive / Sam Altman "io" project) [^1].
