## About

Juno is a San Francisco-based AI-powered tax preparation platform founded in 2023 by CPA Dave Haase [^1]. The platform automates approximately 90% of data entry across over 90 document types for accounting firms, reducing typical tax return preparation time from 2-3 hours to 7-10 minutes [^1]. Juno targets small-to-mid-sized accounting firms, which comprise 90% of the market, and reached nearly 500 customers within a year of launch with mid-seven-figure annual recurring revenue in eight months [^1].
