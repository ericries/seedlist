## About

Orbital is a Los Angeles-based startup developing orbital AI data centers designed to operate on solar power in low-Earth orbit [^1]. Founded by Euwyn Poon, who previously co-founded micromobility company Spin (acquired by Ford), the company plans to deploy satellite constellations containing clusters of NVIDIA-powered GPU servers [^1]. Orbital's first test mission, Orbital-1, is scheduled to launch on a SpaceX Falcon 9 in April 2027 [^1].
