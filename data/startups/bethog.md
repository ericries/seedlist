## About

BetHog is a cryptocurrency-based online casino and sportsbook platform founded in 2024 by FanDuel co-founders Nigel Eccles and Rob Jones, along with Ryan Anderson [^1][^2]. The company offers proprietary games and sports betting services with an emphasis on "provably fair" gameplay, and has launched an AI-powered live dealer product called "Sunny" [^3]. The Series A funding was raised to scale the AI live dealer platform and expand internationally from the UK [^4].
