## About

GetWhys is a Boise, Idaho-based AI-powered customer intelligence platform that builds and maintains a proprietary library of in-depth B2B software buyer interviews, making the analysis searchable and usable for go-to-market teams [^1]. Customers include Intel, Verizon, DocuSign, Mission Cloud (CDW), and Commvault [^1].
