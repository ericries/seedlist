## About

Alloy Therapeutics is a Boston-based tech-enabled biotech infrastructure company that has collaborated with more than 200 partners across antibodies, bispecifics, genetic medicines, and cell therapies [^1]. The company has over 100 licensed therapeutic programs, including 22 in clinical development [^1]. The Series E valued Alloy at $1 billion [^1].
