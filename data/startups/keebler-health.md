## About

Keebler Health is an LLM-native health technology company that processes unstructured clinical documentation to identify missed HCC coding opportunities, enabling accurate risk adjustment and revenue capture without disrupting clinical workflows [^1]. The company was founded in 2023 in Durham, North Carolina [^1].
