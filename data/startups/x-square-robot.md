## About

X Square Robot (自变量机器人) is a Shenzhen-based embodied AI startup building foundation models and humanoid-form robotic platforms [^1][^2]. The company previously raised a $140M Series A++ round in January 2026 led by ByteDance and Sequoia, and was backed earlier by Alibaba and Meituan — making it the only Chinese embodied-intelligence company to have received investment from three of China's largest internet giants [^2][^3]. The April 2026 Series B brought the company's disclosed total funding to roughly $416M and signaled accelerating investor interest in full-stack embodied AI platforms [^1][^2].
