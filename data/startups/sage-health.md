## About

Sage Health is a full-risk primary care and wellness provider serving Medicare-eligible seniors [^1]. The company operates advanced neighborhood health centers in primary care shortage areas, where physicians manage no more than 500 patients each, enabling longer visits and intensive management of chronic conditions [^1]. Sage operates across Alabama, Arkansas, Maryland, and Mississippi [^1].
