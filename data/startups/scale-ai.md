## About

Scale AI is an AI data infrastructure company that provides data labeling, evaluation, and fine-tuning services for machine learning models [^1]. The company was co-founded in 2016 by Alexandr Wang and Lucy Guo through Y Combinator [^2]. In June 2025, Meta Platforms purchased a 49% non-voting stake in Scale AI for $14.3 billion, more than doubling its valuation to $29 billion; as part of the deal, founder Alexandr Wang departed to take a position at Meta and was replaced as CEO by Jason Droege [^3]. Scale AI has raised a total of approximately $15.9 billion across nine funding rounds [^4].
