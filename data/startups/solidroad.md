## About

Solidroad is a Dublin-based AI-powered quality assurance and training platform for customer interactions, working with clients including Crypto.com, Ryanair, and Oura [^1]. The Y Combinator (W25) alumnus evaluates customer interactions across human and AI agents, identifying risks and skills gaps while offering personalized coaching in real time [^2]. The company also has offices in San Francisco [^1].
