## About

Audrey AI is a Dublin-based startup building an agentic AI platform purpose-built for financial auditors [^1][^2]. The system automates evidence gathering and testing — the most manual, time-intensive parts of audit engagements — by orchestrating client data and applying audit procedures end-to-end while learning each firm's specific methodology [^1][^2]. Founded in 2025 by Ryan Loughran and David Burke, the platform has piloted with top-10 and top-20 audit firms, with reported 85%+ time savings in client data collection, validation, and tests of detail [^1][^2]. Proceeds from the pre-seed round will expand engineering and audit specialist teams and scale deployments in Ireland, the UK, and international markets [^1][^2].
