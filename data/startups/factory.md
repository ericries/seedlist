## About

Factory is a San Francisco-based startup building AI coding agents ("Droids") for enterprise software development teams [^1][^2]. Founded in 2023 by Matan Grinberg and Eno Reyes, the company reached a $1.5 billion valuation with its Series C [^1][^2]. Factory's AI agents are used daily by hundreds of thousands of developers at enterprises including Nvidia, Adobe, EY, Palo Alto Networks, Adyen, MongoDB, Bayer, Zapier, Clari, Bilt, Profound, and Rogo [^1]. The platform extends beyond code generation into testing, review, documentation, and deployment [^1]. The company has doubled revenue month-over-month for six consecutive months [^2].
