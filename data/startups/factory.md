## About

Factory is a San Francisco-based startup building AI coding agents ("Droids") for enterprise software development teams [^1]. Founded in 2023, the company has reached a $1.5 billion valuation [^1]. Factory's AI agents are used daily by hundreds of thousands of developers at enterprises including Nvidia, Adobe, EY, Palo Alto Networks, Adyen, MongoDB, Bayer, Zapier, Clari, Bilt, Profound, and Rogo [^1]. The platform extends beyond code generation into testing, review, documentation, and deployment [^1].
