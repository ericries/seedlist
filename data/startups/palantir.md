## About

Palantir Technologies is a data analytics and software company founded in 2003 by Peter Thiel, Alex Karp, Joe Lonsdale, Stephen Cohen, and Nathan Gettings [^1]. Thiel named the company after the "seeing stone" in J.R.R. Tolkien's legendarium [^1]. The company initially focused on intelligence community applications, with the CIA's venture capital arm In-Q-Tel as one of its earliest backers [^2]. Palantir went public via direct listing on the New York Stock Exchange on September 30, 2020, under the ticker symbol "PLTR" [^1].
