## About

Omni is an AI analytics platform building a governed semantic data layer that unifies business intelligence and artificial intelligence for enterprises [^1][^2]. Founded in 2022 and led by CEO Colin Zima (formerly co-founder at Looker), the platform integrates business context, metric definitions, and access controls into a single model that powers dashboards, queries, and AI-driven insights [^1]. The Series C round at a $1.5B valuation marks a significant step-up from Omni's previous $650M valuation in March 2025, driven by 4x year-over-year revenue growth [^1].
