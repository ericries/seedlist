## About

Redpine is a Stockholm-based AI infrastructure company building a licensed-data API for AI agents, founded in 2024 by Anders Hammarbäck and David Österdahl [^1][^2]. The platform lets enterprises securely connect agentic AI workflows to licensed, high-quality, multimodal datasets that aren't otherwise reachable on the public web [^1][^2]. Earlier backers include angels from OpenAI, Spotify, Perplexity, Xiaomi, and Sana [^2].
