## About

RadixArk is a Palo Alto, California-based AI infrastructure company building open, scalable systems for training, deploying, and running frontier AI models [^1] [^2]. The company was founded by Ying Sheng and Banghua Zhu, AI infrastructure and modeling veterans from xAI and NVIDIA [^1]. RadixArk's platform builds on SGLang, an open-source inference engine created by Sheng in 2023 that is deployed across hundreds of thousands of GPUs worldwide and used by Google, Microsoft, NVIDIA, Oracle, AMD, Nebius, LinkedIn, xAI, and Thinking Machines Lab [^1]. On May 5, 2026, RadixArk launched publicly with a $100M seed round led by Accel and co-led by Spark Capital at a $400M post-money valuation [^1] [^3].
