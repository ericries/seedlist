## About

Lucid Bots develops autonomous exterior cleaning robots and software, including the Sherpa cleaning drone and Lavo AI pressure-washing robot [^1]. The company operates a subscription-based model called Lucid Refresh that combines hardware, fleet management software, and operator training for commercial cleaning services [^1]. Nearly 1,000 robots are deployed, with operators generating more than $75 million in cleaning revenue [^2].
