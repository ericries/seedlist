## About

JuliaHub is a Cambridge, Massachusetts-based scientific AI company founded in 2015 by the creators of the Julia programming language [^1][^2]. The company builds an AI-driven engineering platform for modeling and simulating complex industrial systems — spanning aerospace, automotive, HVAC, semiconductors, and utilities — and competes directly with MathWorks' Simulink [^2][^3]. On April 30, 2026, JuliaHub announced a $65M Series B led by Dorilton Capital alongside the launch of Dyad 3.0, an agentic AI platform for hardware engineering [^1][^2].
