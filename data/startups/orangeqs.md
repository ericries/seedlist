## About

OrangeQS (Orange Quantum Systems) is a Delft-based deeptech startup building high-throughput test equipment for quantum processors. The company's FLEX cryogenic testing systems enable quantum chip manufacturers to characterize devices in days rather than weeks, addressing a critical bottleneck for scaling quantum computing hardware [^1][^2]. The company was spun out of QuTech at TU Delft and has previously raised pre-seed and a €12 million seed round in June 2025 led by Icecat Capital with Cottonwood Technology Fund, QBeat Ventures, QDNL Participations, and InnovationQuarter Capital [^2].
