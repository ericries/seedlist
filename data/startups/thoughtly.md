## About

Thoughtly is an AI engagement platform founded in 2023 that embeds voice, SMS, and email agents directly into CRM workflows [^1]. The $5.5M seed round, announced April 22, 2026, was led by Armory Square Ventures with participation from nvp capital, Afore Capital, Greycroft, and K5 Global Tokyo Black, bringing total funding to $8M [^1].
