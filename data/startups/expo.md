## About

Expo is a San Francisco-based open-source platform for building universal native applications with React Native [^1]. Co-founded by Charlie Cheever (early Facebook engineer and Quora co-founder) and James Ide, the platform has close to 4 million weekly downloads and a community of over 3 million developers [^1]. As part of this funding round, Expo launched Expo Agent, which enables AI-assisted app development that can generate functioning mobile apps from ideas in minutes [^1].
