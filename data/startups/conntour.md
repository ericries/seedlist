## About

Conntour is an AI-powered video intelligence platform founded in 2024 and headquartered in Miami and Tel Aviv [^1][^2]. The platform functions as a search engine for security video systems, enabling security teams to query camera feeds using natural language to find specific objects, people, or situations in real time [^1]. Unlike traditional video analytics limited to predefined categories, Conntour supports unlimited, highly specific scenarios described in plain language [^3]. The platform can scale to thousands of camera feeds and monitor up to 50 feeds off a single consumer GPU [^1]. Conntour is a Y Combinator company and is actively deployed for Singapore's Homeland Security operations [^3].
