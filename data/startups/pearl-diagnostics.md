## About

Pearl Diagnostics is a Baltimore-based biotech and Johns Hopkins University spinout founded in 2014, developing a noninvasive fungal infection test [^1]. CEO Eric Gornstein noted "It's extraordinarily rare for a company at the Series A level to have a commercial product," highlighting the company's advanced commercial stage [^1]. The Series A proceeds will be used to launch US sales following recent FDA clearance and expand into Europe, where Pearl has secured CE approval [^1].
