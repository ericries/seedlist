## About

SentinelOne is an AI-powered cybersecurity company founded in 2013 in Tel Aviv, Israel, by Tomer Weingarten, Almog Cohen, and Ehud Shamir [^1]. The company's autonomous endpoint protection platform uses machine learning to detect and respond to threats in real time, moving beyond legacy signature-based antivirus methods [^1]. SentinelOne went public on June 30, 2021, on the NYSE under ticker "S," raising $1.2 billion at approximately $8.9 billion valuation — the highest-valued cybersecurity IPO at the time [^2][^3].
