## About

Pursuit is a govtech startup that helps companies identify and win government contracts by analyzing public data from approximately 11,000 state, local, and education (SLED) entities — crawling budgets, contract registers, FOIA records, and RFPs to surface buying opportunities [^1]. Founded by Mike Vichich and Brandon Max, the company brings $25.5M in total funding through this Series A [^1].
