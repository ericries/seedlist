## About

Ralio is a London-based trust infrastructure provider for AI agentic payments, building the safety layer between AI agents and payment rails [^1]. The company's platform equips AI agents with guardrails, identity verification, and audit tooling before any funds are moved across accounts, cards, or stablecoins [^1]. The round was three times oversubscribed within three months of the company being founded [^1]. CEO Ghali Bennani Laafiret previously scaled revenue operations at fintech unicorn Alan and B2B software company LumApps, while CTO Leonardo Rosales rebuilt payment infrastructure for Tier 1 banks at Form3 [^1].
