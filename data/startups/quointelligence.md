## About

QuoIntelligence is a Frankfurt-based cybersecurity company providing analyst-curated intelligence on cyber threats, physical risks, and geopolitical signals, founded in 2020 by Marco Riccardi [^1][^2]. Its conversational AI analyst KARLA helps European organizations manage cyber risks and maintain compliance with NIS2 and DORA regulations [^1]. The company has offices in Spain and Italy in addition to its Frankfurt HQ [^1].
