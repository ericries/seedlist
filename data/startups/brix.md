## About

Brix is a tokenization startup that aims to broaden access to investment opportunities in emerging markets by tokenizing real-world assets such as stocks, funds, and bonds [^1]. The platform plans to launch on the MegaETH network, with its first product being a digital instrument backed by tokenized Turkish lira-denominated money market funds [^1]. The company targets markets including Turkey, UAE, Egypt, Mexico, Brazil, and South Korea [^1].
