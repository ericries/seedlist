## About

Slash is a San Francisco-based vertical banking platform serving web3, e-commerce, agencies, contractors, affiliate marketers, healthcare suppliers, online travel agencies, and wholesalers [^1]. The company also offers a stablecoin payments product and treasury and working capital tools [^1]. Slash achieved unicorn status with its Series C at a $1.4 billion valuation [^1].
