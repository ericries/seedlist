## About

PlaqueTec is a Cambridge UK-based medtech company pioneering intracoronary liquid biopsy to identify inflammatory drivers of cardiovascular disease and enable precision medicine for coronary artery disease [^1][^2]. The April 2026 oversubscribed financing was funded entirely by the company's existing investor base [^1].
