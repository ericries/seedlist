## About

Also is a Palo Alto-based company spun out of Rivian that develops autonomous delivery vehicles [^1]. The company has partnered with DoorDash in a multi-year commercial agreement to develop and deploy autonomous last-mile delivery vehicles at scale [^1][^2]. The Series C round values the company at $1 billion [^2].
