## About

Smart Bricks is a San Francisco-based proptech startup building AI infrastructure for global real estate investing [^1]. Founded in 2024 by Mohamed Mohamed, the company designs autonomous reasoning systems that allow capital to identify, evaluate, and execute real-estate investments end-to-end, compressing a process that traditionally takes three to six months into minutes [^1]. The platform analyzes millions of public and proprietary data points across pricing, liquidity, transaction history, supply, and financing terms [^1].
