## About

Nebius Group N.V. is an Amsterdam-based AI infrastructure company operating as a "neocloud" provider purpose-built for AI workloads [^1]. The company is publicly traded on NASDAQ under ticker NBIS. Nebius provides full-stack AI cloud services and has signed multibillion-dollar capacity deals with Microsoft and Meta [^2][^3]. The company plans to deploy more than 5 gigawatts of NVIDIA systems by the end of 2030 [^1].
