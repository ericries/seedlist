## About

Magrathea Metals is a California-based startup founded in 2022 by Alex Grant (CEO) and Jacob Brown, both chemical engineers with prior experience working on lithium and battery technology, including time at Tesla [^1]. The company is developing a novel electrolytic process to produce magnesium metal from seawater and underground brines, with the goal of rebuilding US-domestic and ex-China magnesium production capacity for industrial and defense applications [^1][^2].
