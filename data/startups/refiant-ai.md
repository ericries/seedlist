## About

Refiant AI is a South Africa-founded startup that uses nature-inspired algorithms to compress AI models, reducing hardware and energy requirements by over 80% [^1]. The company demonstrated compressing a 120 billion parameter model to run on a standard laptop while preserving near-identical quality [^1].
