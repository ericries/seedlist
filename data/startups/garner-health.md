## About

Garner Health is a healthcare navigation platform founded in 2019 and headquartered in New York City [^1][^2]. The company analyzes over 320 million patient medical records to rank physicians based on clinical quality metrics, then creates financial incentives for patients to see top-performing providers by reducing their out-of-pocket costs [^3]. The platform serves over 2.5 million members across more than 700 employer and health plan clients [^3]. Founder and CEO Nick Reber previously worked at Oscar Health in analytics and provider network development, and at Bridgewater Associates as co-head of Research Analytics [^4].
