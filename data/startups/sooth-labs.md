## About

Sooth Labs is a stealth AI lab founded by former Meta Platforms employees, building AI models that allow businesses to forecast the likelihood of geopolitical and market events [^1][^2]. The April 2026 seed round valued the company at roughly $335 million [^1]. Backers include AI pioneers Yann LeCun (former Meta) and Jeff Dean (Google Chief Scientist); Meta Chief Technology Officer Andrew Bosworth advises the firm [^1][^2].
