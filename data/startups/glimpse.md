## About

Glimpse is an AI-native platform that automates retail dispute management for CPG brands, helping companies recover revenue lost to invalid deductions [^1]. Founded in 2020 by Purdue graduates Akash Raju, Anuj Mehta, and Kushal Negi, the company pivoted from Airbnb product placements to retail dispute tracking in 2024 [^1]. Glimpse works with more than 200 retail brands, including Suave and Chapstick [^1].
