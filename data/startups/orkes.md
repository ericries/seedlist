## About

Orkes is a Santa Clara-based agentic orchestration platform that automates and scales complex business processes across agentic AI, human-in-the-loop tasks, and microservices [^1][^2]. Led by CEO Jeu George, CTO Viren Baraiya, and CPO Dilip Lukose, the company has tripled its customer base since its 2024 Series A, with customers including United Wholesale Mortgage, Quest Diagnostics, Twilio, LinkedIn, Naveo Commerce, and Woodside Energy [^1]. As part of the Series B, Alex Scherbakovsky, general partner at AVP, is joining the board [^1].
