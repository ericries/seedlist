## About

Qualitate is an AI-native primary intelligence platform that automates the expert research workflow — from study design and expert recruitment through interview moderation and insight generation — for hedge funds, private equity, venture capital, credit investors, and corporate strategy teams. Founded in 2024 by Sagar Kadakia, the company reports 30x revenue growth over the prior 12 months, a 100% customer renewal rate, and over 200% net revenue retention [^1][^2].
