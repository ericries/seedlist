## About

Resolve AI is a San Francisco-based company that develops AI-driven systems for software production operations, using custom models and production-specific agents for detection, diagnosis, remediation, and prevention [^1]. Enterprise customers include Coinbase, DoorDash, MSCI, Salesforce, Zscaler, and MongoDB [^1]. The Series A Extension valued Resolve AI at $1.5 billion, bringing total funding to over $190 million [^1]. The company also launched Resolve AI Labs, led by Chief AI Scientist Dhruv Mahajan (formerly Meta Superintelligence Labs) [^1].
