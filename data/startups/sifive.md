## About

SiFive is a Santa Clara-based fabless semiconductor company and leading provider of commercial RISC-V processors and silicon IP, founded in 2015 by UC Berkeley researchers Krste Asanovic, Yunsup Lee, and Andrew Waterman [^1]. The company reports over 10 billion RISC-V cores shipped across more than 500 designs [^2]. Patrick Little serves as Chairman and CEO [^2].
