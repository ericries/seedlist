## About

WHOOP is a Boston-based human performance company founded in 2012 that develops a wearable health and fitness tracker focused on recovery, strain, and sleep monitoring [^1]. The company's valuation tripled to $10.1 billion with its latest funding round [^2].

Notable individual investors include Cristiano Ronaldo, LeBron James, Rory McIlroy, and Reggie Miller [^1].
