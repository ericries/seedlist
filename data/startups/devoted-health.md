## About

Devoted Health is a Medicare Advantage insurance company founded in March 2017 by brothers Todd Park and Ed Park [^1][^2]. Todd Park previously co-founded Athenahealth and Castlight Health and served as U.S. Chief Technology Officer during the Obama administration; Ed Park held senior positions at Athenahealth [^2][^3]. The company is headquartered in Waltham, Massachusetts [^1].

Devoted Health offers Medicare Advantage plans with integrated health insurance, virtual-first medical care, and personalized member support [^4]. The company launched its first plan in Florida in October 2018 [^3] and expanded to 299 counties across 13 states by the 2023 enrollment period [^8]. As of July 2024, Devoted served approximately 227,000 members across its markets [^7]. The company achieved a 4.5 CMS star rating in its inaugural year of eligibility [^2].
