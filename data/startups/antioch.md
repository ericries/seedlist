## About

Antioch is a New York-based startup building a simulation platform for physical AI and robotics, aiming to close the "sim-to-real" gap that makes it difficult for robots trained in virtual environments to operate reliably in the physical world [^1][^2]. The platform allows robot builders to spin up multiple digital instances of their hardware connected to simulated sensors, enabling edge-case testing, reinforcement learning, and training data generation [^1]. The company was founded in May 2025 by Harry Mellsop with four co-founders — Alex Langshur, Michael Calvey (both previously sold Transpose to Chainalysis), Collin Schlager (ex-Meta Reality Labs), and Colton Swingle (ex-Google DeepMind) [^1].
