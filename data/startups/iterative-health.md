## About

Iterative Health is a Cambridge, Massachusetts-based healthcare technology and services company building an AI-driven multispecialty clinical research network [^1]. Founded by Jonathan Ng, the company began in gastroenterology and hepatology and has expanded to cardiology and obesity, with more than 100 research sites across North America, Europe, India, and Australia [^1][^2]. On April 30, 2026, the company closed a $77 million Series C, reportedly valuing it at approximately $1.3 billion post-money [^1][^2][^3].
