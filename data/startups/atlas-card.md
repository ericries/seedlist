## About

Atlas Card (formerly Point) is a New York-based fintech company offering a premium charge card with concierge services focused on dining, travel, and lifestyle experiences for high-net-worth customers [^1][^2]. Originally founded in 2019 as Point, a debit card startup, the company relaunched in 2023 as Atlas with a premium concierge-first model and a $999 annual fee [^3]. The company has over 2,000 high-net-worth members and generates annualized gross revenue exceeding $20 million [^3].
