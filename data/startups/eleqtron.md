## About

eleQtron is a German quantum computing startup developing trapped-ion quantum computers based on its proprietary MAGIC (Magnetic Gradient Induced Coupling) technology platform, which enables precise control of trapped ions through miniaturized microwave techniques rather than lasers [^1][^2]. The company was founded in 2020 by Jan Henrik Leisse, Michael Johanning, and Christof Wunderlich, and is headquartered in Siegburg, Germany [^2][^3]. eleQtron's strategy focuses on transitioning quantum computing from research technology to industrially deployable infrastructure, with key customers including the German Aerospace Center (DLR) and Forschungszentrum Jülich [^1]. As of the May 2026 Series A announcement, the company reported more than €54 million in confirmed orders [^1][^2].
