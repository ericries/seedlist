## About

Rilian is a McLean, Virginia-based agentic AI systems integration company developing the Caspian platform for defense and national security customers [^1][^2][^3]. Founded by Christian Schnedler (CEO), Nick Pompeo, and Dan Fischer, all leading AI security and defense operators, the company's pre-trained agents automate analyst workflows, anticipate adversarial behavior, and preserve institutional knowledge in sovereign and air-gapped environments [^1][^3]. In July 2025, Rilian signed a contract with the UAE Cybersecurity Council to deploy Caspian across the UAE National Security Operations Centre — described as the platform's first nation-scale implementation [^2][^3].
