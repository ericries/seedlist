## About

NanoTech Materials develops high-performance coatings powered by its patented Insulative Ceramic Particle (ICP) technology, engineered to reduce heat transfer in buildings and outdoor infrastructure [^1]. The company operates a 43,000-square-foot manufacturing facility in Katy, Texas, and sells product lines including Cool Roof Coat, Wildfire Shield, and Insulative Coat: Cool Touch [^1].
