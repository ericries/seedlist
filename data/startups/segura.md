## About

Segura is a Brazilian insurtech founded in 2024 by Luis Alberto "Bebeto" Nogueira, Lucca Buffara, and Pedro Nobrega, building distribution infrastructure that connects insurance carriers and brokers through a single operating layer covering quote structuring, policy management, renewals, and billing [^1][^2]. The platform includes an AI assistant ("Helena") that automates customer journeys via WhatsApp while maintaining compliance with Brazil's insurance regulator SUSEP [^1].
