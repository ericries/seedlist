## About

Groove Quantum is a Delft-based quantum computing startup developing scalable germanium spin-qubit processors that are CMOS-manufacturing compatible [^1][^2]. The April 2026 announcement coincided with the unveiling of an 18-qubit semiconductor spin-qubit processor, described as the largest of its kind ever built [^1][^2]. The germanium-based qubits sit between hundreds of nanometres in size — large enough to be reliably controlled and connected, small enough to be densely packed [^2].
