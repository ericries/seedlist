## About

Counterpart is a Los Angeles-based specialty InsurTech company founded by Tanner Hackett that provides management and professional liability insurance for small and medium-sized businesses [^1][^4]. Operating as a tech-enabled managing general agent, Counterpart offers Directors & Officers, Employment Practices Liability, Fiduciary, Commercial Crime, and various Professional Liability products [^4]. The company describes its platform as "Agentic Insurance" — combining underwriting, broker services, risk mitigation, and claims management with modern AI [^1][^2][^3]. Counterpart has processed more than 250,000 applications and written over 35,000 policies through 2,800 brokers and four A-rated carriers, and reports premium growth of nearly 175% in 2025 [^1][^2].

On April 28, 2026, Counterpart announced the close of a $50 million Series C financing round led by Valor Equity Partners, with continued participation from existing investor Vy Capital [^1][^2][^3]. The round brings total capital raised to more than $106 million [^1][^2]. Proceeds will support the launch of new specialty insurance products, industry-specific programs, expanded claims and risk management capabilities, and the collateralization of Counterpart Insurance Company, the company's proprietary risk-bearing entity [^1][^2].
