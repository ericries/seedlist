## About

Casa is a subscription-based home services and repair platform founded by Michael York (CEO) and Michael Mizrahi, both alumni of Travis Kalanick's Uber and CloudKitchens (City Storage Systems) [^1]. The company exited stealth on April 30, 2026 after roughly two years of development, positioning itself as "a full-time concierge that can reach out for any home project you might have," according to York [^1]. York has described the long-term vision as "building the autonomous home" [^1].

The company has raised approximately $27 million to date, including a $20 million Series A led by Forerunner Ventures announced on April 30, 2026, with participation from Travis Kalanick and Sandberg Bernthal Venture Partners (the venture firm of former Meta COO Sheryl Sandberg and her husband Tom Bernthal) [^1][^2].
