## About

Sora Fuel is a Boston-based climate technology company developing technology to produce sustainable aviation fuel (SAF) directly from atmospheric CO2, water, and renewable energy [^1]. The company's proprietary electrolyzer captures ambient air carbon and converts it into syngas in a single integrated step, bypassing the expensive sorbent regeneration processes used in conventional direct air capture [^1]. Sora claims to capture carbon at less than $50 per ton, roughly one-tenth the cost of conventional DAC approaches [^1].
