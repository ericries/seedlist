## About

Nace.AI is a Palo Alto-based AI company building "MetaModel," a system that dynamically generates small, task-specific models as an alternative to large general-purpose foundation models for enterprise use [^1]. Its first product, NAVI (Nace Verification Intelligence), is an AI agent for audit and compliance that detects risks, discrepancies, and regulatory violations in real time, with early use cases including automated loan application review at Mountain America Credit Union [^2]. The company was founded in spring 2024 by developers, researchers, and entrepreneurs from Google, Meta, and the University of Toronto [^1].
