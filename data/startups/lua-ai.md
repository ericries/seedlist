## About

Lua is a London-based startup building an operating system for human-agent collaboration in the workplace [^1]. Founded by Lorcan O'Cathain and Stefan Kruger, the platform enables teams to build, own, and manage their own AI agent workforce [^1]. Lua handles infrastructure, model orchestration, data, channel integrations, and monitoring [^1]. Since launching in October 2025, Lua has grown revenue close to 30% week-on-week [^1]. The company is a Y Combinator alumnus [^1].
