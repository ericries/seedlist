## About

Critical Loop is a Los Angeles-based industrial power company that pairs modular microgrids with flexible grid interconnection to deliver power to industrial sites in days or weeks rather than the years-long utility queue [^1]. The company's systems combine battery storage, generation, and a software-defined controller called Cygnus into a single platform deployable where grid capacity is constrained [^1]. The team includes engineers and operators from SpaceX, Tesla, Palantir, and Rivian [^2]. Critical Loop won a competitive bid to manage solar and battery systems supporting an 11 MW load at San Diego International Airport and has a supply agreement with LG Energy Solution Vertech for U.S.-made batteries [^1].
