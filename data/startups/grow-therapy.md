## About

Grow Therapy is a New York-based mental health platform founded in October 2020 by Jake Cooper and Alan Ni [^1][^3]. The company helps therapists build and manage their private practices while connecting patients with in-network providers. Grow Therapy has reached $1B in annual revenue and serves patients through both in-person and telehealth therapy [^2]. Beginning in March 2026, companies can offer Grow Therapy as a mental health benefit with seamless EAP-to-insurance transitions [^1].
