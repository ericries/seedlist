## About

inploi is a London-based candidate-experience platform focused on enterprises with high-volume hiring needs — hospitality, retail, healthcare and similar industries — applying AI agents to automate sourcing, screening and application workflows alongside systems like Workday and SAP [^1]. The company was founded in 2016 by Matthew de la Hey (CEO) and Alex Hanson-Smith [^2].
