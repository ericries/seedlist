## About

Fence is a Madrid-based blockchain infrastructure company focused on automating the back end of the asset-backed finance market [^1][^2][^3]. Founded in 2023 by Juan Montero and Gonzalo Bandeira, the company uses smart contracts behind the scenes to manage cash, collateral, verification, calculation, and payment functions in securitization deals — replacing manual administration rather than fronting tokens or crypto wallets to clients [^2][^3]. Fence reports overseeing approximately $1.5 billion in assets across its platform, with clients including BlackRock, Fortress, i80 Group, and BBVA [^1][^2][^3]. Following the 2026 round, CEO Juan Montero is relocating to New York to lead growth of the company's NYC team [^1][^2].
