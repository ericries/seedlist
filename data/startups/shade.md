## About

Shade is an NYC-based AI-powered cloud storage platform founded in 2024 that lets creative teams search their media files using natural language [^1]. The company was co-founded by Brandon Fan and Emerson Dove [^1]. The $14M round, announced April 23, 2026, was co-led by Khosla Ventures, Construct Capital, and Bling Capital, bringing total funding to $20M [^1].
