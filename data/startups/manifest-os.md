## About

Manifest OS builds and operates AI-native law firms under the "Manifest Law" brand, using proprietary AI plus centralized back-office operations to deliver legal services on fixed-fee and outcomes-based pricing rather than billable hours [^1][^2]. Founded by Dan Mishin (CEO), the New York-based firm launched its first location 18 months prior under Arizona's alternative business structure program, currently focused on business immigration with plans to expand into global immigration services [^1][^2]. The April 2026 Series A valued the company at $750M [^1][^2][^3].
