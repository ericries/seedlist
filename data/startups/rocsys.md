## About

Rocsys is a Dutch-American robotics company building hands-free EV charging systems for robotaxi fleets, with the M1 platform serving up to 10 charging bays from a single overhead-mounted unit [^1][^2]. The M1 uses an overhead rail-mounted, long-reach robotic arm that autonomously detects vehicles, opens charge ports, plugs in, charges, and disconnects, claiming a 99.9%+ plug-in success rate using AI-enhanced computer vision trained on six years of real-world data [^2]. Total funding to date is $56M with large-scale rollout planned across North America and Europe in 2027 [^1][^2].
