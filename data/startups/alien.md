## About

Alien is a San Francisco-based startup building trust infrastructure for the agentic economy [^1][^2]. The platform verifies human identity through facial recognition on mobile devices without permanently storing biometric data, issuing credentials called Alien ID to humans and Agent ID to AI agents [^1]. The system anchors AI agents to identifiable humans, establishing accountability and reputation before agents conduct transactions on websites or platforms [^2].
