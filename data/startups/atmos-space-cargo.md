## About

ATMOS Space Cargo is a German aerospace company building orbital transfer and return vehicles to enable Earth-to-space-to-Earth logistics. The company was co-founded by CEO Sebastian Klaus and operates from bases in Lichtenau, Germany and Strasbourg, France [^1][^2]. ATMOS successfully completed its PHOENIX 1 demonstration flight in April 2025 and is now building PHOENIX 2, an initial three-vehicle fleet of orbital return spacecraft, while also developing PHOENIX 3, its next-generation vehicle [^1][^2]. The Series A funding also supports the launch of ATMOS WORKS, a service for governmental and defence customers [^1].
