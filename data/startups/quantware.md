## About

QuantWare is a Delft, Netherlands-based company designing, fabricating, and integrating superconducting quantum processors. It was founded in 2021 as a spinout from QuTech at TU Delft by Matt Rijlaarsdam and Alessandro Bruno [^1][^2]. The company's proprietary VIO architecture uses a modular, "open architecture" approach intended to scale superconducting qubits toward utility-scale quantum computing, and as of the May 2026 funding announcement QuantWare reports having shipped quantum processors to more than 50 customers across 20 countries [^1][^3]. Proceeds from the Series B will finance KiloFab, a new dedicated quantum processor fabrication facility in Delft, alongside the VIO-40K processor architecture targeting 10,000 qubits [^1][^2][^3].
