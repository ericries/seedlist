## About

PagerDuty is a digital operations management platform that helps companies manage IT incidents and alerts in real time [^1]. Founded in 2009 by University of Waterloo graduates Alex Solomon, Andrew Miklas, and Baskar Puvanathasan, the company participated in Y Combinator before raising institutional funding [^2]. PagerDuty went public on the NYSE on April 11, 2019, raising $218 million at a $1.8 billion valuation [^3].
