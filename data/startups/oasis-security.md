## About

Oasis Security is a cybersecurity startup focused on agentic access management, securing non-human identities across AI and cloud environments [^1]. Founded in August 2022 by Danny Brickman and Amit Zimerman, the company is headquartered in New York [^2]. Brickman previously spent 11 years in the Israel Defense Forces leading cyber R&D [^2]. Oasis helps manage access from non-human accounts such as AI agents, addressing an environment where machine identities vastly outnumber human users [^1]. The majority of Oasis clients are drawn from the Fortune 500, and new annual recurring revenue grew fivefold over the past year [^1].
