## About

Mixpanel is a product analytics platform that tracks user interactions with web and mobile applications in real time [^1]. The company was founded in 2009 by Suhail Doshi and Tim Trefren, who met as classmates at Arizona State University [^2]. Doshi conceived the idea during an internship at Slide, a company led by PayPal co-founder Max Levchin [^3]. Mixpanel participated in Y Combinator's Summer 2009 batch [^4]. The company is headquartered in San Francisco, California [^1].
