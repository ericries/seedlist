## About

Ayar Labs develops co-packaged optics (CPO) technology for AI infrastructure, replacing traditional electrical interconnects with optical I/O to dramatically increase bandwidth and reduce power consumption in data centers [^1][^2]. The company was founded in 2015 and is based in San Jose, California [^1].
