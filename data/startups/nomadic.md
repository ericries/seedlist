## About

Nomadic builds a platform that converts large volumes of unstructured video and sensor data generated by autonomous vehicles and robotics systems into searchable, structured datasets [^1][^2]. The company was founded by CEO Mustafa Bal and CTO Varun Krishnan to solve the challenge that AV fleets generate far more video data than engineering teams can realistically watch, sort, or label [^1]. Customers include Zoox, Mitsubishi Electric (Automotive America), and Zendar [^1].
