## About

Personio is a Munich-based HR software company founded in 2015 by Hanno Renner, Roman Schumacher, Jonas Rieke, and Arseniy Vershinin [^1]. The four co-founders met while studying at the Center for Digital Technology & Management (CDTM) at the Technical University of Munich [^2]. The company builds an all-in-one HR platform for small and medium-sized enterprises, covering recruiting, onboarding, payroll, and people workflow automation [^3]. As of 2022, Personio served over 6,000 customers covering more than 500,000 employees [^4]. The company has raised a total of $772M across eight funding rounds and was valued at $8.5 billion as of June 2022 [^4][^5].
