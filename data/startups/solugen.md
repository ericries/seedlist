## About

Solugen is a biotech company founded in 2016 by Gaurab Chakrabarti and Sean Hunt that replaces petroleum-based chemicals with plant-derived substitutes [^1]. The company combines enzyme engineering with chemical processing to create carbon-negative chemicals from renewable feedstock such as dextrose [^2]. Solugen is headquartered in Houston, TX and graduated from Y Combinator's 2017 batch [^3].

## What Founders Say

Gaurab Chakrabarti and Sean Hunt were named EY Entrepreneur of the Year 2023 national award winners [^10]. The company's founding story traces back to a conversation at a med school poker game between the two co-founders [^1].
