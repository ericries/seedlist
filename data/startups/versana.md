## About

Versana is a New York-based enterprise data and digital infrastructure firm modernizing the broadly syndicated loan (BSL) and private credit markets [^1][^2]. The company operates a centralized platform that captures real-time loan data and provides transparency into loan-level details and lender positions [^1]. As of the April 2026 funding announcement, active facility coverage on the platform exceeded $4.1 trillion in notional value [^1][^2]. Versana was founded in 2021 with backing from a consortium of major U.S. banks [^2][^3].

Total capital raised to date exceeds $125 million across multiple funding rounds [^1][^2]. The April 2026 financing brought in BNP Paribas as lead, with Fitch Ventures, MassMutual Ventures, Motive Partners and Apollo joining as new strategic investors [^1][^3].
