## About

Luminai is a San Francisco-based enterprise AI automation platform for healthcare operations that helps health systems turn unstructured data into trusted intelligence for administrative and operational workflows [^1]. The company announced a major deployment with the Cleveland Clinic alongside its Series B [^2].
