## About

Afresh is a San Francisco-based AI platform for the grocery industry, focused on fresh food inventory management, forecasting, and supply chain optimization to reduce waste and improve margins [^1]. Founded in 2017, Afresh operates in more than 12,500 grocery departments across 40 states, partnering with retailers including Albertsons Companies, Meijer, and Wakefern [^1][^2]. The company reports 70% year-over-year revenue growth in 2025 and says it has prevented more than 200 million pounds of food waste to date [^1].
