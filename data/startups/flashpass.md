## About

Flashpass is a Columbus, Ohio-based workforce training platform that helps state governments design AI-era retraining programs [^1]. The company hit $4 million in ARR in its first year through its Ohio pilot, earning over $1.5 million in profit [^1]. Flashpass offers micro-certifications across energy, defense, and cybersecurity sectors [^1].
