## About

Reserv is a New York-based AI-native third-party administrator (TPA) and claims technology provider serving the property and casualty (P&C) insurance industry [^1][^2]. The company was founded in 2022 by CJ Przybyl, previously Chief Strategy Officer at Snapsheet, and Martha Dreiling, formerly an executive at Rhino and OnDeck [^4]. Reserv currently employs over 500 claims adjusters and serves nearly 200 insurers, corporate captives, MGAs, and brokers, having reached $100M in annual recurring revenue and more than doubled its claims processing capacity year over year [^1][^2]. The Series C round will support Reserv's expansion of annual claims processing capacity from 500,000 complex claims to a target of 30 million over the next four years [^2].
