## About

Bathhouse is a New York-based social wellness destination offering sauna, contrast therapy, and communal bathing experiences [^1][^2]. The company was founded in 2019 in Williamsburg, Brooklyn, by real estate investor Travis Talmadge and hospitality operator Jason Goodman [^2]. A second location opened in the Flatiron neighborhood of Manhattan in 2024, with planned expansion to Los Angeles (taking over Amoeba Records' Sunset Boulevard location), Chicago, and Nashville [^2]. The May 2026 round was reported as a $35M venture financing led by Imaginary Ventures, with participation from 28 total investors including members of celebrity venture firm Plus Capital's collective [^1][^2].
