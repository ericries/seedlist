## About

DOJO AI is a Lisbon and London-based agentic marketing platform founded in August 2024 by a team from Feedzai and OutSystems [^1][^2]. Its patent-pending technology enables AI agents to take action on marketing performance data rather than just reporting on it, a paradigm the company describes as "agentic marketing," and serves 100+ brands across the U.S. and U.K. [^1][^2]. The $6M (~€5.1M) seed round, announced April 22, 2026, was led by Armilar at a $30M valuation, with participation from Heartfelt VC [^1][^2].
