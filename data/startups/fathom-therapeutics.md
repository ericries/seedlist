## About

Fathom Therapeutics (formerly Atommap Corp.) is a New York-headquartered drug-discovery company using physics-based simulation and AI to design novel small molecules by modeling protein motion at atomic resolution [^1][^2]. Its Microcosmos platform claims to accelerate protein-dynamics modeling by ~10,000x without compromising accuracy [^1]. Founders Huafeng Xu (CEO), Yujie Wu (CTO), and Jesus Izaguirre (Chief Computational Scientist) came together to translate decades of computational chemistry research into a lab-in-the-loop drug design engine; the company also operates a second office in Boston [^1][^2].
