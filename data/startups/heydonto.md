## About

HeyDonto AI Technology is a Knoxville, Tennessee-based dental technology company that builds Conduit, a dental interoperability exchange platform enabling seamless data sharing between dental and medical EHR systems [^1]. The company addresses a longstanding gap in healthcare where dental data has been siloed from broader medical records. HeyDonto is a member of the Oral Health Interoperability Alliance [^1].
