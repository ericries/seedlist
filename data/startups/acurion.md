## About

Acurion is a San Diego-based precision oncology company developing AI-powered image analysis tools for biomarker detection [^1]. The company's OncoGaze platform extracts cancer biomarker insights from routine pathology slides prepared during standard clinical care, enabling scalable access to precision medicine without requiring additional tissue or specialized molecular testing [^1].
