## About

NeoCognition is an AI research lab developing self-learning agents designed to become domain experts through autonomous learning, in the way humans specialize [^1]. The company spun out of Ohio State University in 2025, founded by professor Yu Su, and emerged from stealth with approximately 15 employees, most holding PhDs [^1]. Su has argued that current agents succeed "only about 50% of the time," and NeoCognition's approach focuses on building systems that can rapidly master the unique rules and relationships of any professional environment [^1]. The company is targeting enterprise clients, including SaaS companies looking to integrate AI agent capabilities into existing products [^1].
