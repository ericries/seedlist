## About

EnerVenue is a Fremont, California-based company developing metal-hydrogen battery technology originally proven by NASA for space applications, adapted for grid-scale energy storage [^1]. The company's batteries are designed for long-duration, safe, infrastructure-grade storage with up to 30,000 cycles [^2].
