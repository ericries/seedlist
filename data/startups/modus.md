## About

Modus is an AI-native audit technology platform and holding company that partners with established accounting firms to equip them with modern technology designed to improve audit quality, efficiency, and scalability [^1]. Founded in 2025, the company combines proprietary AI, regulatory expertise, and an investment strategy that partners with growing audit-first accounting firms [^1]. Since its launch in June 2025, Modus has invested in a top 200 accounting platform with over $30 million in revenue [^1].
