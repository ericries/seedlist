## About

Loop is a San Francisco-based full-stack, verticalized AI platform for logistics and supply chains that predicts disruptions [^1][^2]. Co-founders Matt McKinney (CEO) and Shaosu Liu (CTO) met while working at Uber [^1]. The platform helps enterprises convert fragmented operational and financial data into improved visibility and decision-making [^2]. Customers include Outset Medical, Clemens Food Group, Olipop, Kendra Scott, and Dot Foods [^2].
