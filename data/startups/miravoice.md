## About

Miravoice builds an AI voice agent that conducts long-form phone surveys and voice interviews for quantitative data collection, replacing human interviewers [^1]. The platform handles surveys with more than 120 questions lasting over 40 minutes, supporting open-ended responses, multiple choice, and Likert scales [^1]. The system operates multilingually and processes both outbound and inbound calls [^2].
