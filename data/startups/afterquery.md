## About

AfterQuery is a San Francisco-based AI research lab that assembles verified domain experts to create structured reasoning datasets for frontier AI model training [^1]. Founded in January 2025 by Spencer Mateega and Carlos Georgescu, the company has built a network of nearly 100,000 verified professionals across medicine, law, finance, and engineering [^1]. AfterQuery reports surpassing $100 million in annual revenue run rate and counts every major frontier AI lab as a customer [^1][^2].
