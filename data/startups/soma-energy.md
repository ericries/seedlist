## About

Soma Energy is a startup founded by former Amazon Web Services energy managers that uses AI to deliver grid capacity to data centers [^1]. CEO Ath Caramanolis created AWS's renewable energy optimization program, CTO Mario Souto built the machine learning platform at AWS, and Chief AI Scientist Henrique Hoeltgebaum developed AI models at AWS [^1]. The company launched in February 2024 with 18 employees and has deployed 2 gigawatts of assets in the U.S. with customers including independent power producers and five data center companies such as H5 Data Centers [^1][^2].
