## About

Trent AI is a London-based cybersecurity startup that emerged from stealth in April 2026 with $13 million in seed funding [^1]. Founded by former Amazon Web Services engineers Eno Thereska (CEO), Neil Lawrence (Chief Scientist, also a machine learning professor at Cambridge University), and Zhenwen Dai (CTO), the company builds a layered platform to secure AI agents throughout their entire lifecycle [^1]. The platform finds cybersecurity issues in AI agents and the code they generate, using AI agents to identify vulnerabilities, rank their severity, suggest remediation steps, and track security changes over time [^1].
