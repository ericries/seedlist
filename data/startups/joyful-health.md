## About

Joyful Health is a healthcare financial infrastructure platform that helps medical providers recover unpaid insurance claims and optimize revenue cycle management [^1][^2]. Founded in 2023 by Eliana Berger, the platform has processed more than $1.4 billion in transactions and delivers a recovery rate exceeding 95% across healthcare specialties [^1].
