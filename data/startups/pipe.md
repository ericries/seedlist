## About

Pipe is a San Francisco-based fintech providing embedded capital and financial solutions for small and medium-sized businesses [^1]. The company integrates working capital access into existing software platforms such as POS systems and invoicing tools, allowing merchants to tap financing without leaving their operating systems [^1]. Founded in 2019, Pipe previously operated as a revenue-based financing marketplace and pivoted in 2024 to focus on embedded capital under a relaunched product called Pipe Capital [^2]. CEO Claurelle Rakipovic was named to the role in December 2025 after a restructuring that reduced headcount by approximately 50% [^2].
