## About

Airtable is a no-code/low-code platform that combines the functionality of a spreadsheet with a relational database, enabling teams to build collaborative applications without programming expertise [^1]. The company was co-founded in 2012 by Howie Liu (CEO), Emmett Nicholas (CTO), and Andrew Ofstad, and is headquartered in San Francisco [^2]. As of 2024, Airtable serves over 500,000 organizations including 80% of the Fortune 100, with approximately $478 million in ARR [^2]. The company has raised a total of $1.36 billion across seven funding rounds [^3].
