## About

Nura Studios is a San Francisco-based AI-assisted animation platform that emerged from stealth on April 30, 2026 with seed funding from iGlobe Partners, Fellows Fund, and Shanda Ventures [^1][^2]. The company's flagship product, Showcraft, transforms existing game and animation assets into episodic series and films, allowing studios to extend their IP into new formats while preserving artistic intent [^1]. The founding team comprises platform veterans from Apple, Unity, and Roblox: Sylvio Drouin (CEO, co-founder), Stefano Corazza (Chairman, co-founder), and Gabriele Farina (CTO) [^1].
