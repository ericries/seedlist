## About

Chegg is an education technology company that started as a textbook rental service for college students and evolved into a digital learning platform [^1]. The company was incorporated in 2005 by Osman Rashid, Aayush Phumbhra, and Josh Carlson, growing out of "Cheggpost," a Craigslist-style message board for Iowa State University students [^2]. Chegg went public on the NYSE on November 13, 2013, raising $187.5 million at a $1.1 billion valuation [^3].
