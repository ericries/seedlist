## About

Volant Aerotech is a Shanghai-based developer of commercial passenger eVTOL (electric vertical take-off and landing) aircraft [^1][^2]. Its flagship VE25-100 model has completed piloted test flights and secured approximately 1,900 orders from customers including China Southern General Aviation and Asian Express Aviation [^1]. The Series C round is the largest single financing in China's high-airworthiness commercial passenger eVTOL sector to date [^2][^3]. Cumulative funding stands at approximately ¥4 billion (~$586M) [^3].

CYGNUS Equity acted as exclusive financial advisor on the Series C [^1]. Use of proceeds: airworthiness certification of the VE25-100, mass production capacity, flight testing, supply chain development, and next-generation technology reserves [^1][^3].
