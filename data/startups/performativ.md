## About

Performativ is a Copenhagen-based AI-native wealth management operating system, led by CEO Albert Geisler Fox [^1]. The April 2026 Series A is intended to power the next era of wealth management technology, with Deutsche Börse Group and Rabo Investments (Rabobank) joining the cap table [^1].
