## About

Generare is a Paris-based techbio company founded in 2023 that builds the largest dataset of undiscovered molecular chemistry from microbial genomes for drug development [^1]. The company's evolution-based platform unlocks information encoded within microbial genomes, cataloguing novel small molecules characterized by structure, biological activity, and drug potential [^1][^2]. Generare serves pharmaceutical and agrochemical companies and aims to scale its discovery capacity tenfold by 2027 [^2].
