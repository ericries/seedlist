## About

HexemBio is a biotech startup focused on stem cell rejuvenation technology [^1]. The company's approach involves extracting blood stem cells (Hematopoietic Stem Cells) from a patient's bone marrow, rejuvenating them in the lab, and returning them intravenously [^1]. HexemBio is initially targeting bone marrow transplant failure, and in preclinical studies has reduced transplant failure by up to 10x in mice [^1]. The company is an IndieBio (SOSV) portfolio company [^2].
