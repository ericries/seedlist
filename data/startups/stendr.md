## About

Stendr is an Oslo-based AI counter-drone defense startup co-founded by Aleksander Leonard Larsen (also co-founder of Sky Mavis / Axie Infinity) [^1]. The company is building a vertically integrated stack of hardware, software, and AI focused on drone detection and defense [^1]. The $5.4M pre-seed was one of the largest in Nordic defence tech history [^1]. Europe's counter-drone market was $1.24 billion in 2025 and is projected to reach $4.16 billion by 2030 [^2].
