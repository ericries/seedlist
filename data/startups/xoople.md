## About

Xoople is a Spain-based Earth intelligence company developing a satellite constellation to collect high-resolution data for AI and deep learning models [^1]. The company is building sensors in partnership with U.S. space and defense contractor L3Harris Technologies, designed to collect data that is "two orders of magnitude better than existing monitoring systems" [^1]. The company describes itself as the world's first Earth Intelligence company [^2].
