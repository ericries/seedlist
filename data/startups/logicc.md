## About

Logicc is a Hamburg-based secure AI platform for regulated industries, integrating leading AI models (ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini) behind a unified, GDPR- and German-data-protection-compliant interface with zero-data-retention and zero-knowledge architecture, targeting legal, healthcare, and public-sector customers [^1][^2]. The company, founded in 2025, says it surpassed €1 million in annual recurring revenue within months of launch [^2].
