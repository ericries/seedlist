## About

Zamp is a New York-based AI-driven sales tax compliance platform that combines AI agents with tax and legal professionals to manage filing, registration, notices, taxability mapping, and data normalization across all 50 US states and 100+ countries [^1][^2]. Founded in 2022 by Rohit Bhadange (CEO), Edward Lando, and Clete Werts, the company serves more than 120 accounting firms and reports 99.97% filing accuracy on $300M+ in remitted taxes [^1][^2]. The April 2026 round coincided with the global launch of Zamp OS, the company's "operating system of sales tax" [^1].
