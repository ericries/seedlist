## About

Airbase is a software platform that modernizes radio-frequency (RF) spectrum coordination for government, military, and commercial applications [^1]. The company automates the manual spectrum allocation process, including deconflicting spectrum use among operators and managing databases that currently rely on decades-old systems and static PDFs [^1]. Note: this is a different company from Airbase the spend management platform.
