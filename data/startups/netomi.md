## About

Netomi is a San Francisco-based enterprise agentic AI platform for customer experience, founded and led by CEO Puneet Mehta [^1][^2]. The company's platform powers AI customer service agents across chat, email, and voice channels for large enterprises including Delta Air Lines, United Airlines, MetLife, Paramount, DraftKings, the NBA, and Ingram Micro [^1]. Netomi's models leverage technology from OpenAI, Anthropic, and Google [^2]. As of the April 2026 Series C, the company employs approximately 170 people and has raised more than $160 million since its founding [^2].

On April 30, 2026, Netomi announced a $110M Series C led by Accenture Ventures with participation from Adobe Ventures, WndrCo, SLW, NAVER Ventures, Metis Strategy, and Fin Capital [^1][^2][^3]. As part of the round, Accenture entered a global alliance with Netomi to deploy the platform across Accenture's enterprise client base, and Netomi's platform was integrated into Adobe's Brand Concierge agentic ecosystem [^1]. Jeffrey Katzenberg, managing partner of WndrCo and co-founder of DreamWorks, joined Netomi's board of directors [^1][^2]. Earlier individual backers include Greg Brockman, Demis Hassabis, and Mustafa Suleyman [^1].
