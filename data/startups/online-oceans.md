## About

Online Oceans is a UK-based defence and maritime-security startup building autonomous surface vessels and fleet software [^1][^2]. The company raised a £4 million seed round announced April 30, 2026, led by Seraphim Space with participation from Peter Rive, Frank Thieser, Florian Seibel, and Koro Capital [^1][^2]. Its flagship product is Scout, a compact, solar-powered autonomous surface vessel built for long-endurance deployment, paired with Tether, a cloud-based command platform that lets operators monitor missions, manage fleets, and access real-time intelligence from multiple vessels simultaneously [^1]. Founded in early 2025 by George Morton and Alistair Douglas, the company targets anti-submarine operations, subsea-infrastructure protection, border surveillance, and counter-narcotics missions [^1].
