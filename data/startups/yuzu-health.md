## About

Yuzu Health is a next-generation third-party administrator (TPA) serving as the infrastructure behind health insurance plans [^1]. Founded in 2022, the company powers health insurers, brokers, and employers across all 50 states, supporting thousands of employers and facilitating more than $1 billion in claims payments volume [^1].
