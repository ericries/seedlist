## About

Firenze is a UK fintech specializing in Lombard lending — an embedded finance platform that enables wealth managers, IFAs, and investment platforms to offer loans secured against clients' investment portfolios [^1][^2]. The company was founded in 2024 by second-time founder David Newman and is a partner to UK firms representing nearly £200 billion in assets under management [^1]. The April 2026 round will be used to double headcount amid rising borrower demand, enhance SaaS offerings for banks, and expand into new jurisdictions [^1][^2].
