## About

Glydways is a San Francisco-based startup developing personal autonomous pods designed to operate on dedicated 2-meter-wide lanes in cities [^1]. The company has raised over $250 million to date and is valued at approximately $700 million [^2]. Investors include Sam Altman, Khosla Ventures, Suzuki Motor Corporation, ACS Group, and Mitsui Chemicals [^1][^2].
