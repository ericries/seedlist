## About

StairMed is a Shanghai-based company founded in 2021 that develops minimally invasive implantable brain-machine interface (BMI) technologies, including electrodes, systems, algorithms, and surgical robotics [^1]. The company focuses on enabling brain-machine integration for medical applications such as treating Parkinson's disease and epilepsy [^1]. StairMed has completed clinical implants and is advancing toward large-scale multicenter registration clinical trials planned for mid-2026, aiming to enroll approximately 40 patients [^2].
