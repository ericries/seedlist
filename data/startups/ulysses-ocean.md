## About

Ulysses is an Irish-founded company building autonomous ocean robots to restore marine habitats such as seagrass, which acts as a carbon sink [^1]. Founded in 2023 by Akhil Voorakkara, Will O'Brien, Jamie Wedderburn, and Colm O'Brien, the company has built drones that automate ocean habitat restoration [^1].
