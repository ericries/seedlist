## About

K1x is an AI-native private markets tax data platform automating the extraction, aggregation, validation, and movement of K-1, K-3, 1099, and related tax data [^1]. The company serves more than 40,000 organizations, including 44 of the 100 largest U.S. institutional investors and 20 of the top 25 accounting firms [^1]. The $175 million growth investment was led by Sumeru Equity Partners, which became majority shareholder [^1].
