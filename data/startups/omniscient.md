## About

Omniscient is a Paris-based decision intelligence platform built for boards and senior executives [^1]. Founded by former McKinsey consultants Arnaud d'Estienne and Mehdi Benseghir, the platform ingests over 100,000 sources including press, social media, web, video, audio, and internal pipelines, and synthesizes them into executive briefings [^1]. The company claims its AI-native approach operates 50 times faster than legacy manual monitoring workflows [^1].
