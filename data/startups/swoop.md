## About

Swoop is a consumer super-app startup founded by 19-year-old Thiel Fellow Aubrey Niederhoffer, who dropped out of UC Berkeley after his first year and relocated to Lagos in fall 2025 to build the company [^1]. The service launched first in Eswatini (the founder's home country) and has now expanded into Nigeria's Yaba neighborhood on the Lagos Mainland, with food delivery as its initial wedge [^2]. Niederhoffer describes the long-term plan as a super-app integrating food, groceries, ride-hailing, and fintech for a market with "no legacy banking infrastructure" [^1].

Demola Adesina serves as Swoop's Nigerian country manager [^2].
