## About

KEMARO is a Swiss industrial cleaning robotics company building autonomous floor-cleaning robots for complex high-debris environments such as logistics centers and manufacturing plants, founded in 2016 by Martin Gadient, Armin Koller, Thomas Oberholzer, and Nadja Gerber [^1][^2]. The company has sold over 1,500 robots to more than 600 industrial clients and is targeting deployment of 10,000 robots across Europe and the US, with a new US subsidiary headquartered in Atlanta [^1][^2]. Its flagship product is the K900 [^1].
