## About

Mirage (formerly Captions) is an AI lab that builds models for its AI video-editing app Captions, which has over 20 million global users [^1]. The company rebranded from Captions to Mirage to position itself as an AI lab producing different models, also catering to advertising and marketing industries [^1]. Captions has been downloaded over 3.2 million times in the last year and generated $28.4 million in in-app revenue [^1].
