## About

Rork is a San Francisco-based AI platform that enables users to build and launch fully functional iOS and Android mobile applications through natural language prompts [^1]. The platform generates native apps built on Swift, React Native, and Expo that can be submitted directly to the App Store and Google Play [^1]. Founded by Daniel Dhawan and Levan Kvirkvelia, the company launched publicly on February 12, 2025 with a single tweet that went viral [^2]. Rork recently acquired Paperline to strengthen its transition toward native Swift development [^1].
