## About

ComfyUI is a node-based workflow platform for AI-generated media that gives creators granular control over image, video, and audio generation from diffusion models [^1]. Founded in 2023, the company addresses limitations in prompt-based generation tools by allowing users to modify specific generation components without losing quality in adjacent areas [^1]. As of April 2026, ComfyUI has more than 4 million users and reached a $500 million post-money valuation in its Series A [^1]. CEO Yoland Yan leads the company alongside additional co-founders [^1].
