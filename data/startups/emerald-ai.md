## About

Emerald AI is a London-based startup building grid-aware energy management software that helps AI data centers connect to the power grid faster while maintaining 99.999% reliability [^1][^2]. The company has raised $68M total in 16 months since founding [^1]. Emerald partnered with NVIDIA and leading U.S. power producers including AES, Constellation Energy, Invenergy, NextEra Energy, and Vistra to open the first power-flexible, commercial AI factory at NVIDIA's 96-megawatt Vera Rubin AI Factory Research Center in Virginia [^1].
