## About

Mintlify is an AI-powered documentation platform used by over 20,000 companies including Microsoft, Anthropic, Coinbase, and PayPal, reaching more than 100 million people per year [^1]. Over 50% of traffic across Mintlify's customer base is now AI agents rather than humans, positioning the platform as infrastructure for the agent era [^1]. The company raised at a $500 million valuation [^2].
