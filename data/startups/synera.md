## About

Synera is a Bremen, Germany-based company developing an agentic AI platform for engineering that orchestrates complex product development workflows across 80+ engineering tools [^1]. Founded in 2018, the company deploys AI agents that autonomously execute tasks throughout the engineering value chain, from design and simulation to optimization [^1]. Synera has a growing U.S. presence in Boston and serves customers in aerospace, defense, consumer electronics, automotive, and industrial engineering [^1].
