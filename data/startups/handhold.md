## About

Handhold is an Estonian AI startup that builds AI account managers to automate the software buying journey from first interaction through renewal [^1][^2]. The platform deploys three agents: one qualifies leads and answers questions, another gives personalised voice demos, and the third helps onboard new users [^1]. Since its soft launch in September 2025, Handhold has acquired over 15 customers and achieved a strong six-figure ARR run rate [^1].
