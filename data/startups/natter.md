## About

Natter is a New York-based AI-powered conversation intelligence platform founded in 2021 by Charlie Woodward and James Stevens [^1]. The platform conducts one-on-one video conversations at scale to capture employee, customer, and member insights, anonymizing responses and converting thousands of simultaneous conversations into structured insights within hours [^1][^2]. On April 7, 2026, the company announced a $23 million funding round led by Renegade Partners [^1].
