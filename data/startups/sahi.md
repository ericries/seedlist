## About

Sahi is a Bengaluru-based mobile-first stock trading platform aimed at active retail traders in India [^1][^2]. The company was founded in 2023 by Dale Vaz, the former CTO of Swiggy, and Manish Jain, a former Vice President at Kotak Securities, and operates as a SEBI-registered broker on NSE and BSE through Aaritya Broking Private Limited [^2][^3]. Sahi launched operations in January 2025 [^1][^4]. As of the Series B announcement, the platform reported approximately 400,000 demat accounts opened and more than 130 million trades executed [^1][^4].
