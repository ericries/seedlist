## About

Eventbrite is an event management and ticketing platform founded in 2006 by Kevin Hartz, Julia Hartz, and Renaud Visage in San Francisco [^1][^2]. The company went public on the NYSE in September 2018 at $23 per share, raising $230 million [^3][^4]. In March 2026, Bending Spoons completed its acquisition of Eventbrite for approximately $500 million in an all-cash transaction [^5][^6].
