## About

Spotify is a music and audio streaming platform founded in 2006 by Daniel Ek and Martin Lorentzon in Stockholm, Sweden [^1]. The company went public via a direct listing on the New York Stock Exchange on April 3, 2018, bypassing a traditional IPO [^2].
