## About

Modern Relay is a San Francisco and Barcelona-based startup developing shared infrastructure for AI agents in the enterprise [^1]. Founded in 2025 by Ragnor Comerford and Aaron Goh, the company just released its first open-source product, Omnigraph — a Git-style graph database built for a world where agents are first-class operators [^1]. The system gives organizations a shared foundation for how the company works so that software and teams coordinate from the same reality [^1].
