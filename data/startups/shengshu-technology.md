## About

ShengShu Technology is a Beijing-based AI startup developing multimodal generative AI models, best known for its Vidu video generation platform [^1]. Founded in early 2023 by Tsinghua University alum Zhu Jun, ShengShu became the first Chinese company to release a video generation model when it launched Vidu in April 2024 [^2]. The company is building what it calls a "general world model" that uses AI to bridge the digital world of games and AI-generated video with the physical world of autonomous driving and robots [^1].
