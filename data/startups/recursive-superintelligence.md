## About

Recursive Superintelligence is an AI research lab building systems designed to improve themselves without human intervention [^1][^2]. The London-incorporated company was founded in late 2025 by Richard Socher (former Salesforce chief scientist) and Tim Rocktäschel (until recently director and principal scientist at Google DeepMind, where his team won the ICML 2024 Best Paper Award for the Genie interactive world model) [^2][^3]. The founding team of roughly 20 people includes former OpenAI researchers Josh Tobin, Jeff Clune, and Tim Shi, alongside alumni from Google and Meta [^1][^3]. The company was incorporated in London on December 31, 2025, and raised its $500M seed round approximately four months after founding [^2][^3].
