## About

Steno is a Los Angeles-based leader in tech-enabled court reporting and litigation support services [^1]. Founded in 2018, the company launched Transcript Genius in 2024, an AI-powered transcript analysis tool [^1]. Steno has now raised $150M in total, including both equity and debt financing [^1].
