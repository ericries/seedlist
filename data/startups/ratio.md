## About

Ratio is a San Francisco-based AI fintech platform that solves cash flow constraints for B2B tech scale-ups [^1]. The company achieved GAAP profitability in August 2025 and reported 349% year-over-year ARR growth in 2025, following 800%+ growth in 2024 [^1]. Ratio also secured $100M in lending capacity alongside the equity raise [^1]. The company's new AI Proposal Agent helps sellers structure offers to remove buyer hesitation on payment terms [^1].
