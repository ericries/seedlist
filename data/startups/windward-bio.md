## About

Windward Bio is a Basel, Switzerland-based clinical-stage biotechnology company developing long-acting therapies for advanced immunological conditions [^1]. The company was founded in January 2025 by Luca Santarelli, MD, who serves as CEO and Board Chair [^1] [^2]. Its lead program is WIN378, an ultra long-acting anti-TSLP monoclonal antibody in Phase 2/3 development for asthma with planned Phase 2 studies in COPD; the pipeline also includes WIN027, a long-acting anti-TSLPxIL-13 bispecific in Phase 1 [^1]. On May 4, 2026, Windward Bio announced a $165M crossover financing led by OrbiMed, bringing total funding raised since launch to $365M [^1] [^2].
