## About

Treeline is a San Francisco-based startup building a "modern IT operating system" — an AI and software-first alternative to the managed services firms that still power most corporate IT [^1][^2]. Founded by Peter Doyle, who previously spent about a decade in venture capital at Accel backing IT infrastructure and security companies like PagerDuty, Heptio, and ServiceChannel [^1]. Treeline's platform replaces fragmented, labor-driven IT service models with a centralized software architecture that automates routine operations while elevating human experts to focus on architecture, risk, and strategy [^2].
