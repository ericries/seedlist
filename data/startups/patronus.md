## About

Patronus is a Berlin-based eldertech company building a senior-friendly emergency smartwatch and a connected family app, founded in 2022 by Ben Staudt and Tim Wagner [^1][^2]. The watch has an integrated SIM and connects to a 24/7 emergency call center across Germany, while the family app surfaces wear status, location, and emergency notifications [^1]. The company reports 25,000 active users and 50,000 connected family members, with more than half a million emergency calls handled to date [^1]. Funds will expand the company's lead in mobile emergency response and develop new products around family, wellbeing, and an AI-powered daily companion for older adults [^1][^2].
