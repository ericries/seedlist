## About

Golden Child is a Miami-based premium pet food startup co-founded by Jack Abraham (founder of Atomic and co-founder of Hims & Hers), Hilary Coles (co-founder of Hims & Hers), and Quentin Lacornerie (co-founder and Chief Product Officer) [^1][^2]. The company emerged from stealth in April 2026 with a combined Seed and Series A totaling $37M and a mission framed around fresh, intentional pet meals [^1][^2].
