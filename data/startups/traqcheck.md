## About

TraqCheck is a London-headquartered AI-driven platform that helps enterprises conduct background checks and automate hiring workflows [^1]. Founded in 2020 by Jaibir Nihal Singh, Armaan Mehta, and Rishabh Jain, the company serves nearly 300 enterprise customers across India and Europe including Bajaj Capital and Reliance Retail [^1]. Its flagship agent, Trace, automates background verification, and its newly launched agent, Nina, handles talent acquisition from sourcing to qualification [^1].
