## About

SpectronRx is an Indiana-based radiopharmaceutical company specializing in medical isotope production and contract manufacturing [^1]. The company operates a campus at Grissom Aeroplex spanning 34 acres with approximately 200,000 square feet of GMP-compliant manufacturing space [^1]. SpectronRx provides CDMO and CMO services for diagnostic imaging and radioligand therapies [^1].
