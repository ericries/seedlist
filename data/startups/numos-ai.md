## About

Numos is an AI platform built for enterprise finance teams that analyzes transactions across accounting systems, billing tools, data warehouses, and spreadsheets to help organizations make faster, more strategic decisions [^1]. Co-founded by Parijat Sarkar (former SVP at Zenefits) and Mitul Tiwari (PhD, AI systems builder at LinkedIn and ServiceNow), the platform orchestrates specialized agents that understand financial context, automate repetitive workflows, and continuously evaluate their outputs [^1]. Unlike black-box AI finance tools, Numos shows its reasoning at every step, enabling traceability for variance analysis, reconciliations, and quote-to-cash automations [^1]. The company reports 80% faster FP&A reporting cycles and book-close times cut by more than half [^1].
