## About

Cloneable is a Raleigh, North Carolina-based AI startup founded in 2023 that shadows human experts in heavy industries like energy and utilities and replicates their specialized workflows into autonomous agents [^1]. The company offers automated infrastructure inspection and agentic products that codify expert knowledge for scalable AI automation, and reports 100x ARR growth between February 2025 and the end of 2025, with customers including American Electric Power, Southern California Edison, and Burns & McDonnell [^1]. Cloneable was founded by Lia Reich (CEO), Tyler Collins (CTO), and Patrick Lohman (CRO) [^1].
