## About

Via Separations develops modular filtration systems that replace heat-based industrial separation processes with energy-efficient alternatives [^1]. Founded in 2016, the company's technology can reduce energy use at the separation step by up to 90 percent [^1]. Via has proven commercial performance in pulp and paper and is expanding into refining and chemicals [^1].
