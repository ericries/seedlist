## About

INXY is a Cyprus-based, EU-authorised cross-border payment infrastructure provider that bridges digital assets with traditional payment systems [^1]. The platform enables businesses to accept stablecoins, convert them to fiat or other stablecoins, and process international payouts [^1]. INXY serves 100+ global businesses including advertising networks, freelance platforms, gaming studios, and SaaS companies, processing over $2 billion in annual transaction volume with 500% year-on-year growth [^1].
