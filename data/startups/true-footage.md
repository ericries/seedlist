## About

True Footage is an Austin, Texas-based technology-driven residential appraisal and appraiser services company that combines valuation expertise with proprietary AI-powered software, including TrueTracts, to deliver faster and more consistent home valuations [^1]. Founded in 2021, the company employs hundreds of appraisers across 33 states and partners with 300 mortgage lenders, including 7 of the top 10 [^1]. The company has raised over $105 million to date [^1].
