## About

Aria Networks is building AI-native networking infrastructure for data centers, developing hardware-agnostic Ethernet switches and cluster software designed to maximize token efficiency across AI workloads [^1]. The company's Deep Networking platform includes the Aria Switch 800G and 1.6T alongside software that embeds AI reasoning for telemetry [^1]. The platform operates across AI chips from providers such as Nvidia and Google, enabling enterprises to scale or switch hardware without redesigning infrastructure [^1].
