## About

Belo is a Buenos Aires-based crypto fintech that combines fiat currencies (Argentine peso, Brazilian real) with digital dollars and stablecoins to enable cross-border payments across Latin America [^1][^2]. The company was founded in 2021 by Manuel Beaudroit (CEO) and Edwin Rager (Strategy Director) [^2][^3]. Belo was one of the first platforms to issue a crypto Mastercard in Latin America and reports more than 3 million users with over $1.3 billion in processed volume [^2]. The platform supports payments in Argentina through Brazil's Pix network using interoperable QR codes [^1]. The company says it has been profitable for three consecutive years [^1][^3].
