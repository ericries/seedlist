## About

Zig.ai is a San Francisco-based AI-powered sales execution platform that embeds AI agents into revenue workflows, founded in 2025 and publicly launched in January 2026 [^1][^2]. The company positions its product as a "revenue OS" unifying CRM, enrichment, sequencing, and call recording, priced on an outcome-based model rather than per-seat [^1]. Its flagship agent ZigScribe attends sales meetings, drafts follow-up emails, updates CRMs, and schedules next steps autonomously [^3].
