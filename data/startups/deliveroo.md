## About

Deliveroo is a food delivery platform founded in 2013 in London by Will Shu and Greg Orlowski [^1]. The company IPO'd on the London Stock Exchange in March 2021 at a valuation of approximately $10.5 billion [^2], and was acquired by DoorDash in October 2025 for $3.9 billion [^3].
