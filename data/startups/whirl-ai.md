## About

Whirl AI is a San Francisco-based startup building generative AI agents for enterprise IT modernization [^1][^2]. Founded by Sunny Bedi, who spent two decades in CIO and IT leadership positions at VMware, NVIDIA, and Snowflake [^1]. The platform continuously ingests metadata from enterprise systems and converts it into a living, searchable knowledge base, with AI agents helping IT teams research, design, develop, implement, and test changes [^1]. The company emerged from stealth on March 31, 2026 [^1].
