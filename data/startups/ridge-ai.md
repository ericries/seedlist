## About

Ridge AI is a Seattle-based startup building AI-native embedded analytics for B2B software companies [^1][^2]. Co-founded by Ellie Fields, who spent over a decade at Tableau before serving as Chief Product and Engineering Officer at Salesloft, and Jeff Heer, a Stanford professor whose open-source tools include Vega, Vega-Lite, D3.js, and the Mosaic framework [^1][^2]. The platform enables companies to deploy interactive dashboards and AI data agents in hours using browser-native processing via DuckDB and WebAssembly, eliminating the need for server-side infrastructure [^1]. The company emerged from stealth on April 6, 2026 [^1].
