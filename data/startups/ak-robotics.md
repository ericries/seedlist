## About

A&K Robotics is a Vancouver, British Columbia-based autonomous mobility company building self-driving passenger pods for airports, led by CEO Matthew Anderson and COO Jessica Yip [^1][^2]. The flagship product, Cruz, transports passengers through high-traffic dynamic environments like airport terminals to address accessibility and congestion challenges [^1][^2]. The Series A funds support the transition from pilot programs to permanent deployments and expand production capacity [^2].
