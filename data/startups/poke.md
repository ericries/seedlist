## About

Poke, created by The Interaction Company of California, is a Palo Alto-based AI agent that lives inside iMessage, SMS, Telegram, and WhatsApp [^1]. Users interact with Poke via text messages to handle everyday tasks including daily planning, calendar management, health and fitness tracking, smart home control, and photo editing [^1]. The company was co-founded by Marvin von Hagen and Felix Schlegel, who previously co-founded TUM Boring and won Elon Musk's 2021 Not-a-Boring Competition [^2].
