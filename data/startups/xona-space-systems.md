## About

Xona Space Systems is building a next-generation satellite navigation system designed as a high-precision, resilient alternative to GPS [^1]. The company develops low-earth orbit (LEO) satellites that deliver centimeter-accurate positioning for defense, autonomous vehicles, and critical infrastructure.
