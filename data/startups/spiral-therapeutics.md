## About

Spiral Therapeutics is a clinical-stage biotechnology company focused on developing novel therapies for inner ear disorders, including hearing loss and balance disorders [^1]. Its lead program, SPT-2101, delivered a statistically significant reduction of overall vertigo in patients with Meniere's disease, and the company is preparing for a registrational clinical trial [^1]. Concurrent with the financing, Spiral announced a strategic R&D collaboration with Advanced Bionics to explore drug delivery at the time of cochlear implantation [^1].
