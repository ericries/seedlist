## About

Tipalti is a fintech company providing AI-powered accounts payable, procurement, and global mass payments automation for mid-market and high-growth companies [^1]. Founded in 2010 by Chen Amit and Oren Zeev, the company was created to address the problem that popular payment processors like PayPal, Square, and Stripe focused on collecting payments rather than making them [^2]. Tipalti is headquartered in Foster City, California [^3]. As of 2022, the company processed $43 billion in annualized payment volume [^4]. Clients include GoDaddy, Scribd, Noom, and Typeform [^1].
