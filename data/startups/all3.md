## About

All3 is a London-based deep-tech construction company that closed a $25 million seed round on April 29, 2026 led by RTP Global [^1][^2]. The company is building an integrated stack for construction automation comprising an AI architecture platform, robotised off-site factories, and Mantis — an autonomous legged robot for on-site assembly [^1][^3]. Founded in 2023 by Rodion Shishkov and Slava Bocharov, the team previously co-founded Samokat, the dark-store rapid-delivery service that became Russia's largest food-delivery platform and was acquired for $1.5 billion within five years of launch [^1][^3]. The company says its approach can deliver up to 30% cost savings, 50% timeline reductions, and 25% less embodied carbon than traditional construction [^1]. R&D operations are based in London and Belgrade, with first commercial deployments planned in Germany [^1].
