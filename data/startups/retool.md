## About

Retool is a developer platform for building internal tools, founded in 2017 by David Hsu. The company went through Y Combinator's Winter 2017 batch and has grown to serve tens of thousands of companies [^1]. Retool has taken a distinctive approach to fundraising, raising small rounds at lower valuations to minimize dilution and preserve upside for employees [^2].
