## About

CMR Surgical is a Cambridge, UK-based surgical robotics company founded in 2014 by Martin Frost, Luke Hares, Mark Slack, and Keith Marshall [^1][^2]. The company develops the Versius Surgical Robotic System, a modular robot-assisted surgery platform designed to expand access to minimally invasive surgery [^1]. Versius features independent modular arms intended to be more flexible and cost-effective than incumbent systems like Intuitive Surgical's da Vinci [^3]. As of 2025, the Versius system has been used in over 30,000 procedures across more than 30 countries, making it the second most-adopted soft tissue surgical robot globally [^8]. The company received FDA 510(k) clearance for its Versius Plus system and is expanding into the U.S. market [^8].

Martin Frost served as CEO from founding until January 2020, when Per Vegard Nerseth took over [^2]. Massimiliano Colella became CEO in January 2025 after serving as interim CEO from October 2024 [^9]. The company achieved a $3 billion valuation in its 2021 Series D round [^5].
