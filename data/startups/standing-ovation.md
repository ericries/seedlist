## About

Standing Ovation is a Paris-based precision fermentation company producing dairy proteins — specifically casein — from dairy waste streams using a proprietary fermentation process [^1][^2]. Founded in 2020, the company has developed a patented ingredient called Advanced Casein, which offers nutritional, taste, and functional qualities equivalent to livestock-derived protein with significantly lower CO2 emissions and reduced water and land use [^1]. The company plans to launch commercially in the United States first, followed by Europe and Asia starting in late 2027 [^1][^2].
