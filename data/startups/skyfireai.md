## About

SkyfireAI is a dual-use AI company building an AI-native platform for autonomous, coordinated multi-ship drone operations and robotic systems, headquartered in Huntsville, Alabama [^1] [^2]. The company was founded in late 2022 by entrepreneurs and veterans drawn from the U.S. Navy, the intelligence community, and DARPA [^1]. SkyfireAI serves public safety, defense, and critical-infrastructure customers, including first responders [^1] [^2].

Andrew Ng's AI Fund has been involved with the company since its founding [^2].
