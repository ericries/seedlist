## About

Sycamore builds an operating system for autonomous enterprise AI, enabling organizations to discover, build, deploy, observe, and evolve AI agents with governance and security controls [^1]. The company was founded by Sri Viswanath, former CTO of Atlassian and former investor at Coatue Management [^1][^2].

| 2026-03-30 | Seed | $65M | Coatue, Lightspeed Venture Partners | Abstract Ventures, Dell Technologies Capital, 8VC, Fellows Fund, E14 Fund [^1][^2] |

Notable angel investors include Bob McGrew (former OpenAI CRO), Lip-Bu Tan (Intel CEO), Ali Ghodsi (Databricks CEO), BJ Jenkins (Palo Alto Networks President), Frederic Kerrest (Okta co-founder), Bipul Sinha (Rubrik CEO), and Mike Knoop (Zapier co-founder) [^1].
