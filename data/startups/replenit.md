## About

Replenit is a Warsaw-based startup building an AI decision engine for retail that turns customer signals into real-time, individualized decisions [^1]. The platform adds a reasoning layer on top of existing data warehouses and marketing orchestration tools to help retailers improve retention and increase revenue [^1]. Notable angel investor Mati Staniszewski, CEO and co-founder of ElevenLabs, participated in the round [^1]. L'Occitane en Provence reported a 235% increase in post-purchase revenue after deploying Replenit's engine, and iBOOD attributes 6.3% of total company revenue to Replenit-driven decisions [^2].
