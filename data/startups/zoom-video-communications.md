## About

Zoom Video Communications is a video-first communications platform founded in 2011 by Eric Yuan, a former Cisco/WebEx engineer [^1]. The company priced its IPO at $36/share on April 17, 2019 and began trading on NASDAQ under ticker "ZM" on April 18, 2019, opening at $65 (up ~80% from the offer price) and closing near $62 for a first-day market value of approximately $16 billion [^2][^5][^6][^7].
