## About

Zoom Video Communications is a video-first communications platform founded in 2011 by Eric Yuan, a former Cisco/WebEx engineer [^1]. The company went public on the NASDAQ in April 2019 with shares opening at $65, more than 70% above the $36 offer price [^2].
