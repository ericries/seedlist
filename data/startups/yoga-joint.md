## About

Yoga Joint is an infrared fitness brand offering signature vinyasa yoga and high-intensity strength training, founded by Paige Held [^1]. The capital raised in April 2026 supports expansion into New York City, with first locations planned for Fall 2026 and a goal of 15+ studios across New York City and surrounding markets by 2030 [^1].
