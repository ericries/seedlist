## About

Verda (formerly DataCrunch) is a Helsinki-based AI cloud infrastructure company building what it describes as Europe's first AI cloud hyperscaler [^1][^2]. The company offers on-demand GPU clusters, serverless inference, and AI model hosting powered by 100% renewable energy from the Nordics [^2]. Verda rebranded from DataCrunch in November 2025 and operates data center capacity in Finland and Iceland, with additional offices in London and San Francisco [^2]. The company reported that its revenue run rate doubled to over $60M in Q1 2026 and that it is cash flow positive [^1]. With the April 2026 financing, Verda plans to hire 100+ employees by year-end 2026 [^1][^3].
