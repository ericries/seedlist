## About

Lighthouse Pharmaceuticals is a clinical-stage biopharmaceutical company developing novel small-molecule therapies for neurodegenerative and inflammatory diseases [^1]. The company is based at the Buck Institute for Research on Aging in Novato, California [^1][^2]. Its lead program, LHP588, is a next-generation gingipain inhibitor being evaluated in the SPRING trial — a Phase 2, double-blind, placebo-controlled study in patients with P. gingivalis-positive mild to moderate Alzheimer's disease [^1][^2]. The SPRING trial is supported by a $49.2 million grant from the National Institute on Aging [^1].
