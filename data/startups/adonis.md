## About

Adonis is an AI orchestration platform for healthcare revenue cycle management (RCM), helping providers automate claims processing, flag invalid deductions, and file disputes [^1]. Founded in 2022, the company achieved more than 4x revenue growth in 2025 with net retention above 130% [^1].
