## About

Decade Energy is a Paris-based company building power infrastructure at logistics depots to electrify truck fleets across Europe [^1][^2][^3]. It was founded by Carl-Magnus Norden (founder of Volta Trucks) and the team behind its Truck-as-a-Service solution; Casper Norden, who built Truck-as-a-Service, serves as CEO [^1][^2][^3]. The €22M round, announced April 23, 2026, was led by Eiffel Investment Group (€16M equity commitment via its Eiffel Transition Infrastructure fund) and SET Ventures (€5.5M investment), with continued backing from prior seed investors Ananda Impact Ventures and Contrarian Ventures [^1][^2][^3]. The company has conducted 1,500+ depot electrification feasibility studies across Europe and has 50 projects set to begin construction in 2026 [^1].
