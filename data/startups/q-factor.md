## About

Q-Factor is a Tel Aviv, Israel-based quantum computing company that emerged from stealth in April 2026 [^1][^2]. The company develops neutral-atom quantum computing architecture designed to scale from thousands to over one million qubits [^1]. Founded by Prof. Nir Davidson (former Dean of Physics, Weizmann Institute), Prof. Ofer Firstenberg (Weizmann), Prof. Yoav Sagi (Technion), and Dr. Guy Raz, the startup commercializes decades of foundational research in atomic physics from the Technion and the Weizmann Institute of Science [^1][^2]. Both institutions are shareholders through their technology transfer arms [^1].
