## About

Credit Karma is a personal finance platform founded in 2007 by Kenneth Lin, Ryan Graciano, and Nichole Mustard in San Francisco [^1][^2]. The company provides free credit scores, credit monitoring, and financial product recommendations to over 110 million members [^3]. Intuit completed its acquisition of Credit Karma on December 3, 2020, for approximately $8.1 billion in a combination of $3.4 billion in cash and 13.3 million shares of Intuit stock and equity awards valued at $4.7 billion [^3].
