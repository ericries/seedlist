## About

The Better Money Company is building a stablecoin clearinghouse that enables customers to cheaply exchange different dollar-backed tokens [^1]. Co-founded in November 2025 by Sam Broner, a former a16z crypto investor who specialized in stablecoin investments, and Adam Zuckerman, former general counsel at Eigen Labs [^1]. The company has received commitments from issuers including Paxos, Stripe's Bridge, and MoonPay, and intends to support any token compliant with the Genius Act stablecoin legislation [^1].
