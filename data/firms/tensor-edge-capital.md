## About

Tensor Edge Capital, LLC is an investment firm founded in 2025 by Romit Shah, headquartered at 100 Federal Street, Suite 2804, Boston, Massachusetts [^1]. The firm was formerly known as Matrix Capital Partners, LLC, with the legal name change effective January 1, 2025 [^2]. The fund entity, Tensor Edge Capital Fund, LP (formerly Matrix Capital Partners Fund, LP), is managed by Matrix Capital Partners GP, LLC, based at 6 Walnut Road, Weston, Massachusetts [^3].

Tensor Edge Capital operates as a long/short equity hedge fund specializing in semiconductors and select software sectors [^1]. The firm is registered as an investment adviser with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission [^4]. The firm describes itself as employing a "concentrated, research-intensive approach to public markets, built on bottom-up conviction and a deep understanding of industry dynamics" [^1]. The firm has approximately 7 employees [^1].

**Important note:** Tensor Edge Capital is primarily a public-market fund, not a traditional venture capital firm. Its predecessor entity (Matrix Capital Partners / Matrix Capital Management) did participate in private company investments, most notably in Rivos Inc. The firm is included in this directory because of that private investment activity and because its sector expertise (semiconductors, AI) intersects with the startup ecosystem.

## Stated Thesis

Tensor Edge Capital publicly describes its focus as semiconductors and select software, emphasizing "deep knowledge, industry connectivity, and long-term perspective" as competitive advantages [^1]. The firm states it employs a "concentrated, research-intensive approach to public markets, built on bottom-up conviction and a deep understanding of industry dynamics" [^1].

Founder Romit Shah's LinkedIn profile describes his career as spanning "25 years across three distinct phases — as a Wall Street analyst, as Sector Head at Matrix Capital Management, and now as a portfolio manager" with "deep domain expertise in the semiconductor ecosystem — from core technologies to supply chains to industry leadership" [^5]. Shah states that he "leverages that experience to identify variant views and generate alpha in one of the most dynamic and consequential sectors in the market" [^5].

The firm's investment philosophy is described as "grounded in focus, discipline, and alignment with partners and portfolio companies" [^1].

## Inferred Thesis

Tensor Edge Capital's investment activity is difficult to analyze through a venture lens because the firm primarily operates in public equities. Only one private company investment has been independently verified — Rivos Inc., a semiconductor startup — which was made through the predecessor entity Matrix Capital Management.

Based on this extremely limited private investment sample (1 verified investment), no meaningful sector or stage percentages can be computed. Qualitatively, the one known private investment is consistent with the firm's stated semiconductor focus and suggests a willingness to back deep-tech hardware startups at significant scale ($250M+ round sizes).

**Notable pattern:** The connection to David Goel's Matrix Capital Management — a large technology-focused hedge fund — suggests Tensor Edge Capital may continue Matrix Capital's approach of selectively participating in private semiconductor companies while primarily operating in public markets. Shah's board seat at Rivos (representing Matrix Capital as the largest investor) indicates hands-on involvement in private investments when they occur [^6].
