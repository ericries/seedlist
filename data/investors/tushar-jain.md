## Background

Tushar Jain is the Co-Founder and Managing Partner of Multicoin Capital, a thesis-driven crypto investment firm based in Austin, Texas [^1][^2]. He leads portfolio construction, risk management, and trading operations at the firm [^1].

Before entering crypto investing, Jain co-founded ePatientFinder, a healthcare IT company that leveraged data science to identify eligible patients for clinical trials [^1][^3]. Under his leadership, ePatientFinder grew to 25 employees with over 2 million patients and hundreds of clients nationwide, raising over $11.4 million in venture capital [^3]. The company was acquired by Elligo Health Research in May 2018 [^3].

Jain holds degrees in Finance and Political Science from New York University [^1][^2].

In May 2017, Jain co-founded Multicoin Capital with Kyle Samani [^1][^2]. The firm launched its first hedge fund in October 2017 [^4]. Multicoin has since raised three venture funds: Fund I ($17M), Fund II ($100M, closed November 2020, announced May 2021), and Fund III ($430M, closed January 2022, announced July 2022) [^5][^6][^7]. Union Square Ventures is an investor in Multicoin Capital [^8].

In December 2021, Multicoin reported to investors that its first hedge fund's assets had increased by 20,287 percent since inception, driven primarily by early bets on Solana, Helium, and Arweave [^4].

## Stated Thesis

Multicoin Capital publicly describes itself as a "thesis-driven" firm that invests in "cryptoassets, tokens, and blockchain companies reshaping entire sectors of the global economy" [^1]. In a February 2026 blog post, the firm articulated its foundational belief: "Blockchains are the first-principles-correct technology to move money, coordinate and program capital formation, and power global financial markets" [^9].

As of early 2026, Multicoin organizes its investing around eight thematic areas [^9]:

1. **Fintech 4.0** — Stablecoin-based financial services with lower costs than traditional fintech
2. **DeFi Mullet** — Full-stack DeFi from customer frontends to protocol-level risk management
3. **Financial Globalization** — Tokenizing previously restricted markets for worldwide access
4. **More Efficient Borrow/Lend** — Global underwriting via direct crypto capital movement
5. **Entertainment Finance** — Reducing intermediary fees in entertainment and speculative economies
6. **Programmable Ownership** — Token-based network bootstrapping, including DePIN
7. **Credibly-Neutral Blockchains** — Neutral-by-design Layer 1 chains where competitors can coexist
8. **Cryptographic Primitives** — Enabling AI agent transactions and online identity verification

Jain co-authored Multicoin's widely cited "Proof of Physical Work" thesis in April 2022, which defined protocols that "incentivize people to do verifiable work that builds real-world infrastructure" [^10]. This thesis helped pioneer the DePIN (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure Networks) category [^10].

The firm emphasizes highly concentrated, non-consensus bets — sometimes allocating 30-50%+ of a fund into a single asset [^4]. Jain has stated: "The crypto markets are the least efficient markets I've ever seen in my life, and that means active management has an opportunity to shine" [^3].

## Inferred Thesis

Based on 35 verified investments across Multicoin's portfolio (see Portfolio table below), the following patterns emerge. Note: Multicoin has made 128+ total investments per DropsTab [^11]; this analysis covers approximately 27% of known deals, so percentages should be treated as directional rather than definitive.

**Sector Distribution (35 verified investments):**
- DeFi / Financial Infrastructure: 10 of 35 (29%) — Drift, dFlow, Kamino, Marginfi, Across Protocol, Kalshi, BetDEX, Yield.xyz, P2P.me, Mevvy
- DePIN / Physical Infrastructure: 6 of 35 (17%) — Helium, Hivemapper, Render, Geodnet, Gradient Network, Pipe Network (implied via thesis)
- Layer 1 / Blockchain Infrastructure: 6 of 35 (17%) — Solana, Aptos, Sei, NEAR Protocol, Internet Computer, Algorand
- Web3 Infrastructure / Tooling: 5 of 35 (14%) — The Graph, Livepeer, LayerZero, DoubleZero, Ceramic
- Consumer / Creator: 3 of 35 (9%) — Audius, Metaplex, FanTV
- AI / Compute: 2 of 35 (6%) — Inference, CrunchDAO
- Prediction Markets / Betting: 2 of 35 (6%) — Novig, Kalshi (also counted in DeFi)
- Other: 1 of 35 (3%) — Forward Industries (Solana treasury strategy)

**Stage Distribution (35 verified investments):**
- Seed: 12 of 35 (34%) — dFlow, Sei, Marginfi, Inference, CrunchDAO, Gradient Network, P2P.me, Mevvy, and others
- Series A: 8 of 35 (23%) — Solana Series A, Hivemapper, Jito, and others
- Series B: 4 of 35 (11%) — Drift, Novig, and others
- Series C: 2 of 35 (6%) — Kalshi, Helium (Series C)
- Token sales / strategic rounds: 9 of 35 (26%) — Metaplex, DoubleZero, Across Protocol, Geodnet, and others

Per Tracxn data, across all 111+ investments, Multicoin has made 56 investments at Seed stage with an average round size of $6.46M, and 41 investments at Series A with an average round size of $19.3M [^12].

**Geographic Focus:** Predominantly US-based projects, with increasing attention to India and Southeast Asia [^7][^9]. Team members Shayon Sengupta and Vishal Kankani focus on emerging markets, particularly India and Dubai [^7].

**Ecosystem Concentration:** Extremely heavy Solana ecosystem orientation. Multiple portfolio companies (Drift, Jito, Metaplex, Hivemapper, Marginfi, Sei) build on or are closely associated with Solana. Multicoin was a seed investor in Solana itself and has participated in three rounds of its funding [^13].

**Co-Investor Patterns:** Frequently co-invests with Framework Ventures (Jito, dFlow), Jump Crypto (Metaplex), Alameda Research (Metaplex, historically), Solana Ventures, and a16z (Helium). Union Square Ventures co-led the Helium Series C [^14].

**Notable Gaps vs. Stated Thesis:** Despite crypto-native focus, Multicoin invested in Forward Industries' $1.65B PIPE deal for a Solana treasury strategy alongside Galaxy Digital and Jump Crypto — an unusual public-market, non-crypto-native investment [^11]. The firm has acknowledged making mistakes and described crypto as "the most cyclical industry possibly in the history of capitalism" [^15].

## In Their Own Words

"The Helium vision is the most ambitious we have seen in the blockchain space since the advent of smart contracts on Ethereum. The implications of this are profound. I cannot emphasize that enough. Helium is the largest private investment we have made." — Tushar Jain, Multicoin Capital blog post announcing Helium investment, June 2019 [^14]

"I think the use cases around: (1) digital gold not controlled by a government, (2) open finance which allows anyone in the world to participate, and (3) non fungible tokens is pretty clear." — Tushar Jain, on-stage conversation with Fred Wilson at Multicoin Summit, October 2018 [^8]

"It's *crazy* to me that a middle class person in India or Brazil in 2018 can't easily buy Apple stock (or a derivative that closely tracks AAPL)." — Tushar Jain, conversation with Fred Wilson, October 2018 [^8]

"I expect the value of various crypto networks to follow a power law distribution because of the way value of a blockchain and security of said blockchain are related." — Tushar Jain, conversation with Fred Wilson, October 2018 [^8]

"Instead of democratizing access and putting market participants onto an equal playing field, what we actually have is a structure that leads to low-float, high-[fully-diluted valuation tokens] that are unfair." — Tushar Jain, debate at Solana Breakpoint conference, Singapore, September 21, 2024 [^22]

"We should give people the ability to buy tokens and have true price discovery as quickly as possible." — Tushar Jain, debate at Solana Breakpoint conference, September 21, 2024 [^22]

"I would say the market still has a bit of a hangover to work through." — Tushar Jain, fireside chat at Messari Mainnet conference, October 2, 2024, on the crypto VC market [^15]

"This is the most cyclical industry possibly in the history of capitalism." — Tushar Jain, Messari Mainnet conference, October 2, 2024 [^15]

"Trading on blockchains like Solana settles nearly instantly, eliminating the settlement delays common in traditional finance." — Tushar Jain, Multicoin Capital blog, discussing on-chain securities, 2025 [^23]
