## Background

Sumeet Singh is the Founder and CEO of LighthouseAI, a Los Angeles-based AI compliance platform for the pharmaceutical and life sciences supply chain that raised an $8M Series A led by Boxcars Ventures in April 2026 [^1]. LighthouseAI was launched on February 1, 2023 as a venture unit spun out of Pharma Solutions USA, where Singh remains Founder and Chairman [^2]. The company closed a $4.6M seed round in October 2023 led by Healthy Ventures and BluePointe Ventures, with participation from Eileses Capital, Bertelsmann Next, The Legal Tech Fund, and angel investors [^3].

Singh founded the predecessor company, Five Rivers Rx, in 2016 as a pharmaceutical state-licensing and compliance consulting firm; the business later rebranded as Pharma Solutions and grew into a leading provider of state and federal compliance services to the drug and device supply chain [^4][^5]. By the time LighthouseAI launched, the parent company was serving over 600 pharmaceutical clients, ranging from international manufacturers to family-owned pharmacies [^2]. According to Singh, LighthouseAI itself reports doubling its customer base since January 2025 to 250 customers, including 15+ pharmaceutical enterprises with over $1 billion in annual revenue [^1].

In a podcast appearance on *The Business of Pharmacy Podcast* (August 2024), Singh recounted starting his career as an intern filling out pharmacy applications by hand and working out of a bedroom in Minnesota with a mailbox before founding Five Rivers Rx; he described scaling Five Rivers Rx for five years as a services company before pivoting to AI-enabled compliance [^6]. Singh has spoken at industry conferences including PBOA, ACI, and NASCA, and has been featured in *Pharmaceutical Commerce* and *Pharmacy Times* [^4].

Singh attended UC Irvine (2008–2011) [^7].

## Stated Thesis

Singh does not publish a formal investing thesis and is currently a full-time operator. His public commentary focuses on LighthouseAI's product domain — applying AI to pharmaceutical and pharmacy compliance. On *The Business of Pharmacy Podcast* he framed the company's focus as: "AI is focused on automating and enabling the compliance journey for pharma and pharmacy" and reduced the problem to "Compliance is really only two things. It's what do I need to do? And then making sure that it gets done" [^6].
