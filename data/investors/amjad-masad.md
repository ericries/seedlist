## Background

Amjad Masad is a Jordanian-American entrepreneur and software engineer, born circa 1987-1988 in Amman, Jordan [^1]. He attended Mashrek International School and later earned a B.S. in Computer Science from Princess Sumaya University for Technology in Jordan [^1].

Masad was employee #1 at Codecademy, where he handled tech leadership, product development, and infrastructure from November 2011 to October 2013 [^2]. He then worked as a software engineer and tech lead on the JavaScript infrastructure team at Facebook from October 2013 to April 2016, where he contributed to open-source tools including the Babel compiler, Jest, and the React Native packager [^2].

In April 2016, Masad co-founded Replit with his wife Haya Odeh and his brother Faris [^3]. Replit is a browser-based coding platform designed to make software development accessible to anyone. Under his leadership, Replit has grown to over 30 million users [^1]. In March 2026, Replit raised a $400 million Series D at a $9 billion valuation, led by Georgian Partners [^4]. Forbes estimated Masad's net worth at approximately $2 billion following that round [^5].

Beyond running Replit, Masad is an active angel investor with an estimated 38-63 investments on record, primarily in AI and developer tools startups [^6][^7].

## Stated Thesis

Masad has not published a formal angel investing thesis, but his public statements reveal a strong orientation toward AI-powered tools and the democratization of software creation. His core belief, expressed repeatedly over a decade, centers on bringing "the next billion software creators online" [^2].

He has stated that AI will fundamentally reshape the economy: "AI agents are going to take off so much, and they're going to change the economy fundamentally" [^8]. On the potential for small teams powered by AI, he tweeted: "I expect $1B solo founder startups to become normal pretty soon" [^9].

Masad's investment in HoundDog.ai reflected his thesis about AI-generated code security. In a press release, he stated: "As an increasing number of companies turn to AI-generated code to accelerate development, embedding security best practices and ensuring the security of the generated code becomes essential. HoundDog.ai is leading the way in securing PII data early in the development cycle, making it an indispensable component of any AI code generation workflow. This is the reason I chose to invest in this company" [^10].

Based on his verified portfolio, Masad's investments cluster around companies building AI infrastructure, developer tools, and creative AI applications — consistent with the same thesis that drives Replit.
