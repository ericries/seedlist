## Background

Tommaso Conca (known professionally as Tom Conca) is a Partner at QuantumLight, the AI-driven venture capital firm founded by Revolut CEO Nik Storonsky [^1][^2]. Conca is based in London, United Kingdom [^3].

Conca holds a Master of Science in International Management from Universita Commerciale Luigi Bocconi (Bocconi University) in Milan [^3]. Prior to joining QuantumLight, Conca held roles at SellerX (a Berlin-based Amazon aggregator), UBS, and Rabobank [^4]. At SellerX, he was involved in the company's investment and acquisition activities related to e-commerce businesses [^5].

At QuantumLight, Conca is listed as a Partner on the firm's website and is part of the core investment team alongside CEO Ilya Kondrashov, fellow partners Dora Zikouli and Louis Fontaine, and Chief of AI Andrew Stepanov [^1]. Some sources also describe his role as Investment Principal [^3][^4].

Conca has been with QuantumLight since at least early 2024, working across the firm's portfolio of AI, fintech, SaaS, healthtech, and Web3 investments [^1][^3].

## Stated Thesis

QuantumLight publicly describes its focus as identifying "outlier growth-stage companies" using its proprietary AI model, Aleph, which tracks over 700,000 VC-backed companies across more than 10 billion data points [^1]. Conca has not made extensive public statements articulating a personal investment thesis distinct from the firm's.

In a LinkedIn post announcing the closure of QuantumLight's $250 million Fund I, Conca described the firm's hiring playbook as representing "the same approach that helped Revolut scale from a handful of people to thousands — now in a step-by-step guide for founders and talent leads" [^6]. This suggests an emphasis on operational support and systematic scaling as core to the firm's value proposition to founders.

The firm invests across AI, Web3, fintech, SaaS, and healthtech, targeting growth-stage companies that have demonstrated initial traction [^1][^7].
